NMF / ICA / Sparse Coding 線性因子化三劍客

三種「無監督因子化」演算法都解一個共同問題：X ≈ W · H。NMF 強制非負（Lee & Seung 1999, Nature）、ICA 強制獨立（Comon 1994）、Sparse Coding 強制稀疏（Olshausen & Field 1996, Nature）。它們是現代生物資訊（mutation signatures、cell-type deconvolution、fMRI 訊號分離）的隱形主力。

Three "unsupervised factorization" algorithms solve one shared problem: X ≈ W · H. NMF enforces non-negativity (Lee & Seung 1999, Nature), ICA enforces independence (Comon 1994), Sparse Coding enforces sparsity (Olshausen & Field 1996, Nature). They are the silent workhorses of modern bioinformatics — mutation signatures, cell-type deconvolution, fMRI signal separation.

核心概念

一個方程式三個約束：X ≈ W · H

共同目標：把 (m × n) 資料矩陣 X 分解為 X ≈ W · H，其中 W 是 (m × k) 的「基底/特徵」、H 是 (k × n) 的「係數/活性」，k 通常遠小於 m, n。這就是無監督學習的最古老形式之一。

① NMF (Lee & Seung 1999, Nature)：強制 W ≥ 0、H ≥ 0。為什麼有用？非負約束讓分解產生「parts-based representation」——例如人臉資料會自動分離出「眼睛、鼻子、嘴巴」這類局部組件，而非 PCA 那種「整張臉混合 ± 的 eigenfaces」。生物上特別有用，因為基因表達量、突變計數、photon count 都是天然非負的。

② ICA (Comon 1994, Bell & Sejnowski 1995)：假設源訊號 H 各 row 統計獨立、非高斯。和 PCA 只要 uncorrelated（二階統計）不同，ICA 要求更強的高階獨立性。典型應用：cocktail-party problem（在嘈雜環境分離不同說話者）、fMRI 神經訊號分離。

③ Sparse Coding (Olshausen & Field 1996, Nature)：強制 H 大部分元素為 0（L1 norm 或 L0 約束）。每個樣本只用少數 atoms。生物動機：Olshausen 原文證明對自然影像做 sparse coding 自動學出 V1 神經元的 Gabor-like receptive fields——大腦早期視覺皮層其實在做 sparse coding！

Shared goal: factor an (m × n) matrix X ≈ W · H where W is (m × k) "basis/features" and H is (k × n) "coefficients/activations", with k ≪ m, n. One of the oldest forms of unsupervised learning.

① NMF (Lee & Seung 1999, Nature): require W ≥ 0, H ≥ 0. Why useful? Non-negativity gives a "parts-based representation" — on face data NMF discovers "eyes / nose / mouth" instead of PCA's signed eigenfaces. Especially good for biology because gene-expression counts, mutation counts, and photon counts are intrinsically non-negative.

② ICA (Comon 1994, Bell & Sejnowski 1995): assume the rows of H are statistically independent and non-Gaussian. Unlike PCA which only enforces uncorrelated (2nd-order), ICA imposes higher-order independence. Classic uses: cocktail-party speaker separation, fMRI source unmixing.

③ Sparse Coding (Olshausen & Field 1996, Nature): require H to be mostly zero (L1 or L0). Each sample reconstructs from a few atoms. The biological motivation: applying sparse coding to natural images automatically yields Gabor-like receptive fields matching V1 neurons — your early visual cortex appears to be doing sparse coding!

➕

NMF：非負

W, H ≥ 0 → parts-based、可解釋。表達計數、突變、信號強度。

W, H ≥ 0 → parts-based and interpretable. For counts, mutations, intensities.

🎵

ICA：獨立

H 各源統計獨立、非高斯。雞尾酒會問題、fMRI 訊號分離。

Statistically independent, non-Gaussian sources in H. Cocktail party, fMRI.

🕳

Sparse：稀疏

H 中多數為 0。學到「字典 atoms」——影像、神經、生物 signature。

Most of H is zero. Learns a "dictionary of atoms" — images, neurons, bio-signatures.

演算法

三家演算法各自怎麼解？

① NMF 解法：

  • Multiplicative Update (Lee & Seung 2001)：W ← W ⊙ (XH⊤) ⊘ (WHH⊤)、H ← H ⊙ (W⊤X) ⊘ (W⊤WH)。元素相乘相除，自動保持非負。簡單但收斂慢。
  • ALS / HALS（Hierarchical ALS, Cichocki 2007）：交替固定 W 解 H、固定 H 解 W；HALS 每次只更新一個 column/row，收斂更快。
  • Coordinate Descent (Lin 2007)：精細控制每個元素，現代 sklearn 預設。
  • Online NMF (Mairal 2010, JMLR)：streaming 資料、mini-batch 更新——可處理百萬細胞 scRNA-seq。

② ICA 解法：

  • FastICA (Hyvärinen 1999)：fixed-point iteration on whitened data，最大化 negentropy（用 G(u) = log cosh(u) 或 exp(−u²/2) 近似）。最常用。
  • Infomax (Bell & Sejnowski 1995)：用 sigmoid + maximize mutual information。被 fMRI 領域廣泛使用。
  • JADE (Cardoso 1999)：用 4 階累積量 (cumulants) 對角化，閉式解較穩定但 scale 大時慢。

③ Sparse Coding 解法：

  • K-SVD (Aharon, Elad & Bruckstein 2006, IEEE TSP)：交替「sparse coding step (用 OMP)」與「dictionary update step (對殘差做 rank-1 SVD)」。經典。
  • Lasso / FISTA：固定 W 解 H 用 L1 regression；可加速為 ISTA → FISTA (Beck & Teboulle 2009)。
  • Online Dictionary Learning (Mairal 2009, ICML)：streaming + 線上字典更新，是 SPAMS 套件核心。

① NMF solvers:

  • Multiplicative Update (Lee & Seung 2001): W ← W ⊙ (XH⊤) ⊘ (WHH⊤); H ← H ⊙ (W⊤X) ⊘ (W⊤WH). Element-wise — automatically preserves non-negativity. Simple but slow.
  • ALS / HALS (Hierarchical ALS, Cichocki 2007): alternate fixing W to solve H, and vice versa; HALS updates one column/row per step — faster.
  • Coordinate Descent (Lin 2007): fine element-wise control; default in modern scikit-learn.
  • Online NMF (Mairal 2010, JMLR): streaming data, mini-batch updates — handles million-cell scRNA-seq.

② ICA solvers:

  • FastICA (Hyvärinen 1999): fixed-point iteration on whitened data; maximize negentropy (via G(u) = log cosh u or exp(−u²/2)). Most common.
  • Infomax (Bell & Sejnowski 1995): sigmoid + maximize mutual information. Standard in fMRI.
  • JADE (Cardoso 1999): diagonalize 4th-order cumulants; closed-form, stable but slow at scale.

③ Sparse coding solvers:

  • K-SVD (Aharon, Elad & Bruckstein 2006, IEEE TSP): alternate "sparse-coding step (via OMP)" and "dictionary update (rank-1 SVD on residual)". Classic.
  • Lasso / FISTA: fix W, solve H by L1 regression; ISTA → FISTA (Beck & Teboulle 2009) accelerates.
  • Online Dictionary Learning (Mairal 2009, ICML): streaming + online dictionary updates — core of the SPAMS toolbox.
共同陷阱：三類方法的 cost function 都是 非凸，因此局部最小值依賴初始化。實務技巧：(i) 多次 random init 取最佳；(ii) NMF 用 NNDSVD 初始化（Boutsidis 2008）；(iii) cNMF (Kotliar 2019) 對 k 個 NMF run 做 consensus clustering 求穩定 program。
Shared pitfall: all three are non-convex; local minima depend on init. Practical tips: (i) multiple random inits, pick best; (ii) NMF with NNDSVD init (Boutsidis 2008); (iii) cNMF (Kotliar 2019) consensus-clusters across runs for stable programs.

🎯 什麼時候用哪一個？

情境1:
計數型資料 + 想要可解釋的 program（scRNA-seq、mutation count、photon count）→ NMF。基因 program、突變 signature 都用它。
情境2:
混合訊號分離（fMRI、EEG、麥克風陣列）→ ICA。重點不是「重建好」而是「源能分開」。
情境3:
影像/訊號 patch + 高過完備字典 → Sparse coding / K-SVD。每個 patch 用 dictionary 中少數 atoms 表達。
情境4:
spatial transcriptomics deconvolution（每個 spot 含多種 cell type）→ NMF-based（SPOTlight、CARD）。
不適合:
資料可正可負且結構簡單（如金融 return）→ PCA 即可。三劍客的代價（非凸、調 k）不划算。
Case 1:
Count data + want interpretable programs (scRNA-seq, mutation counts, photon counts) → NMF. Used for gene programs and mutation signatures.
Case 2:
Mixed-source separation (fMRI, EEG, microphone arrays) → ICA. Goal is source separation, not reconstruction.
Case 3:
Image/signal patches + over-complete dictionary → sparse coding / K-SVD. Each patch is a sparse combination of dictionary atoms.
Case 4:
Spatial-transcriptomics deconvolution (each spot mixes cell types) → NMF-based methods (SPOTlight, CARD).
Not for:
Signed data with simple structure (financial returns) → PCA is enough. The cost of these methods (non-convex, tune k) isn't worth it.
生物應用

NMF / ICA / Sparse 在生物資訊

LIGER：跨樣本整合

Welch et al. (2019, Cell) 提出 LIGER (iNMF) — 整合多個 scRNA-seq 資料集（甚至跨 modality 如 RNA + ATAC）。學「共享」+「特定」兩組 NMF program，可比 Seurat-CCA 更穩處理 batch effect。已是 single-cell 整合的主流工具之一。

Welch et al. (2019, Cell) introduce LIGER (integrative NMF) — integrates multiple scRNA-seq datasets (even cross-modality RNA + ATAC). Learns "shared" + "specific" program factors; more robust than Seurat-CCA for some batch effects. A mainstream single-cell integration tool.

cNMF：穩定 gene program

Kotliar et al. (2019, eLife) 提出 consensus NMF — 多次 NMF run 後用 hierarchical clustering 找出最穩定的 k 個 program。解決 NMF 局部最佳解不一致問題，被廣泛用於發現 single-cell gene module。

Kotliar et al. (2019, eLife) consensus NMF — runs NMF many times and clusters factors to find the k most stable programs. Resolves NMF's local-optimum inconsistency; widely used for discovering single-cell gene modules.

Mutation signatures：癌症基因體

Alexandrov et al. (2013, Nature) 用 NMF 從 96-channel mutation context 矩陣中解出 ~30 個 mutational signature（如 SBS1 deamination、SBS4 tobacco smoking、SBS7 UV、SBS3 BRCA / HRD）。COSMIC mutational signatures 資料庫至今 100+ 條 signature 皆源於 NMF。SignatureAnalyzer (Kim 2016, Nat Genet) 用 Bayesian NMF 自動選 rank。

Alexandrov et al. (2013, Nature) used NMF on 96-channel mutation context to extract ~30 mutational signatures (SBS1 deamination, SBS4 tobacco, SBS7 UV, SBS3 BRCA/HRD). The COSMIC database (100+ signatures) is fundamentally an NMF result. SignatureAnalyzer (Kim 2016, Nat Genet) adds Bayesian NMF for auto-rank selection.

SPOTlight / CARD：空間轉錄體

10x Visium 每個 spot 包含 5–20 個細胞混合 — Elosua-Bayes et al. (2021, NAR, SPOTlight) 用 seeded NMF，每個 cell type 對應一個 program，spot 的 H 列即 cell-type 比例。CARD (Ma & Zhou 2022, Nat Biotech) 加入空間平滑先驗。

10x Visium spots mix 5–20 cells. Elosua-Bayes et al. (2021, NAR, SPOTlight) use seeded NMF — each cell type = one program; the H column gives cell-type proportions. CARD (Ma & Zhou 2022, Nat Biotech) adds a spatial smoothness prior.

ICA for fMRI

Calhoun et al. (2009, NeuroImage) 開創 group-ICA — 從 fMRI BOLD 訊號中分離出 default mode network、salience network 等獨立 brain network。是 fMRI 分析的事實標準（GIFT toolbox）。

Calhoun et al. (2009, NeuroImage) pioneered group-ICA — separates fMRI BOLD into independent brain networks (default mode, salience). De-facto standard in fMRI analysis (GIFT toolbox).

Sparse coding for V1

Olshausen & Field (1996, Nature) 原始論文發現對自然影像 patch 做 sparse coding 自動學出 oriented edge filters，與 V1 simple cell receptive field 高度相似。生物視覺系統的數學解釋。

Olshausen & Field (1996, Nature) showed that sparse coding on natural-image patches yields oriented edge filters matching V1 simple cells — a mathematical explanation for biological vision.

Sparse PCA on GWAS

Zou et al. (2006, JCGS) Sparse PCA 把 PC loading 變稀疏 — 每個 PC 只有少數 SNP 顯著。應用於 GWAS 可發現「少數 SNP 解釋的祖源結構」，比 standard PCA 更可解釋。

Zou et al. (2006, JCGS) sparse PCA — only a few SNPs load on each PC. Applied to GWAS, it discovers "ancestry components explained by a small SNP set", more interpretable than standard PCA.

Topic models 與 NMF

LDA (Blei 2003) 與 NMF 本質都是「文件 ≈ 主題 × 詞」的分解。在 single-cell 上類比為「細胞 ≈ program × 基因」。FastTopics (Carbonetto 2022) 與 hierarchical Poisson factorization 把兩者統一。

LDA (Blei 2003) and NMF both factor "document ≈ topic × word". Single-cell analog: "cell ≈ program × gene". FastTopics (Carbonetto 2022) and hierarchical Poisson factorization unify them.

深入解析

進階主題與細節

① NMF 的唯一性問題

NMF 解不唯一：任何可逆 Q 使 W' = WQ⁻¹、H' = QH 仍然滿足 X ≈ W'H'，只要 Q 保持非負約束。解：(i) 加 sparsity penalty（uniqueness 在某些條件下可保證 — Donoho & Stodden 2003）；(ii) consensus NMF 用多次 run 找穩定 program；(iii) anchor-word/separability assumption（Arora 2012）可保證可恢復。

② 選 rank k 的方法

  • Cophenetic correlation (Brunet 2004, PNAS)：對每個 k 做多次 NMF，看 consensus matrix 的階層聚類穩定性。k* 在曲線開始下降前。
  • SVD spectrum gap：看 X 的奇異值有無 gap。
  • Cross-validation：mask 部分 entry 做 reconstruction error。
  • ARD-NMF (Tan & Févotte 2013)：自動相關決定，給每個 component 一個 prior，不需要的會被剪掉。

③ Beta-divergence NMF

標準 NMF 用 Frobenius² loss（假設 Gaussian noise）。但對 count data 應該用 Poisson NLL（β = 1，KL divergence）；對 spectra 用 Itakura-Saito（β = 0）。Févotte & Idier (2011) 把這些統一為 β-divergence NMF family。Single-cell 適合 β = 1（KL），因 count noise 是 Poisson。

④ ICA 的 identifiability

ICA 假設 sources 獨立非高斯 — 若 sources 都是 Gaussian，ICA 退化為 PCA + 任意旋轉。實務上：用 kurtosis 或 negentropy 判斷 non-Gaussianity；對 sub-Gaussian (kurtosis < 3) 與 super-Gaussian (kurtosis > 3) 用不同的 G 函數。

⑤ Sparse coding 與 compressed sensing 的關係

Sparse coding 學「字典 + 稀疏係數」；compressed sensing 假設「字典已知」（如 wavelet）僅恢復稀疏係數。兩者皆基於「稀疏先驗讓 underdetermined 問題唯一可解」（Donoho 2006, Candès & Tao 2006）。

⑥ Convex NMF 與 Semi-NMF

Ding et al. (2010, IEEE TPAMI) 提出 Convex NMF — 限制 W 的 column 為原始 X column 的凸組合，即「prototypes 是真實樣本的加權平均」。Semi-NMF 放鬆 W 可正可負，僅要求 H ≥ 0。對 mixed-sign 資料適用。

⑦ 深度版本

Deep NMF / hierarchical NMF (Trigeorgis 2017)：多層 NMF 堆疊；每層學上一層 H 的 sub-program。Deep ICA：把 ICA 視為單層 nonlinear ICA；Hyvärinen & Morioka 2016 證明 nonlinear ICA 一般不可識別，但加 time / auxiliary variable 可（time-contrastive learning, permutation-contrastive learning）。

① NMF non-uniqueness

NMF is not unique: any invertible Q yields W' = WQ⁻¹, H' = QH with same product, provided non-negativity is preserved. Fixes: (i) sparsity penalty (uniqueness under conditions — Donoho & Stodden 2003); (ii) consensus NMF (cNMF) over many runs; (iii) anchor-word / separability assumption (Arora 2012).

② Choosing rank k

  • Cophenetic correlation (Brunet 2004, PNAS): multiple NMF runs per k; look at consensus-matrix clustering stability.
  • SVD spectrum gap: look for a gap in singular values.
  • Cross-validation: mask entries and measure reconstruction error.
  • ARD-NMF (Tan & Févotte 2013): automatic relevance determination; unused components shrink to zero.

③ Beta-divergence NMF

Standard NMF uses Frobenius² (Gaussian noise). Counts → Poisson NLL (β = 1, KL); audio spectra → Itakura-Saito (β = 0). Févotte & Idier (2011) unify these as β-divergence NMF. scRNA-seq prefers β = 1 (KL) since counts are Poisson.

④ ICA identifiability

ICA requires non-Gaussian sources — if all sources are Gaussian, ICA collapses to PCA + arbitrary rotation. Practical: use kurtosis / negentropy to check; sub-Gaussian (kurtosis < 3) vs super-Gaussian (kurtosis > 3) need different G functions.

⑤ Sparse coding vs compressed sensing

Sparse coding learns "dictionary + sparse coefficients". Compressed sensing assumes the dictionary is known (e.g. wavelet) and only recovers coefficients. Both rest on "sparsity makes underdetermined systems uniquely solvable" (Donoho 2006, Candès & Tao 2006).

⑥ Convex NMF and Semi-NMF

Ding et al. (2010, IEEE TPAMI) — Convex NMF constrains W columns to be convex combinations of X columns ("prototypes are weighted real samples"). Semi-NMF relaxes W to be signed (keeps H ≥ 0) — good for mixed-sign data.

⑦ Deep variants

Deep NMF / hierarchical NMF (Trigeorgis 2017): stacked NMF layers; each layer factors the previous H. Deep ICA / Nonlinear ICA (Hyvärinen & Morioka 2016): generally non-identifiable, but identifiable with time/auxiliary variables (time-contrastive learning, permutation-contrastive learning).

潛在改良應用

還能改良哪些任務？

🎯 1. 單細胞 multi-modal 整合 NMF

LIGER (iNMF) 已是雛形 — 但對 RNA + ATAC + spatial + protein 四模態 simultaneous NMF 仍少。提案：tensor-NMF（每模態一個 W^m, 共享 H）做四維 atlas。

🎯 2. Temporal NMF 用於 perturb-seq 動態

當前 cNMF 只看靜態 snapshot。提案：把 time-course Perturb-seq 用 dynamic NMF（program activation 隨時間變化）找出「擾動誘導的早晚期 program」。

🎯 3. ICA + diffusion model 的混合

diffusion 學的 latent 通常 entangled；用 ICA post-hoc disentangle latent，找到「獨立 semantic axes」。提案：iVAE 已類似，但配合 diffusion 還少有人嘗試。

🎯 4. K-SVD on scRNA-seq cell-type signatures

把 ~50k cell-type signature panel 看成 over-complete dictionary，每個 query cell 用 K-SVD-style sparse coding 找最契合 atoms。提案：新的 deconvolution / annotation tool。

🎯 5. 自監督 NMF 用 contrastive program loss

結合 SimCLR 風格的 contrastive loss 與 NMF 重建 loss — 同細胞型別 H 應接近、不同型別 H 應遠。提案：CL-NMF for stable cross-batch programs。

🎯 6. Mutation signature × clinical outcome 聯合 NMF

不只解 mutation context，還聯合 NMF on (mutation, treatment response, survival) — 找到「對某 signature 的 immunotherapy 反應特別好」這類臨床有用 program。提案：Bayesian supervised NMF。

🎯 7. NMF for chromatin signature discovery

對 ChIP-seq / CUT&Tag 數十種 histone mark 做 NMF，發現「chromatin program」（如 H3K27me3 + H3K9me3 = constitutive heterochromatin）。提案：Cross-mark NMF + 表觀遺傳調控學。

🎯 8. NMF 與 LLM 聯合 — gene program 自動命名

cNMF 解出 gene program 後人工查 GSEA。提案：LLM 自動讀 program top genes + 生物 pathway 文獻，生成 program 名稱 + 證據 — 加速大規模 atlas 註釋。

🎯 1. Single-cell multi-modal NMF

LIGER (iNMF) is a start, but RNA + ATAC + spatial + protein simultaneous NMF is rare. Proposal: tensor-NMF (one W^m per modality, shared H) for 4-modality atlas.

🎯 2. Temporal NMF for Perturb-seq dynamics

Current cNMF is static. Proposal: apply dynamic NMF (program activation varying with time) to time-course Perturb-seq to discover early vs late perturbation programs.

🎯 3. ICA + diffusion hybrid

Diffusion latents are usually entangled; ICA post-hoc disentanglement finds independent semantic axes. Proposal: iVAE is related, but ICA-on-diffusion is under-explored.

🎯 4. K-SVD on scRNA-seq cell-type signatures

Treat a ~50k cell-type signature panel as an over-complete dictionary; K-SVD-style sparse coding selects the best atoms for each query cell. Proposal: new deconvolution / annotation tool.

🎯 5. Self-supervised NMF with contrastive program loss

Combine SimCLR-style contrastive loss with NMF reconstruction — same cell-type H close, different cell-type H far. Proposal: CL-NMF for cross-batch program stability.

🎯 6. Mutation signature × clinical outcome joint NMF

Beyond decomposing mutation contexts, jointly NMF (mutation, treatment response, survival) — discovers "signature X correlates with immunotherapy response". Proposal: Bayesian supervised NMF.

🎯 7. NMF for chromatin signature discovery

NMF on dozens of ChIP-seq / CUT&Tag marks — discover "chromatin programs" (H3K27me3 + H3K9me3 = constitutive heterochromatin). Proposal: Cross-mark NMF for epigenetic regulation.

🎯 8. NMF + LLM — auto-name gene programs

After cNMF, humans manually GSEA the programs. Proposal: use an LLM to read top genes + pathway literature and auto-generate program names + supporting evidence — accelerates large atlas annotation.

程式碼

實作範例

# NMF, ICA, Sparse Coding all in sklearn
from sklearn.decomposition import NMF, FastICA, MiniBatchDictionaryLearning
import numpy as np

X = load_count_matrix()             # shape (n_samples, n_features), >= 0

# --- NMF ---
nmf = NMF(n_components=10, init='nndsvda',
          beta_loss='kullback-leibler', solver='mu',
          max_iter=500, random_state=0)
H = nmf.fit_transform(X)            # activations
W = nmf.components_                 # program × feature

Why is NMF preferred over PCA for scRNA-seq and mutation-count data?

What is the key difference between ICA and PCA?

A. ICA 用神經網路A. ICA uses neural networks
