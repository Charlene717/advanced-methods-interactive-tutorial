進階流形學習

UMAP / t-SNE 的局限與替代——Diffusion Maps、PHATE、MDE 等方法在保留全局結構與軌跡連續性上更勝一籌。

Beyond UMAP / t-SNE — Diffusion Maps, PHATE, MDE preserve global structure and trajectory continuity more faithfully.

動機

UMAP / t-SNE 的隱藏代價

t-SNE 與 UMAP 擅長分群視覺化，但它們的目標函數本質上 強調局部結構，犧牲了全局距離與軌跡連續性。Chari & Pachter (2023, PLOS Comp Biol)「The specious art of single-cell genomics」明確指出：UMAP/t-SNE 投影可能誤導生物學結論，特別是在軌跡推論與細胞型別距離比較上。

替代方案的共同目標：保留全局結構（global structure preservation） 與 連續軌跡（trajectory continuity）。

t-SNE and UMAP excel at cluster visualization, but their objectives emphasize local structure at the cost of global distance and trajectory continuity. Chari & Pachter (2023, PLOS Comp Biol) "The specious art of single-cell genomics" warns that UMAP/t-SNE projections can mislead biological conclusions, especially for trajectory inference and cell-type distance comparison.

Alternatives share the goal of preserving global structure and trajectory continuity.

主要方法

四種替代方案

Diffusion Maps

Coifman & Lafon (2006)：用「擴散過程」定義細胞間距離（隨機漫步多步後的轉移機率）。優點：在連續發育軌跡上保留全局距離；缺點：對噪音較敏感。

Coifman & Lafon (2006): define cell distance via diffusion (multi-step random walk transition probabilities). Preserves global distance on continuous trajectories; sensitive to noise.

PHATE (Moon 2019)

把 diffusion maps 升級——用 potential of heat-diffusion 變換 + MDS，同時 保留分群與軌跡，在發育資料表現特別好。

Upgrades diffusion maps with potential of heat-diffusion + MDS, preserving both clusters and trajectories — excels on developmental data.

MDE (PyMDE, 2021)

Minimum-Distortion Embedding：把降維視為通用最佳化問題，可指定「保留 k-NN」、「保留全局距離」等不同 distortion 函數。彈性極高。

Minimum-Distortion Embedding: treats dim reduction as a general optimization with customizable distortion (preserve k-NN, preserve global distances, etc.). Very flexible.

ForceAtlas2 / SPRING

用力導向圖佈局——細胞–細胞 kNN 圖直接視覺化，邊權當作彈簧。SPRING (Weinreb 2018) 在血液系統發育上廣為使用。

Force-directed layout — cell–cell kNN graph visualized directly with edges as springs. SPRING (Weinreb 2018) widely used in hematopoiesis.

演算法核心

Diffusion Maps 的數學骨幹

  1. 建立相似性矩陣：k(xi, xj) = exp(−‖xi − xj‖² / 2σ²)
  2. 歸一化為轉移矩陣：P = D⁻¹ K，其中 D 為對角度矩陣
  3. 特徵分解：P 的特徵向量 ψ1, ψ2, ... 即為新坐標；特徵值 λi 衡量重要性
  4. 嵌入：Φ(x) = (λ1ψ1(x), λ2ψ2(x), ...) — 投影到前幾維

數學魔力：嵌入空間的歐式距離 ≈ 原空間的 diffusion distance — 即「需要多少步隨機漫步才能從 i 到 j 的機率變化」。這個距離天然忽略噪音、強調連通結構。

  1. Build affinity matrix: k(xi, xj) = exp(−‖xi − xj‖² / 2σ²)
  2. Row-normalize to transition matrix: P = D⁻¹ K, with D diagonal degrees
  3. Eigendecompose: eigenvectors ψ1, ψ2, ... give new coordinates; eigenvalues λi indicate importance
  4. Embed: Φ(x) = (λ1ψ1(x), λ2ψ2(x), ...)

The math magic: Euclidean distance in embedding ≈ diffusion distance in original space — robust to noise and emphasizes connectivity.

深入解析

深入解析流形學習：數學、超參數、評估與最新方法

① Diffusion Maps 的 multi-scale 性質與時間參數 t

Diffusion map 的核心是「在資料圖上做隨機漫步」。轉移矩陣 P 的特徵值 λk 對應第 k 個 diffusion direction，diffusion distance Dt(x,y) = √Σ λk2t (φk(x)−φk(y))²。

  • t 小（如 t=1）：近距離主導——看到 local 結構（細粒度群、bursty subpopulation）。
  • t 中等（t=10–100）：看到 fate branches、developmental trajectories。
  • t 大（t→∞）：只剩平穩分布——所有點趨於相同 diffusion distance。

實務技巧：對同一資料跑多個 t 值，做 multi-resolution analysis——這正是 Palantir、PHATE 的核心想法。

② PHATE 演算法的完整步驟（Moon 2019, Nature Biotech）

PHATE 是 diffusion + information geometry 的巧妙結合，比 t-SNE/UMAP 更能保留 global trajectory。詳細步驟：

  1. kNN graph：對 PCA 後的資料建 k-nearest neighbors（adaptive bandwidth）。
  2. Transition matrix P：對 distance 用 adaptive kernel α-decay K(x,y) = exp(−(d/ε(x))α)，row-normalize 得到 P。
  3. 選最佳 t：對 Pt 的 von Neumann entropy H(t) = −Σ λkt log λkt 求一階差分，找「彎折處」——這是 noise vs signal 的最佳分隔點。
  4. Potential distance：把 Pt row 取 −log，得到 "potential of heat diffusion"——log 轉換放大遠距差異（這是 PHATE 比 diffusion map 強在保留 global 的關鍵）。
  5. MDS embedding：對 potential distance 跑 metric MDS 或 NLDR → 2D / 3D 可視化。

結果：在 hematopoiesis、reprogramming 等資料上，PHATE 同時保留 trunk 與 branches，t-SNE/UMAP 常把 branches 折斷或重疊。

③ t-SNE 數學：KL 散度、perplexity、學習率

t-SNE 最小化 KL(P‖Q)，其中 P 是高維 Gaussian 親近度、Q 是低維 t-distribution 親近度。重要細節：

  • Perplexity（5–50）：等效於「考慮多少鄰居」。小 perplexity 強調 local、大強調 global。Wattenberg 2016 在 distill.pub 強調：同一資料的 t-SNE 結果在不同 perplexity 下差異巨大，正確做法是跑多個 perplexity 比較穩定的結構。
  • 學習率：傳統預設 200，但 Belkina 2019 (Nature Comms) 證明對大資料（n > 10k）應改為 n/12——可避免 t-SNE 在大資料上「散開」。
  • Early exaggeration：前 250 iter 把 P 放大 12 倍，迫使 cluster 分離；之後關閉避免過度聚集。
  • 「Cluster sizes 不可信」：t-SNE 會把所有 cluster 拉到相似大小（t-distribution 的長尾），所以「大 cluster vs 小 cluster」不能直接從圖讀出。

④ UMAP 數學：fuzzy simplicial sets + cross-entropy

UMAP 的數學基礎是 fuzzy topology：把資料視為「fuzzy simplicial set」，high-dim 與 low-dim 的 fuzzy 集合用 cross-entropy 對齊。實務超參數：

  • n_neighbors（5–100）：類似 t-SNE perplexity。小 = local 結構，大 = global trajectory。
  • min_dist（0.0–0.99）：控制低維點之間的最小距離。min_dist=0 + 大 n_neighbors 適合 trajectory；min_dist=0.5 + 小 n_neighbors 適合 cluster 可視化。
  • UMAP vs t-SNE：UMAP 用 cross-entropy 而非 KL，所以「保留 global」較好；但 Damrich & Hamprecht (2021) 指出 UMAP 的 global 並不真正反映 high-dim 距離——只是不同的扭曲方式。

⑤ PaCMAP, TriMap：用三元組平衡 local/global

Wang 2021 系列方法核心觀念：t-SNE/UMAP 只看「正向 pair」（鄰居），不利於保留 global。改用三元組 (anchor, near, far)，最小化「anchor 與 near 距離 < anchor 與 far 距離」的 hinge loss。

  • TriMap (Amid & Warmuth 2019)：對每個點抽 10 個 far、5 個 mid、5 個 near，optimize triplet loss——比 t-SNE 在 global 保留上勝。
  • PaCMAP (Wang 2021)：進一步動態調整 mid-pair 數量隨 iter 變化，前期重 global，後期重 local。在 single-cell benchmark 上常打敗 UMAP。
  • 適用情境：當你既要看 trajectory（global）又要看 cluster（local）時，PaCMAP 是首選。

⑥ DensMAP：保留 local density 訊息

Narayan, Berger, Cho (2021, Nature Biotech) 指出：UMAP/t-SNE 把所有 cluster 拉到「均勻密度」，丟失了「哪個 cluster 比較緻密」的訊號。DensMAP 在 UMAP loss 上加 density-preservation term：每個點的 local density 估計（用 kNN 距離倒數）要在 high-dim 與 low-dim 一致。

單細胞應用：能保留「rare cell types 在 UMAP 上呈現稀疏」的特徵——對 CITE-seq、Perturb-seq 中尋找 rare subpop 特別有用。

⑦ Anchor-based 大規模降維：LandmarkMDS、Ivis

當 n > 1M 時，t-SNE/UMAP 即使 GPU 也跑不動。解法：

  • LandmarkMDS：先 random pick 或 farthest-first 選 k 個 landmark，計算 landmark 間 MDS embedding，再把其他點透過「triangulation」放入空間。
  • Ivis (Szubert 2019, Sci Reports)：用 Siamese network + triplet loss 做降維——訓練後 embedding 可泛化到新樣本（不像 t-SNE/UMAP 要重跑）。對 1M+ mass cytometry 資料是首選。
  • OpenAI/Atlas-style：用 transformer encoder 預訓練，把 embedding 直接降為 2D——可處理 100M+ data points。

⑧ 評估指標：trustworthiness、continuity、MRRE、RNX

「UMAP 比 t-SNE 好嗎？」是錯誤問題——應該量化「local 還是 global 保留得好」。指標：

  • Trustworthiness：低維鄰居中，多少在高維也是鄰居？衡量「假鄰居 false positives」。
  • Continuity：高維鄰居中，多少在低維也是鄰居？衡量「漏鄰居 false negatives」。
  • MRRE (Mean Relative Rank Error)：對每個點的 k 個鄰居計算 rank 差異——越小越好。
  • RNX curve (Lee & Verleysen 2013)：從 k=1 到 k=n 連續評估，得到一條曲線——曲線下面積 (AUCRNX) 給出綜合分數。

實務：在挑降維方法時跑這些指標而非「看圖好不好看」。

⑨ Manifold hypothesis 在 scRNA-seq 是否成立？

核心問題：「高維表現空間真的是低維流形嗎？」要回答需估計 intrinsic dimensionality (ID)：

  • Levina–Bickel (2004)：對每個點看 k 個最近鄰距離的比率，用 MLE 估 ID。
  • TwoNN (Facco 2017)：更穩健——只用第 1、第 2 近鄰的距離比 μ = r₂/r₁，分布滿足 Pareto，可直接擬合。
  • 實證：scRNA-seq 在 PBMC 約 ID = 8–12；hematopoiesis 約 ID = 15–25；reprogramming 早期甚至 > 50。所以 manifold 假設大致成立，但 ID 比預期高——這也是 t-SNE 強制 2D 必然丟訊息的根源。
  • Chari & Pachter (2023, PLOS Comp Bio)：嚴厲批評 2D 視覺化——展示同一資料用不同 perplexity/seed 得到截然不同的「故事」。建議：把 2D plot 視為「圖示」而非「分析工具」。

① Multi-scale diffusion maps and the time parameter t

Diffusion maps run a random walk on the data graph. Eigenvalues λk of the transition matrix P give the k-th diffusion direction; diffusion distance Dt(x,y) = √Σ λk2t (φk(x)−φk(y))².

  • Small t (t=1): local structure dominates — fine-grained clusters, bursty subpopulations.
  • Medium t (t=10–100): fate branches, developmental trajectories.
  • Large t (t→∞): stationary distribution — all points equidistant.

Tip: run multiple t values for multi-resolution analysis — the core idea of Palantir and PHATE.

② Full PHATE algorithm (Moon 2019, Nature Biotech)

PHATE elegantly fuses diffusion + information geometry, retaining global trajectory better than t-SNE/UMAP. Steps:

  1. kNN graph: build k-NN on PCA-reduced data with adaptive bandwidth.
  2. Transition matrix P: α-decay kernel K(x,y) = exp(−(d/ε(x))α), row-normalize.
  3. Pick optimal t: minimize first difference of von Neumann entropy H(t) = −Σ λkt log λkt — find the elbow separating noise from signal.
  4. Potential distance: take −log of Pt rows — "potential of heat diffusion" — log amplifies distant differences (the key over plain diffusion maps).
  5. MDS embedding: metric MDS or NLDR on potential distances → 2D / 3D.

Result: on hematopoiesis, reprogramming, PHATE preserves both trunk and branches, where t-SNE/UMAP fragment or overlap them.

③ t-SNE math: KL divergence, perplexity, learning rate

t-SNE minimizes KL(P‖Q), with P high-dim Gaussian affinities and Q low-dim t-distribution affinities. Key details:

  • Perplexity (5–50): effective neighborhood size. Wattenberg 2016 (distill.pub) emphasized: same data, different perplexities → wildly different layouts. Best practice: scan multiple perplexities for stable structure.
  • Learning rate: default 200 — Belkina 2019 (Nat Comm) proved that for n > 10k, use n/12 — avoids the "fragmentation" issue on large datasets.
  • Early exaggeration: first 250 iters multiply P by 12 to force cluster separation, then turn off.
  • "Cluster sizes are unreliable": t-SNE's t-distribution tails pull all clusters to similar size — never read cluster size from the plot.

④ UMAP math: fuzzy simplicial sets + cross-entropy

UMAP treats data as a fuzzy simplicial set; cross-entropy aligns high-dim and low-dim fuzzy sets. Key hyperparameters:

  • n_neighbors (5–100): like t-SNE perplexity. Small = local, large = global trajectory.
  • min_dist (0.0–0.99): minimum point spacing. min_dist=0 + large n_neighbors for trajectory; min_dist=0.5 + small n_neighbors for cluster vis.
  • UMAP vs t-SNE: cross-entropy preserves global better; but Damrich & Hamprecht (2021) showed UMAP's global doesn't truly reflect high-dim distance — just a different kind of distortion.

⑤ PaCMAP, TriMap: triplet-based local/global balance

Wang 2021 series core idea: t-SNE/UMAP only use positive pairs (neighbors), hurting global. Use triplets (anchor, near, far) with hinge loss "anchor-near < anchor-far."

  • TriMap (Amid & Warmuth 2019): per point, sample 10 far + 5 mid + 5 near, optimize triplet loss — beats t-SNE on global preservation.
  • PaCMAP (Wang 2021): dynamically anneals mid-pair count across iters — global early, local late. Often beats UMAP on single-cell benchmarks.
  • Use case: when you need both trajectory and cluster — PaCMAP is the go-to.

⑥ DensMAP: preserving local density

Narayan, Berger, Cho (2021, Nat Biotech) showed UMAP/t-SNE flatten cluster densities — losing "which cluster is denser." DensMAP adds a density-preservation term: per-point local density (1/kNN distance) must match between high and low dim.

Single-cell use: preserves "rare cell types appear sparse on UMAP" — useful for CITE-seq and Perturb-seq rare subpopulation hunting.

⑦ Anchor-based large-scale reduction: LandmarkMDS, Ivis

For n > 1M, even GPU t-SNE/UMAP struggles. Solutions:

  • LandmarkMDS: random or farthest-first pick of k landmarks, MDS on landmarks, triangulate remaining points.
  • Ivis (Szubert 2019, Sci Rep): Siamese network + triplet loss — generalizes to new samples (unlike t-SNE/UMAP, no need to refit). First choice for 1M+ mass cytometry.
  • OpenAI/Atlas-style: pretrained transformer encoders that directly output 2D — scales to 100M+ points.

⑧ Evaluation: trustworthiness, continuity, MRRE, RNX

"Is UMAP better than t-SNE?" is the wrong question. Quantify local vs. global preservation:

  • Trustworthiness: fraction of low-dim neighbors that are also high-dim neighbors (false positives).
  • Continuity: fraction of high-dim neighbors retained in low dim (false negatives).
  • MRRE: mean relative rank error over k-NN — smaller is better.
  • RNX curve (Lee & Verleysen 2013): evaluate across all k; AUCRNX gives a single score.

Practice: run these metrics rather than judging by visual appearance.

⑨ Does the manifold hypothesis hold for scRNA-seq?

Key question: is the high-dim expression space really a low-dim manifold? Requires intrinsic dimensionality (ID) estimation:

  • Levina–Bickel (2004): MLE on kNN distance ratios.
  • TwoNN (Facco 2017): more robust — use only the ratio μ = r₂/r₁ of first two NN distances; follows a Pareto and fits in closed form.
  • Empirical: PBMC scRNA-seq has ID ≈ 8–12; hematopoiesis ID ≈ 15–25; early reprogramming > 50. The manifold hypothesis roughly holds, but ID is higher than expected — explaining why forcing 2D loses information.
  • Chari & Pachter (2023, PLOS Comp Bio): harsh critique of 2D visualization — same data with different perplexity/seed gives different "stories." Recommendation: treat 2D plots as illustrations, not analysis tools.
詳細已知應用

流形學習在生物資訊的具體案例

🧬 案例 1：PHATE on Hematopoiesis (Moon et al. 2019, Nature Biotechnology)

PHATE 的旗艦應用。對人類 bone marrow CD34+ scRNA-seq 跑 PHATE，同時呈現「主幹（multipotent progenitor）→ 分支（myeloid vs lymphoid vs erythroid）」結構。比較對照組：t-SNE 把分支折斷成獨立 islands；UMAP 把 trunk 拉成不自然的弧形。PHATE 也用於 embryoid body 分化、iPSC reprogramming，皆顯示其 trajectory-preserving 優勢。

🧬 案例 2：Palantir (Setty et al. 2019, Nature Biotechnology)

建立在 diffusion maps 上推 fate probability。對 CD34+ HSPC 資料：(1) 跑 diffusion maps 找 multi-scale fates；(2) 用 random walk 在 diffusion graph 上計算「給定 cell，到達各 terminal state 的機率」；(3) 對每個 fate 量「entropy」識別決定點。發現 GATA1 早期上升是 erythroid commitment 的關鍵。已成為 fate analysis 標準工具之一。

🧬 案例 3：Diffusion Pseudotime (Haghverdi et al. 2016, Nature Methods)

DPT 是 pseudotime 領域的奠基論文之一。核心：對 cell-cell similarity 跑 diffusion map，取 root cell 與其他 cell 的 diffusion distance 為 pseudotime。對 monocle1 (ICA-based)、Wanderlust (kNN graph) 等前期方法的進步：對 branching 結構穩健。已整合進 Scanpy 的 sc.tl.dpt。

🧬 案例 4：ForceAtlas2 + SPRING (Weinreb et al. 2018, PNAS)

SPRING 用 force-directed layout 在 kNN graph 上做降維——physical simulation 把連通分量拉開、cluster 內部聚緊。在小鼠 hematopoiesis 與 Drosophila gastrulation 上顯示比 t-SNE 更能保留「lineage continuity」。其輕量級 web tool 是早期 single-cell 教學的標配。

🧬 案例 5：TriMap 在大規模 single-cell viral discovery

2023 年數個論文用 TriMap 對 SARS-CoV-2、Influenza 的 RNA-seq 序列空間做降維，找到傳統 phylogenetic tree 漏掉的「跨亞型重組事件」——TriMap 的 global preservation 保留遠緣樣本間的真實 distance，比 UMAP 更不易把不同 clade 的樣本混在一起。

🧬 案例 6：PaCMAP 在 cell atlas 規模

HCA (Human Cell Atlas) 1.5M cells 資料上，PaCMAP 比 UMAP 更能同時保留 organ-level structure 與 cell-type-level resolution。Tabula Sapiens 後續分析（2023）顯示 PaCMAP 上能直接讀出「同一細胞類型跨組織的相似度」，UMAP 上則被 batch effect 強制分離。

🧬 案例 7：Chari & Pachter (2023, PLOS Comp Bio) — 重要綜述

「The specious art of single-cell genomics」直接挑戰 UMAP/t-SNE 在 single-cell 的地位。展示：同樣的 PBMC 資料用 11 個不同 random seed 的 UMAP 給出 11 個「視覺上明顯不同」的圖。建議：把 2D plot 當作 sanity check，定量分析還是要在 high-dim 空間做。對整個 single-cell community 引發「降維方法選擇要更謹慎」的反省。

🧬 案例 8：BBKNN + UMAP for batch correction (Polański et al. 2019)

BBKNN（batch-balanced kNN）在建 kNN graph 時強制每個 batch 都有等量鄰居——把 batch effect 在 kNN level 消除，再跑 UMAP 得到 batch-mixed embedding。在 Tabula Muris、HLCA 等 cross-batch atlas 上是 baseline 方法之一。

🧬 案例 9：Ivis 在 mass cytometry

CyTOF 資料常 1–10M cells × 40+ markers。Ivis 用 Siamese network 訓練 embedding，新樣本可直接 inference（不必重訓）——這對 clinical pipeline 至關重要（每個病人來都不能重跑全部資料）。Szubert 2019 在 Sci Reports 比較 ivis vs UMAP vs t-SNE，在 generalization 與 speed 上顯著勝出。

🧬 Case 1: PHATE on Hematopoiesis (Moon et al. 2019, Nature Biotechnology)

PHATE's flagship application. On human bone-marrow CD34+ scRNA-seq, PHATE simultaneously shows the trunk (MPP) and the branches (myeloid, lymphoid, erythroid). Comparison: t-SNE fragments branches into islands; UMAP bends the trunk into an unnatural arc. PHATE also handles embryoid-body differentiation and iPSC reprogramming, demonstrating trajectory preservation.

🧬 Case 2: Palantir (Setty et al. 2019, Nature Biotechnology)

Diffusion-map based fate probability inference. On CD34+ HSPCs: (1) diffusion maps for multi-scale fates; (2) random walks on the diffusion graph compute "probability a cell reaches each terminal state"; (3) per-fate entropy locates decision points. Identified early GATA1 rise as the erythroid commitment driver. Now a standard fate analysis tool.

🧬 Case 3: Diffusion Pseudotime (Haghverdi et al. 2016, Nature Methods)

DPT is a foundational pseudotime paper. Core: diffusion map on cell-cell similarity, pseudotime = diffusion distance from root cell. Advance over Monocle1 (ICA) and Wanderlust (kNN): robust to branching. Integrated into Scanpy as sc.tl.dpt.

🧬 Case 4: ForceAtlas2 + SPRING (Weinreb et al. 2018, PNAS)

SPRING runs force-directed layout on the kNN graph — physical simulation separates components and tightens clusters. On mouse hematopoiesis and Drosophila gastrulation, beats t-SNE at preserving lineage continuity. The lightweight web tool became standard in single-cell tutorials.

🧬 Case 5: TriMap for large-scale viral discovery

Several 2023 papers used TriMap on SARS-CoV-2 and influenza RNA-seq sequence space, finding cross-subtype recombination events missed by phylogenetic trees. TriMap's global preservation keeps distal samples truly distal, avoiding UMAP's tendency to mix distant clades.

🧬 Case 6: PaCMAP at cell atlas scale

On HCA's 1.5M cells, PaCMAP preserves both organ-level structure and cell-type-level resolution better than UMAP. Tabula Sapiens follow-up (2023) showed PaCMAP reveals cross-tissue similarity of the same cell type, while UMAP forces batch-driven separation.

🧬 Case 7: Chari & Pachter (2023, PLOS Comp Bio) — Pivotal Critique

"The specious art of single-cell genomics" challenged UMAP/t-SNE's dominance. Showed the same PBMC data under 11 different random seeds gives 11 visually different UMAPs. Recommendation: treat 2D plots as sanity checks; do quantitative analysis in high dim. Sparked community-wide reflection on dimensionality reduction choices.

🧬 Case 8: BBKNN + UMAP for batch correction (Polański et al. 2019)

BBKNN (batch-balanced kNN) forces each batch to contribute equally to a cell's neighborhood — eliminating batch effect at the kNN level before UMAP. A baseline method on Tabula Muris, HLCA, and other cross-batch atlases.

🧬 Case 9: Ivis on mass cytometry

CyTOF data: 1–10M cells × 40+ markers. Ivis uses a Siamese network to learn an embedding that generalizes to new samples without refitting — crucial for clinical pipelines. Szubert 2019 (Sci Rep) showed Ivis beats UMAP/t-SNE on generalization and speed.

潛在改良應用

流形學習還能改良 / 新解決哪些任務？

🎯 1. Multi-scale diffusion 做 multi-resolution clustering

當前 Leiden 在單一 resolution 下分群——對「巢狀結構」（如 T cell 大群內含多個亞群）難以同時呈現。提案：對 diffusion map 跑多個 t 值（t=1, 5, 20, 100），對每個 t 做 spectral clustering，建立 hierarchical 結構。比現有 hierarchical clustering 強：t 參數有明確物理意義（時間尺度），且 multi-scale 結構是 data-driven 而非超參數選的。應用：腫瘤異質性、免疫 repertoire 階層分析。

🎯 2. PHATE + RNA velocity 整合可視化

scVelo 在 UMAP 上畫向量箭頭，但 UMAP 不保留 trajectory，velocity 方向常與真實 fate 不符。提案：把 RNA velocity 視為「修正 transition matrix」的訊號——P̃(i→j) = P(i→j) · exp(α · cos(velocityi, positionj−positioni))，再跑 PHATE。預期得到「velocity-aware PHATE embedding」，箭頭與軌跡形狀一致。對 hematopoiesis、neurogenesis 等系統可大幅改善 fate prediction 解讀性。

🎯 3. 跨切片 spatial PHATE

連續切片的 spatial transcriptomics（Visium、Stereo-seq）目前對每片獨立分析；跨切片的「3D 流形」建構困難。提案：把所有切片的 spots 視為點雲，距離 = α · spatial_distance（含切片 z 軸）+ (1−α) · expression_distance。跑 PHATE 得到 3D embedding，可同時呈現組織形態學與表現分群。應用：肝臟 zonation 3D 重建、發育胚胎 organogenesis 連續切片整合。

🎯 4. 多模態 PHATE — RNA + ATAC 共同 manifold

多模態整合（MOFA、totalVI）給出 joint latent，但通常用 linear factorization 找。提案：對 RNA kNN graph 與 ATAC kNN graph 做 weighted graph fusion（如 SNF, Wang 2014），再跑 diffusion + PHATE。優勢：(a) 非線性整合；(b) 對缺失模態 robust（partial modality 也可加入）；(c) 同時保留兩模態的 trajectory 訊號。

🎯 5. 跨物種 manifold 對應

把人類 hematopoiesis manifold 與小鼠 hematopoiesis manifold 對齊——目前用 SCENIC 找 conserved TF，但 manifold-level 比較未充分發展。提案：對兩物種分別跑 PHATE，得到 2D coordinates；用 Gromov–Wasserstein (見第 4 章 OT) 對齊兩個 manifold——找「結構對應」而非「點對點對應」。能識別人類特有 lineage branches、跨物種保守的 fate decision points。

🎯 6. scLLM embedding 後的「真實 manifold」評估

scGPT、Geneformer 的 latent space 號稱「保留生物結構」，但驗證方式多停留在 cell-type ARI。提案：對 ground-truth 資料（如有 lineage tracing 的 zebrafish embryo）的「原始表現空間」與「scLLM latent」分別跑 PHATE，用 trustworthiness/continuity 量化 latent 是否真的保留了 manifold 結構。可作為 scLLM 的新 benchmark，補充 cell-type 分類 accuracy。

🎯 7. Disease vs healthy 的 manifold geodesic

單細胞病理學常比較 disease vs healthy 的「平均表現」——但兩者在 manifold 上的「結構性差異」常被忽略。提案：對 healthy 與 disease 樣本合併跑 manifold 學習，計算每個 disease cell 在 manifold 上到「最近 healthy cell」的 geodesic distance（沿 manifold 而非歐式距離）。可量化「這個細胞偏離正常多遠」、識別「病理特有 fate branches」。應用：cancer progression、autoimmune disease cell states。

🎯 8. Perturbation 在 manifold 上的軌跡

Perturb-seq 量「擾動前後表現變化」，但傳統 DE 看不到「擾動把細胞推向哪個 fate」。提案：對 control 細胞建立 manifold，把 perturbed 細胞「投影」到 manifold 上，計算 (a) projection 偏移向量；(b) 沿 manifold 移動的 geodesic 路徑。比 differential expression 提供更多「動力學」訊息——能識別「哪個 perturbation 把細胞推向 erythroid，哪個推向 myeloid」。

🎯 9. 用 manifold curvature 找 transition states

Waddington landscape 上的「saddle point」對應 fate decision——這在 manifold 上對應 high curvature 區域。提案：對 diffusion-map 嵌入估計局部 Ricci curvature（Ollivier-Ricci on graph）。高 curvature 區域 = potential transition states。比現有的「pseudotime entropy peak」更穩健，且有明確幾何意義。應用：找 EMT transition states、HSC commitment points、neuronal fate decisions。

🎯 10. 主題模型 (topic models) + manifold 整合

scHPF、LDA 等主題模型給「軟分群」（每個細胞是 topics 的混合），但缺乏 trajectory 概念。提案：把 topic proportions 視為 simplex 上的點（K-1 維），跑 simplex-aware manifold learning（如 Aitchison geometry-based diffusion）。能同時保留「軟分群」與「軌跡」訊息——特別適合 reprogramming 等 intermediate state 多的系統。

🎯 1. Multi-scale diffusion for multi-resolution clustering

Leiden runs at one resolution — nested structures (e.g. a T-cell macro-cluster with subclusters) are hard to show together. Proposal: diffusion map at multiple t (1, 5, 20, 100), spectral cluster each, build a hierarchy. Better than standard hierarchical clustering: t has clear physical meaning (time scale), and the hierarchy is data-driven, not hyperparameter-chosen. Applications: tumor heterogeneity, immune repertoire hierarchy.

🎯 2. PHATE + RNA velocity integrated visualization

scVelo arrows are drawn on UMAP — but UMAP distorts trajectory, so arrows often mismatch real fate. Proposal: use RNA velocity to correct the transition matrix — P̃(i→j) = P(i→j) · exp(α · cos(velocityi, posj−posi)) — then run PHATE. Get a "velocity-aware PHATE" where arrows align with trajectory shape. Big win for hematopoiesis, neurogenesis fate interpretation.

🎯 3. Cross-section spatial PHATE

Serial-section spatial transcriptomics is currently analyzed per slide — 3D manifold reconstruction is hard. Proposal: all spots as point cloud with distance = α · spatial_distance (including z) + (1−α) · expression_distance. PHATE gives a 3D embedding co-displaying tissue morphology and expression. Applications: 3D liver zonation, embryonic organogenesis from serial sections.

🎯 4. Multi-modal PHATE — joint RNA + ATAC manifold

MOFA, totalVI give joint latents via linear factorization. Proposal: fuse RNA kNN and ATAC kNN graphs (SNF, Wang 2014) and run diffusion + PHATE. Advantages: nonlinear integration; robust to missing modalities; preserves trajectory in both.

🎯 5. Cross-species manifold correspondence

Aligning human and mouse hematopoiesis manifolds is currently done via conserved TFs (SCENIC) — manifold-level comparison is undeveloped. Proposal: run PHATE per species for 2D coords; align by Gromov–Wasserstein (Ch 4 OT) — structural matching, not point-to-point. Identifies human-specific branches and conserved fate decisions.

🎯 6. Evaluating the "true manifold" in scLLM embeddings

scGPT, Geneformer claim to preserve structure — usually only validated via cell-type ARI. Proposal: on a ground-truth dataset (e.g. zebrafish with lineage tracing), run PHATE on raw vs. scLLM latent; trustworthiness/continuity quantify true manifold preservation. A new scLLM benchmark.

🎯 7. Disease-vs-healthy manifold geodesic

Pathology usually compares mean expression — ignoring manifold-level structural difference. Proposal: co-embed healthy + disease, compute per disease cell the geodesic distance (along manifold, not Euclidean) to the nearest healthy cell. Quantifies "how far this cell deviates" and reveals disease-specific fate branches. Applications: cancer progression, autoimmune cell states.

🎯 8. Perturbation trajectories on the manifold

Perturb-seq measures pre/post expression but doesn't show which fate the perturbation pushes cells toward. Proposal: build a manifold on control cells; project perturbed cells; compute (a) projection offset vector and (b) geodesic path. Provides kinetic info beyond DE — distinguishes "this perturbation pushes erythroid, that one pushes myeloid."

🎯 9. Manifold curvature for transition states

Waddington saddle points = fate decisions = high curvature regions. Proposal: estimate Ollivier-Ricci curvature on the diffusion graph. High curvature = transition state candidates. More robust than "pseudotime entropy peak" with clear geometric meaning. Applications: EMT, HSC commitment, neuronal fate decisions.

🎯 10. Topic models + manifold integration

scHPF, LDA topic models give soft cluster assignments but no trajectory. Proposal: topic proportions live on a simplex (K-1 dim); run simplex-aware manifold learning (Aitchison-geometry diffusion). Co-preserves soft clusters and trajectory — ideal for reprogramming and other intermediate-state-rich systems.

程式碼

實作範例

import scanpy as sc
sc.pp.neighbors(adata, n_neighbors=30)
sc.tl.diffmap(adata, n_comps=15)
sc.pl.diffmap(adata, color="celltype")
import phate
phate_op = phate.PHATE(knn=30, t="auto", decay=40)
Y = phate_op.fit_transform(adata.X)
adata.obsm["X_phate"] = Y
sc.pl.embedding(adata, "phate", color="celltype")
import pymde
emb = pymde.preserve_neighbors(
    adata.obsm["X_pca"],
    constraint=pymde.Standardized(),
    embedding_dim=2
).embed()
