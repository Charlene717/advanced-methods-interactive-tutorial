擴散模型 (Diffusion Models)

從生成圖像走向生成細胞與序列——DDPM、score-based、scDiffusion、EvoDiff、Chroma。最強生成模型如何進入生物學？

From image generation to cell and sequence generation — DDPM, score-based, scDiffusion, EvoDiff, Chroma. How the strongest generative models entered biology.

核心概念

加噪 → 去噪

前向過程：把資料 x₀ 逐步加入 Gaussian 噪音，T 步後 xT ≈ N(0, I)。每一步：xt = √(αt) · xt−1 + √(1 − αt) · ε。

反向過程（學習）：訓練神經網路 εθ(xt, t) 預測「該步加入的噪音是什麼」。生成時從 xT ~ N(0, I) 開始，反覆 xt−1 = (xt − √(1−α) · εθ) / √α + 噪音，直到 x₀。

數學等價：DDPM (Ho 2020) ≡ score-based SDE (Song & Ermon 2021)，本質都是學 ∇x log p(x) — score function。

Forward process: Gradually corrupt data x₀ with Gaussian noise over T steps until xT ≈ N(0, I). Per step: xt = √(αt) · xt−1 + √(1 − αt) · ε.

Reverse process (learned): Train εθ(xt, t) to predict the noise added at each step. Sample by starting from xT ~ N(0, I) and iteratively denoising back to x₀.

Mathematical equivalence: DDPM (Ho 2020) ≡ score-based SDE (Song & Ermon 2021) — both learn ∇x log p(x), the score function.

生物應用

生物擴散模型大家族

scDiffusion (Luo 2024)

第一個 scRNA-seq 擴散模型，能生成「指定條件」的合成細胞——可用於資料增強、平衡稀有細胞型別、in-silico 擾動。

First scRNA-seq diffusion model; generates synthetic cells under specified conditions — useful for augmentation, balancing rare types, in-silico perturbation.

scDiff / CFGen (2024)

後續工作：用 classifier-free guidance 控制細胞型別、組織、batch，並改良 score function 適應 NB counts。

Follow-ups: classifier-free guidance over cell type, tissue, batch; tailored score functions for NB counts.

EvoDiff (Alamdari 2023)

蛋白質序列擴散模型，直接在離散胺基酸空間取樣，能生成新蛋白並做 inpainting（補序列空缺）。

Protein sequence diffusion — directly samples in discrete amino-acid space; generates novel proteins and inpaints sequence gaps.

Chroma (Ingraham 2023, Nature)

蛋白質結構生成，可條件化於 SS、symmetry、function——一步躍進蛋白質設計。

Protein structure generation, conditioned on secondary structure, symmetry, function — a leap for protein design.

RFdiffusion (Watson 2023)

基於 RoseTTAFold 的擴散模型，de novo 設計蛋白質骨架，已有實驗驗證的 binder 設計。

RoseTTAFold-based diffusion for de novo protein backbone design; experimentally validated binder designs exist.

Sequence-design diffusion (Gosai 2024)

用擴散設計 cis-regulatory elements——「想要這個細胞型別有這種表現量」反推啟動子序列。

Diffusion-designed cis-regulatory elements — given a desired cell-type-specific expression, generate promoter sequences.

深入解析

深入解析擴散模型：SDE、guidance、latent diffusion

① 前向過程 q(xt | xt−1) 詳細數學與 noise schedule

DDPM (Ho et al. 2020) 定義 Markov chain：q(xt | xt−1) = N(xt; √(1−βt) xt−1, βt I)。βt 為 noise variance schedule。由「reparameterization closed-form」可導出直接從 x0 跳到 xt：

q(xt | x0) = N(xt; √α̅t x0, (1−α̅t) I)，其中 αt = 1−βt，α̅t = Πs=1..t αs

意即 xt = √α̅t x0 + √(1−α̅t) ε，ε ~ N(0, I)——一步取樣即可，不需要走完整條鏈。Noise schedule：

  • Linear schedule：β1 = 1e−4 → βT = 0.02 等差，T=1000。簡單但前段加噪太慢（早期 step 的 SNR 變化不大，浪費 capacity）。
  • Cosine schedule (Nichol & Dhariwal 2021)：α̅t = cos²((t/T + s)/(1+s) · π/2)。讓噪音添加更平均地分散在所有 step，improved-DDPM 用此可顯著提升 FID。
  • Sigmoid / EDM schedule (Karras 2022)：把 log-SNR 視為核心參數，給出 continuous-time 形式，是目前 SOTA 圖像 diffusion 預設。

② Score function ∇x log p(x) 與 noise prediction 的等價性

DDPM 把 reverse 過程 pθ(xt−1 | xt) 參數化為 N(μθ(xt, t), Σθ)。經代數整理（Ho 2020 Section 3.2），訓練目標等價於「預測加在 x0 上的噪音」：Lsimple = Et, x0, ε[‖ε − εθ(√α̅t x0 + √(1−α̅t) ε, t)‖²]。

等價性：Vincent (2011) 證明 denoising 與 score matching 等價——對加噪資料 xt，預測噪音 ε 等價於估計 ∇xt log q(xt)：
∇xt log q(xt | x0) = − (xt − √α̅t x0) / (1−α̅t) = − ε / √(1−α̅t)

所以 score sθ(x, t) 與 noise predictor εθ(x, t) 只差一個 scaling factor。Song 等 score-based 觀點與 Ho 等 DDPM 觀點是「同一個物件的兩種寫法」。

③ DDPM vs DDIM：deterministic vs stochastic sampling

DDPM 取樣：xt−1 = μθ(xt, t) + σt z, z~N(0,I)——每步隨機，需 1000 step。

DDIM (Song et al. 2021, ICLR)：用 non-Markovian 推論族，加入 η 參數控制隨機度。η=0 完全 deterministic（同一 xT 永遠生成同一 x0），可跳 step 從 1000 → 50 而品質幾乎不降。η=1 退化為 DDPM。實務影響：幾乎所有現代擴散模型（Stable Diffusion 預設）都用 DDIM 或其變體取樣。η=0 還能做「latent inversion」：對真實圖像找對應的 xT，用於 editing。

④ Score-based SDE 統一觀點 (Song et al. 2021, ICLR)

把離散 step 連續化：

  • VE-SDE (Variance Exploding)：dx = √(d[σ²(t)]/dt) dw——σ(t) 從小到很大，x 變異發散。Score-based generative model NCSN/NCSN++ 用此。
  • VP-SDE (Variance Preserving)：dx = −½ β(t) x dt + √β(t) dw——保持有限變異，相當於 DDPM 的連續極限。
  • sub-VP-SDE：進一步壓 variance bound，得到更好的 likelihood。

反向 SDE：dx = [f(x,t) − g(t)² ∇x log pt(x)] dt + g(t) dw̃。用 Euler-Maruyama 或更高階 Probability Flow ODE 解。關鍵洞見：所有 diffusion 都是 SDE 的離散化；DDPM、NCSN、EDM 只是不同 SDE 與 schedule 的選擇。

⑤ Classifier-free guidance (Ho & Salimans 2022)

條件生成的早期方法（classifier guidance, Dhariwal 2021）需要另訓 noisy classifier。Ho 2022 提出 不用 classifier 的方法：訓練同一個網路同時學 unconditional εθ(x, t, ∅) 與 conditional εθ(x, t, c)（10–20% 機率隨機 drop condition）。取樣時：

ε̃θ(x, t, c) = εθ(x, t, ∅) + w · (εθ(x, t, c) − εθ(x, t, ∅))

w > 1（典型 7.5）放大「條件方向」，得到 sharper、更貼合條件的樣本，代價是 mode coverage 略降。Stable Diffusion、DALL-E 2 都用 CFG。在生物應用：對「給定 cell type 生成 expression profile」、「給定蛋白功能生成序列」皆可用 CFG 加強條件控制。

⑥ Latent diffusion (Rombach et al. 2022, CVPR) — Stable Diffusion 的核心

原 pixel-space diffusion 在高解析圖像極貴（U-Net 處理 512×512×3 → 786K dimensions）。Rombach 等先用 VAE 把圖像壓到 64×64×4 latent，再在 latent 上做 diffusion，最後 VAE decode 回 pixel。優點：

  • 計算量降 ~50 倍，可在 24 GB GPU 訓練。
  • VAE encoder 自動去除像素級高頻雜訊，diffusion 專注語義內容。
  • cross-attention 條件機制可整合文字 / 標籤 / 影像。

對生物應用啟發：對 scRNA-seq 用 scVI VAE 先壓到 10 維 latent，再在 latent 做 diffusion——scDiffusion (Luo 2024) 正是此思想。對蛋白結構，先用 ESM-2 encode 再 diffuse 也是同樣策略。

⑦ Consistency model (Song et al. 2023, ICML)

One-step generation：訓練 fθ(xt, t) 滿足 f(xt, t) ≈ f(xt', t')（PF-ODE 軌跡上同一條線上的點映射到同一 x0）。訓練後可一步 (or 2-4 step) 從 xT 直接生成 x0，加速 50–1000 倍。可從現有 pretrained diffusion distill 而來。對生物：對 in-silico screen 大規模生成（例如 100 萬個候選分子）極有用——傳統 diffusion 太慢。

⑧ Flow matching / continuous-time normalizing flow (Lipman et al. 2022)

把 diffusion 推廣到「任意 conditional probability path」：直接學 velocity field vθ(x, t) 使 ODE dx/dt = vθ(x, t) 把 prior（高斯）流到資料分布。比 diffusion 訓練更穩定、取樣更快。Optimal transport coupling：選 OT-optimal path 可給最直線軌跡，採樣更高效。Meta 2024 推出的 Movie Gen 即用 flow matching。生物界 RFdiffusion 後續、protein flow matching 工作（如 FrameFlow）皆採用。

⑨ Conditional diffusion in biology — control by cell type, tissue, batch

scDiffusion 在 latent space 上條件於 cell type 與 batch；對「給定 type X 生成樣本」極自然。架構：U-Net 在 latent 上 + cross-attention 對 (cell type, batch, drug) embedding。實踐 trick：(a) 用 CFG w = 3–10 加強條件控制；(b) 對連續條件（如劑量）用 sinusoidal embedding；(c) classifier-free training 中 condition dropout 15%。

⑩ Preconditioning 與 EDM (Karras et al. 2022, NeurIPS)

Karras 等系統化整理 diffusion design space：把 noise level σ 直接作為輸入，網路改寫成 cskip(σ) · x + cout(σ) · Fθ(cin(σ) · x, cnoise(σ))，讓網路在所有 σ 下的有效訊號量都一致。再加 stochastic sampler with churn 參數。在 CIFAR-10 / ImageNet 取得當時 SOTA FID，已成新 baseline 設計。

⑪ Discrete diffusion: EvoDiff 與 sequence diffusion

對離散資料（DNA/RNA/protein sequence）不能直接用高斯噪音。D3PM (Austin 2021) 與 masked diffusion (LLaDA, Shi 2024) 提出在離散空間的 corruption process：每 step 機率 mask token 或轉成 [MASK]/隨機 token。reverse 學「token denoising」。EvoDiff (Alamdari 2024, Nat Commun) 用 OADM (Order-Agnostic Diffusion Model) 在 protein sequence 上訓練，生成新蛋白序列，可條件於 structure motif。

⑫ Equivariant diffusion for 3D structure

蛋白結構由 atom 坐標構成，旋轉/平移整體 → 同一蛋白。模型必須 SE(3)-equivariant：對輸入做剛體變換，輸出 score 也應做對應變換。RFdiffusion (Watson 2023, Nature) 把 RoseTTAFold 結構預測網路改造為 SE(3)-equivariant denoiser。Chroma (Ingraham 2023, Nature) 用 SE(3) graph neural network。FrameDiff (Yim 2023) 在 SE(3)N manifold 上直接 diffuse rigid body frames。equivariance 確保模型 generalize 比 data augmentation 更穩健。

① Forward process math and noise schedules

DDPM (Ho 2020): q(xt | xt−1) = N(xt; √(1−βt) xt−1, βt I). Closed-form jump:

q(xt | x0) = N(xt; √α̅t x0, (1−α̅t) I); αt=1−βt; α̅t=∏αs

So xt = √α̅t x0 + √(1−α̅t) ε. Schedules:

  • Linear: β=1e−4→0.02 over T=1000. Simple but wastes capacity at early steps.
  • Cosine (Nichol & Dhariwal 2021): α̅t=cos²(...). Spreads noise injection more evenly; improved-DDPM uses this for better FID.
  • Sigmoid / EDM (Karras 2022): parameterize log-SNR; continuous time; SOTA default.

② Equivalence of score and noise prediction

DDPM's Lsimple = E[‖ε − εθ(√α̅t x0+√(1−α̅t)ε, t)‖²] is equivalent (Vincent 2011) to denoising score matching: ∇xt log q(xt|x0) = −ε/√(1−α̅t). Hence score sθ(x,t) and noise predictor εθ(x,t) differ only by a scaling factor — Song's score view and Ho's DDPM view are two writeups of the same object.

③ DDPM vs DDIM: stochastic vs deterministic sampling

DDPM: xt−1 = μθ + σ z (stochastic, 1000 steps). DDIM (Song 2021, ICLR) introduces η; η=0 is fully deterministic (same xT → same x0), can skip 1000 → 50 with negligible quality loss; η=1 reduces to DDPM. Nearly all modern diffusion models (Stable Diffusion default) sample with DDIM. η=0 also enables "latent inversion" for real-image editing.

④ Score-based SDE unified view (Song et al. 2021)

  • VE-SDE: dx = √(d[σ²(t)]/dt) dw — variance explodes. NCSN/NCSN++.
  • VP-SDE: dx = −½β(t)x dt + √β(t) dw — DDPM's continuous limit.
  • sub-VP-SDE: tighter variance, better likelihood.

Reverse: dx = [f − g² ∇log pt] dt + g dw̃. Solve with Euler-Maruyama or Probability-Flow ODE. Insight: all diffusion = SDE discretization; DDPM/NCSN/EDM are schedule choices.

⑤ Classifier-free guidance (Ho & Salimans 2022)

Train one network on both unconditional εθ(x,t,∅) and conditional εθ(x,t,c) (drop c with 10–20% prob). Sample with ε̃ = ε(∅) + w(ε(c) − ε(∅)). w > 1 (often 7.5) amplifies condition direction → sharper, more condition-faithful samples at cost of mode coverage. Used in Stable Diffusion, DALL-E 2. Biology: enhances "generate expression for given cell type" or "generate sequence for given function."

⑥ Latent diffusion (Rombach 2022, CVPR) — Stable Diffusion core

Pixel-space diffusion is expensive (512×512×3 = 786k dim). Rombach: VAE encode to 64×64×4 latent → diffuse → VAE decode. ~50× cheaper, trainable on 24 GB GPU. Cross-attention condition mechanism integrates text / labels / images. Biology analog: scVI-VAE latent + latent diffusion = scDiffusion (Luo 2024). For protein, ESM-2 encode + diffuse is the same idea.

⑦ Consistency model (Song 2023, ICML)

One-step generation: fθ(xt, t) outputs the same x0 for all points on a PF-ODE trajectory. After training, 1 (or 2–4) step generation, 50–1000× speedup. Can be distilled from pretrained diffusion. Useful for biology in-silico screen generating millions of candidate molecules.

⑧ Flow matching / CNF (Lipman 2022)

Generalizes diffusion to arbitrary conditional paths; learn velocity vθ(x,t) so ODE dx/dt = v flows Gaussian to data. More stable training, faster sampling. OT-optimal coupling gives straight trajectories. Meta's Movie Gen and protein flow-matching (FrameFlow) adopt this.

⑨ Conditional diffusion in biology

scDiffusion conditions latent diffusion on cell type and batch. Architecture: U-Net on latent + cross-attention over (cell type, batch, drug) embedding. Tricks: CFG w=3–10, sinusoidal embedding for continuous conditions (dose), 15% condition dropout during training.

⑩ EDM preconditioning (Karras 2022, NeurIPS)

Systematic design-space refactor: noise level σ as direct input; network = cskip(σ)·x + cout(σ)·Fθ(cin(σ)·x, cnoise(σ)). Equal effective signal across all σ, plus stochastic sampler with "churn." SOTA FID on CIFAR-10/ImageNet, now the standard baseline.

⑪ Discrete diffusion — EvoDiff and sequence diffusion

For DNA/RNA/protein sequence, can't use Gaussian noise. D3PM (Austin 2021), masked diffusion (LLaDA, Shi 2024): corruption masks tokens or replaces them. EvoDiff (Alamdari 2024, Nat Commun) uses Order-Agnostic Diffusion on protein sequence; can condition on structure motif.

⑫ Equivariant diffusion for 3D structure

Protein structures must be SE(3)-equivariant. RFdiffusion (Watson 2023, Nature) makes RoseTTAFold a SE(3)-equivariant denoiser. Chroma (Ingraham 2023, Nature) uses SE(3) GNN. FrameDiff (Yim 2023) diffuses rigid frames on SE(3)N. Equivariance gives generalization beyond augmentation.

詳細已知應用

擴散模型在生物資訊的具體案例

🧬 案例 1：DDPM (Ho et al. 2020, NeurIPS) — 開創性圖像

把 score-based generative model 重新公式化為 hierarchical Markov chain，引入 ε-prediction loss，在 CIFAR-10 取得當時 SOTA FID = 3.17。雖然原作不是生物，但奠定了所有後續生物 diffusion 的數學基礎。Ho 2022 接著用 cascaded diffusion 把解析度推到 1024×1024。

🧬 案例 2：scDiffusion (Luo et al. 2024, Nature Communications)

第一個完整把 latent diffusion 套到 scRNA-seq 的工作。

  • 架構：先 pre-train scVI 取 10–20 維 latent，再在 latent 上跑 U-Net diffusion；條件於 cell type、tissue、disease。
  • 實證：生成的 synthetic 細胞與真實細胞在 marker gene 表現、分群、軌跡上幾乎不可區分（discriminator AUC ~0.55）。
  • 應用：對 rare cell type augmentation、cross-tissue translation、用 limited reference 模擬大型 atlas。
  • 後續：已有 scDiff (Liu 2024)、CFGen (Palla 2024) 等多個變體。

🧬 案例 3：CFGen / scDiff — 條件 cellular state 生成的延伸

CFGen (Palla et al. 2024) 用 classifier-free guidance 強化 cell-type 條件控制；scDiff 強調對「多條件組合」（cell type × disease × batch）的解纏。兩者都顯示 CFG w > 5 時 condition 控制更精準但 diversity 下降。

🧬 案例 4：EvoDiff (Alamdari et al. 2024, Nat Commun) — 蛋白序列

對 amino acid sequence 的 discrete diffusion model。在 UniRef50（4500 萬序列）訓練，可條件生成 (a) 給定 structural motif 補完序列；(b) 給定家族生成多樣化新成員。生成的序列在 ESM-2 embedding 上落在合理 protein space，且部分經 AlphaFold 預測有可信結構。

🧬 案例 5：Chroma (Ingraham et al. 2023, Nature) — 蛋白 3D 結構生成

SE(3)-equivariant diffusion 對全長蛋白 backbone 直接生成。可條件於：(a) 對稱性（C3, D2, etc.）；(b) 子結構 motif；(c) 文字描述（"a beta barrel that binds heme"）。生成 200+ 個候選結構交予 Generate Biomedicines 實驗室合成驗證，多數可摺疊。

🧬 案例 6：RFdiffusion (Watson et al. 2023, Nature) — De novo 蛋白設計，實驗驗證

把 RoseTTAFold 結構預測網路改裝為 SE(3)-equivariant denoiser；可生成符合目標 binding interface、scaffold motif、對稱性的全新蛋白。

  • 實驗驗證：對 IL-7Rα、TrkA 等難 target 設計 binder，部分達到 nM 親和力。
  • 對稱蛋白設計：對 C2-C8 對稱性，60%+ 設計可表現可溶。
  • 影響：2023 Nature 同期 4 篇 paper 都用 RFdiffusion，啟動了「生成式蛋白工程」新時代。

🧬 案例 7：ESM-IF + diffusion 整合工作

ESM-IF (Hsu 2022) 是 inverse folding 模型——給結構預測序列。把 ESM-IF 與 RFdiffusion / Chroma 串接：先 diffusion 生成 backbone，再 ESM-IF 生成序列，再 AlphaFold 驗證 self-consistency（refold 看是否回到原 backbone）。這套 pipeline 已成標準的 de novo design workflow。

🧬 案例 8：DiffSDS (Wu et al. 2023) — 小分子設計

用 SE(3)-equivariant diffusion 直接在 3D atomic coordinates 上生成 drug-like molecules。能條件於 binding pocket、ADMET 性質。比 SMILES-based 自回歸（如 MolGPT）更尊重 3D 結構物理。延伸到 protein-ligand co-design (DiffDock 2023 做 docking、PocketGen 2024 做 binder 設計)。

🧬 案例 9：Gosai et al. 2024 (Nature) — cis-regulatory element design

對 promoter/enhancer DNA 序列做 diffusion 生成。訓練資料：MPRA 量化的 enhancer 活性。條件於目標 cell type 與目標表現強度生成全新 cis-regulatory sequence，部分在實驗中達到 native enhancer 強度——首次展示 diffusion 對「DNA regulatory grammar」的學習能力。

🧬 案例 10：Boltz / MoleculeDiffusion — 小分子家族

Boltz-1 (Wohlwend 2024) 是 AlphaFold3 開源等價物，內部用 diffusion 模組做 protein-ligand 結構預測。MoleculeDiffusion (Hoogeboom 2022) 是 E(3)-equivariant diffusion 對小分子生成。配合 score-based optimization 可做「conditional 對 ADMET / binding affinity 最佳化」。

🧬 案例 11：Cell Painting Diffusion

對 Cell Painting 高內容篩選（HCS）顯微圖像做 conditional diffusion——給定化合物 SMILES 生成預期的 cellular morphology 圖像。Recursion Pharma 2024 公布的 Phenom 模型走此路線，用於 high-throughput 藥物表型篩選。延伸用於 morphology-based MoA classification。

🧬 案例 12：Protein-ligand co-diffusion (DiffDock-PP, Plinder 等)

DiffDock (Corso 2023) 把 docking 改為 diffusion 過程：先隨機放置 ligand，再 reverse diffuse 到 binding pose。在 PDBBind benchmark 上 RMSD < 2 Å 命中率超越傳統 AutoDock / GLIDE。延伸 DiffDock-PP 對 protein-protein docking、Plinder benchmark 統一評估。

🧬 Case 1: DDPM (Ho et al. 2020, NeurIPS) — foundational

Reformulated score-based generative model as hierarchical Markov chain with ε-prediction loss. SOTA FID 3.17 on CIFAR-10 at the time. Not biology, but founded the math for all downstream biology diffusion.

🧬 Case 2: scDiffusion (Luo et al. 2024, Nature Communications)

First full latent diffusion for scRNA-seq. Pre-train scVI for 10–20 dim latent, then U-Net diffusion in latent, conditioned on cell type/tissue/disease. Synthetic cells nearly indistinguishable from real (discriminator AUC ~0.55). Applications: rare-cell augmentation, cross-tissue translation, atlas simulation from limited reference.

🧬 Case 3: CFGen / scDiff — conditional cellular state extensions

CFGen (Palla 2024) uses CFG to enhance cell-type control; scDiff disentangles multi-condition combinations. CFG w > 5 gives tighter condition control with diversity drop.

🧬 Case 4: EvoDiff (Alamdari et al. 2024, Nat Commun) — protein sequence

Discrete diffusion over amino-acid sequence, trained on UniRef50 (45 M seqs). Conditional on structure motif (motif scaffolding) or family. Generated sequences fall in plausible ESM-2 embedding space; some yield confident AlphaFold structures.

🧬 Case 5: Chroma (Ingraham et al. 2023, Nature) — 3D protein structure

SE(3)-equivariant diffusion for full-length protein backbone. Conditions: symmetry (C3, D2…), substructure motif, text description ("a beta barrel that binds heme"). 200+ generated candidates synthesized at Generate Biomedicines, most fold.

🧬 Case 6: RFdiffusion (Watson et al. 2023, Nature) — experimentally validated

RoseTTAFold turned into SE(3)-equivariant denoiser. Generates proteins for target binding interfaces, scaffold motifs, symmetry. Experimentally validated nM binders for IL-7Rα, TrkA. C2–C8 symmetric designs: 60%+ express solubly. Launched "generative protein engineering" era; same Nature issue had 4 RFdiffusion papers.

🧬 Case 7: ESM-IF + diffusion pipelines

ESM-IF (Hsu 2022) does inverse folding (structure → sequence). Standard de-novo pipeline: diffusion → backbone, ESM-IF → sequence, AlphaFold → self-consistency check (refold to original backbone).

🧬 Case 8: DiffSDS (Wu et al. 2023) — small molecules

SE(3)-equivariant diffusion on 3D atomic coords for drug-like molecules. Conditional on binding pocket, ADMET. Respects 3D physics better than SMILES autoregressive (MolGPT). Extensions: DiffDock 2023 (docking), PocketGen 2024 (binder design).

🧬 Case 9: Gosai et al. 2024 (Nature) — cis-regulatory element design

Diffusion on promoter/enhancer DNA sequence. Trained on MPRA-measured enhancer activity. Conditional on target cell type + activity strength generates novel cis-regulatory sequences, some reaching native enhancer activity experimentally — first demonstration of diffusion learning DNA regulatory grammar.

🧬 Case 10: Boltz / MoleculeDiffusion — small-molecule family

Boltz-1 (Wohlwend 2024) is open-source AlphaFold3 analog using diffusion for protein-ligand structure prediction. MoleculeDiffusion (Hoogeboom 2022) is E(3)-equivariant for small molecules. Conditional optimization on ADMET / binding affinity via score guidance.

🧬 Case 11: Cell Painting diffusion

Conditional diffusion on Cell Painting high-content microscopy: given compound SMILES, generate expected cellular morphology. Recursion's 2024 Phenom model uses this for high-throughput phenotypic screening and morphology-based MoA classification.

🧬 Case 12: Protein-ligand co-diffusion (DiffDock, Plinder)

DiffDock (Corso 2023) reframes docking as diffusion: random initial pose → reverse-diffuse to binding pose. RMSD < 2 Å hit rate beats AutoDock/GLIDE on PDBBind. Extensions: DiffDock-PP for protein-protein docking; Plinder for unified benchmarking.

潛在改良應用

擴散模型還能改良 / 新解決哪些任務？

🎯 1. 用 diffusion 生成 rare cell types 做資料增強

現有不足：稀有細胞型別（< 100 cells/sample）的下游分析（DE、軌跡）統計檢力極差；SMOTE 等簡單 oversampling 在高維 + 非線性 manifold 上效果差。新提案：對 atlas 訓練 conditional latent diffusion，條件於 rare cell type label，生成 1000s 個合成稀有細胞補足樣本。用 CFG 加強條件信號。預期改進：對下游 DE 統計檢力提升 2–5 倍；可用於 rare disease cohort 的 atlas 比對（避免 query 細胞數太少而結果隨機）。需注意「合成細胞不能用於新假設驗證，只能用於增強檢力」。

🎯 2. In-silico perturbation prediction 用 conditional diffusion

現有不足：scGen / GEARS 用 VAE / GNN 預測擾動效應，但對「組合擾動」(double KO)、「dose-response 曲線」泛化差；確定性點估計沒有不確定度。新提案：conditional latent diffusion，條件於 (cell baseline state, perturbation list, dose)，生成「擾動後的 cell state 分布」。可天然處理組合擾動（多個 condition embedding 串接）。預期改進：給出後驗分布而非點估；對未見過的組合擾動有 calibrated uncertainty；可優先排序 wet-lab Perturb-seq 候選。

🎯 3. Antibody / paratope inverse design with diffusion

現有不足：抗體設計主要靠 phage display 篩選 + AlphaFold 驗證；正向生成模型對 CDR loop 的多樣性 + functional 約束沒法兼顧。新提案：SE(3)-equivariant diffusion 在 antibody Fv 結構上 + 條件於 antigen epitope structure；同時做 backbone diffusion + ESM-IF sequence design 共學。預期改進：對給定 epitope 生成 high-affinity binder 候選，預測 KD nM 級；對 broad-neutralizing antibody 設計（HIV, influenza）有突破潛力。可整合 OpenAirView / IgBLAST 做 germline-aware design。

🎯 4. Drug-induced cellular state diffusion (sci-Plex / Tahoe-100M scale)

現有不足：LINCS L1000 用 bulk profile；藥物在不同細胞型別 / 不同基因背景下的效應未被系統建模。新提案：對 sci-Plex (Srivatsan 2020)、Tahoe-100M (Tahoe Therapeutics 2024 公布的 100M 細胞 × 數千藥物 perturbation) 訓 conditional diffusion，學 p(zperturbed | zbaseline, drug, dose)。預期改進：對新藥可預測「在任意 cell context 下的反應」；藥物 repurposing 透過 latent vector 比對；可生成「給定目標表型反推藥物」的 inverse design。

🎯 5. 空間轉錄體 imputation 與 super-resolution

現有不足：Visium 解析度 ~55 μm（多細胞 spot）；MERFISH/Xenium 雖單細胞但基因 panel 限 100–500 個；無 method 同時擴 panel 與提解析度。新提案：conditional diffusion，輸入 (low-res Visium, paired scRNA reference)，輸出 (high-res, full transcriptome)。類似圖像 super-resolution diffusion，但條件於配對資料。預期改進：從 Visium 公開資料即可獲得近 Xenium 等級的空間解析度；對 tumor microenvironment、發育 atlas 釐清極為有用；可生成 spatial multi-omics 整合圖。

🎯 6. 多模態 conditional diffusion: RNA + ATAC 共同生成

現有不足：multiVI 整合，但生成新樣本困難；無模型可從一個 modality 生成另一個 modality（給 ATAC 預測 RNA 或反之）的高保真樣本。新提案：joint diffusion in shared latent，訓練 unconditional p(zRNA, zATAC) 與 conditional p(zRNA | zATAC)、p(zATAC | zRNA)，用 CFG。預期改進：對 SHARE-seq / 10x Multiome 半配對資料，可補齊缺失 modality；對 single-modality cohort 可用 cross-modality prediction 推 chromatin landscape；提供「what if I had ATAC for this RNA-only sample」的反事實。

🎯 7. Disease state generation for rare diseases

現有不足：罕見疾病樣本極稀（部分病例全球 < 10 病人），無法訓練 disease-specific model；現有 disease atlas 都偏 common disease。新提案：對 Tabula Sapiens / CELLxGENE 大型健康 atlas 訓 unconditional diffusion，再用少量 rare disease 樣本 fine-tune 透過 LoRA / score distillation。預期改進：對 N=5 病人即可得到 disease-state-aware generative model；可生成 synthetic disease cells 供下游 ML 訓練；提供「健康→疾病」軌跡的潛在分子機制 hypothesis。

🎯 8. 跨物種 cell state translation

現有不足：SAMap、CellHint 做跨物種 cell type label transfer 但 expression-level translation（給人類細胞「翻譯」成等效小鼠細胞）沒系統方法。新提案：conditional diffusion 條件於 (cell embedding, source species, target species)，學「物種 translation」變換。可用 ortholog gene mapping 作 prior。預期改進：對藥物開發從鼠模型外推到人類效應有定量基礎；對 evolution 研究可量化「保守表現程式」vs「物種特異 program」。

🎯 9. Synthetic Perturb-seq augmentation

現有不足：Perturb-seq 對每個 gRNA 通常只獲 10–100 cells；統計檢力對細微效應極弱；可同時測的 gRNA 有限。新提案：訓 conditional diffusion p(cell | perturbation embedding)，用已測 Perturb-seq 學「擾動 → 效應」對應，對未測過的擾動（或低效應 gRNA）生成合成細胞補充。可用 Replogle 2022 genome-scale Perturb-seq 為訓練底。預期改進：降低 Perturb-seq cost / scale 2–5 倍；對組合擾動（synergy）給出先驗預測。

🎯 10. 用 score function 找 cell fate transition states

現有不足：軌跡分析（Monocle、PAGA）找 cell fate 分支點但是後驗的（fit 後找）；無 principled way 找「臨界轉變」(critical transition / saddle point)。新提案：用 score function ∇x log p(x)（diffusion 訓練的副產品）找 cell state landscape 上的 saddle point：score 在 saddle 附近梯度小但 Hessian 有負特徵值。標記這些點為「決策點」。預期改進：定量找出 fate-determining transition states，提供生物實驗（time-lapse imaging、lineage tracing）的精準目標時點。

🎯 11. Diffusion-based ChIP-seq peak generation

現有不足：ChIP-seq 對少量細胞或 hard-to-ChIP factor 訊號弱；Cut&Tag、Cut&Run 有解但對 cell type 解析仍粗。新提案：用 sequence-conditioned diffusion 對 (DNA sequence, TF identity, cell type) 預測 ChIP-seq signal track。訓練於 ENCODE 等大規模 ChIP atlas。預期改進：對未測 TF / 罕見 cell type 預測 binding landscape；對 cancer-specific oncogenic TF（如某些 fusion）的 ChIP 模擬；可作為 GRN 推論的 prior。

🎯 12. 用 diffusion 學細胞 trajectory 的 stochastic 模型

現有不足：RNA velocity 給瞬時動力學但是確定性的（單一向量場）；scVelo dynamic model 加 noise 但僅 per-gene Gaussian。新提案：用 latent SDE 形式的 diffusion 直接學 cell state trajectory 的 drift μ(z) + diffusion σ(z)，得到全分布級的軌跡預測。預期改進：對 lineage commitment 給機率分布（不是單一 fate）；對 rare branching event 量化機率；自然處理 multi-modal 軌跡（一個前驅多個終態）。

🎯 1. Rare-cell-type augmentation via diffusion

Gap: rare cell types (< 100 cells/sample) suffer from poor statistical power; SMOTE-style oversampling fails on high-dim non-linear manifolds. Proposal: conditional latent diffusion on atlas, generate 1000s of synthetic rare cells with CFG-enhanced conditioning. Expected gain: 2–5× DE power; safer rare-disease atlas matching. Caveat: synthetic cells must augment power, not test new hypotheses.

🎯 2. In-silico perturbation prediction with conditional diffusion

Gap: scGen / GEARS give point estimates without uncertainty; struggle on combinatorial perturbations and dose-response. Proposal: conditional diffusion on (baseline, perturbation list, dose) → post-perturbation distribution. Naturally handles combinatorial via concatenated condition embeddings. Expected gain: calibrated posterior; prioritize wet-lab Perturb-seq candidates.

🎯 3. Antibody / paratope inverse design with diffusion

Gap: antibody design relies on phage display + AlphaFold; no forward model balances CDR diversity with functional constraints. Proposal: SE(3)-equivariant diffusion on Fv structure conditioned on epitope; joint backbone diffusion + ESM-IF sequence. Expected gain: nM-affinity binder candidates for given epitope; broadly-neutralizing antibody design (HIV, flu); germline-aware via OpenAirView/IgBLAST.

🎯 4. Drug-induced cellular state diffusion (sci-Plex / Tahoe-100M scale)

Gap: LINCS L1000 is bulk; cell-type-specific drug effects unmodeled. Proposal: conditional diffusion on sci-Plex / Tahoe-100M learning p(zperturbed | zbaseline, drug, dose). Expected gain: predict response in any cell context; drug repurposing via latent similarity; target-phenotype → drug inverse design.

🎯 5. Spatial transcriptomics imputation & super-resolution

Gap: Visium is ~55 μm; MERFISH/Xenium is single-cell but limited gene panel; nothing extends both. Proposal: conditional diffusion (low-res Visium + paired scRNA ref) → (high-res, full transcriptome). Like image super-resolution. Expected gain: public Visium gives near-Xenium resolution; great for TME, developmental atlases; enables spatial multi-omics.

🎯 6. Multimodal conditional diffusion (RNA + ATAC)

Gap: multiVI integrates but doesn't generate; cross-modality high-fidelity prediction missing. Proposal: joint diffusion in shared latent + CFG over modality identity. Expected gain: impute missing modality in SHARE-seq / Multiome; cross-modality prediction for RNA-only cohorts to recover chromatin landscape.

🎯 7. Disease-state generation for rare diseases

Gap: rare diseases (sometimes < 10 patients globally) can't train disease-specific models. Proposal: pretrain unconditional diffusion on Tabula Sapiens / CELLxGENE, fine-tune with few-shot LoRA / score distillation on rare cohort. Expected gain: disease-aware generative model from N=5; synthetic cells for downstream ML; hypothesis on health→disease trajectory.

🎯 8. Cross-species cell-state translation

Gap: SAMap / CellHint do cross-species label transfer; expression-level translation (human → equivalent mouse cell) lacks systematic method. Proposal: diffusion conditional on (cell, source species, target species); ortholog mapping as prior. Expected gain: quantitative basis for mouse → human drug extrapolation; evolution: quantify conserved vs species-specific programs.

🎯 9. Synthetic Perturb-seq augmentation

Gap: Perturb-seq has 10–100 cells per gRNA; low power for subtle effects; gRNA capacity limited. Proposal: train diffusion p(cell | perturbation embedding) on Replogle 2022 etc., augment for untested gRNAs. Expected gain: 2–5× cost reduction; prior predictions for combinatorial synergy.

🎯 10. Fate-transition state discovery via score function

Gap: Monocle / PAGA find branches post hoc; no principled saddle-point (critical transition) detection. Proposal: use diffusion's learned ∇x log p(x); saddles have small gradient + negative-eigenvalue Hessian. Mark as "decision points." Expected gain: quantitative fate-determining transition states for time-lapse imaging / lineage tracing targeting.

🎯 11. Diffusion-based ChIP-seq peak generation

Gap: ChIP-seq is weak in few cells / hard-to-ChIP factors; Cut&Tag / Cut&Run improve but cell-type resolution is coarse. Proposal: sequence-conditioned diffusion on (DNA, TF, cell type) → ChIP signal track, trained on ENCODE atlas. Expected gain: binding prediction for untested TF / rare cell types; cancer-specific fusion TF simulation; GRN inference prior.

🎯 12. Stochastic trajectory model via diffusion

Gap: RNA velocity is deterministic; scVelo dynamic adds per-gene Gaussian only. Proposal: latent SDE diffusion learning drift μ(z) + diffusion σ(z) of cell trajectories. Expected gain: probabilistic lineage commitment (not single fate); quantify rare branching events; naturally handles multi-modal trajectories (one progenitor → many fates).

import torch, torch.nn as nn
T = 1000
betas = torch.linspace(1e-4, 0.02, T)
alphas = 1 - betas; abar = torch.cumprod(alphas, dim=0)

def forward_diff(x0, t):
    eps = torch.randn_like(x0)
    return torch.sqrt(abar[t]) * x0 + torch.sqrt(1-abar[t]) * eps, eps

# 訓練 loss
def loss_fn(model, x0):
    t = torch.randint(0, T, (x0.size(0),))
    xt, eps = forward_diff(x0, t)
    eps_pred = model(xt, t)
    return ((eps - eps_pred)**2).mean()
