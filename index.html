進階方法演算法 互動式教學

收錄不會出現在教科書、但實際做 scRNA-seq / 空間轉錄體 / 多體學 / scLLMs 時很有殺傷力的數據方法與演算法——從 Chatterjee 相關性到 scLLM tokenization，14 個主題，附互動模擬與程式碼。

A toolbox of advanced (often non-textbook) methods and algorithms for scRNA-seq, spatial transcriptomics, multi-omics, and scLLMs — from Chatterjee correlation to scLLM tokenization. 14 topics with interactive simulations and code examples.

01 現代相關性與依賴性偵測

🔀
Ch 1

Chatterjee 相關係數

2021 年提出的新型秩相關係數，能偵測任意函數依賴關係——線性、非單調、振盪都看得到。

A 2021 rank-based correlation that detects any functional dependency — linear, non-monotonic, oscillatory alike.
🌀
Ch 2

HSIC 與 dCor

基於核方法與能量距離的獨立性檢定，可推廣到高維與多模態變數。

Kernel-based and energy-distance independence tests, generalizable to high-dimensional and multimodal variables.
📡
Ch 3

互資訊估計

KSG、MINE、PIDC——非參數與神經網路兩派估計法，GRN 推論的關鍵工具。

KSG, MINE, PIDC — non-parametric and neural estimators, key tools for GRN inference.

02 最佳傳輸與拓撲方法

🚚
Ch 4

最佳傳輸 (OT)

Sinkhorn 演算法、Wasserstein 距離、Waddington-OT、MOSCOT——時間序列與空間對齊的數學基礎。

Sinkhorn algorithm, Wasserstein distance, Waddington-OT, MOSCOT — the math behind temporal and spatial alignment.
🍩
Ch 5

持久同調 (TDA)

用拓撲不變量描述細胞狀態空間的「形狀」——孔洞、迴圈、分支結構一覽無遺。

Use topological invariants to describe the "shape" of cell-state space — voids, loops, and branches revealed.
🗺️
Ch 6

進階流形學習

Diffusion maps、PHATE、MDE——比 UMAP/t-SNE 更忠實保留全局結構與軌跡。

Diffusion maps, PHATE, MDE — more faithful to global structure and trajectories than UMAP/t-SNE.

03 動力學與軌跡

🏎️
Ch 7

RNA 速度與動力系統

spliced/unspliced 比值揭示細胞狀態瞬時方向，scVelo 與 Dynamo 推估向量場與 ODE。

Spliced/unspliced ratios reveal instantaneous cell-state direction. scVelo and Dynamo reconstruct vector fields and ODEs.
🔁
Ch 8

循環追蹤

細胞週期、晝夜節律、振盪基因——非單向軌跡的循環假時間，Tricycle、Cyclum、reCAT。

Cell cycle, circadian rhythm, oscillatory genes — circular pseudotime for non-monotonic trajectories. Tricycle, Cyclum, reCAT.

🕸️
Ch 9

圖訊號與 GNN

Laplacian 譜分析、GCN/GAT、SpaGCN、STAGATE——把細胞–細胞關係與空間鄰近結構納入模型。

Laplacian spectral analysis, GCN/GAT, SpaGCN, STAGATE — encode cell–cell and spatial neighborhood structure.
📍
Ch 10

空間自相關與 GP

Moran's I、Geary's C、SpatialDE、SPARK、nnSVG、BayesSpace——空間變異基因的統計骨幹。

Moran's I, Geary's C, SpatialDE, SPARK, nnSVG, BayesSpace — the statistical backbone of spatially variable genes.

Ch 11

變分推論與 VAE

ELBO、重參數技巧、scVI/totalVI/MultiVI——多體學整合與計數模型的機率骨架。

ELBO, reparameterization, scVI/totalVI/MultiVI — the probabilistic backbone of multi-omics integration and count modeling.

Ch 12

擴散模型

DDPM、score-based、scDiffusion、EvoDiff——從圖像生成走進細胞與序列生成。

DDPM, score-based, scDiffusion, EvoDiff — from image generation to cell and sequence generation.

Ch 13

scGPT, Geneformer, scFoundation — tokenization strategies, pretraining objectives, zero-shot capabilities.

06 因果推論

⚖️
Ch 14

單細胞因果推論

DAG、do-calculus、Perturb-seq、CausalCell——從相關走向干預。

DAGs, do-calculus, Perturb-seq, CausalCell — moving beyond correlation to intervention.

07 退使雙曲與連續模型

🌐
Ch 15

退使雙曲嵌入

Poincaré disk 等負曲率空間天然容納樹/階層——細胞型別 ontology、phylogeny、分化階層的最佳幾何。

Negative-curvature spaces (Poincaré disk) natively host hierarchies — best geometry for cell-type ontology, phylogeny, lineage trees.
🧮
Ch 16

Neural ODE

把神經網路視為連續動力系統，scTour、Dynamo 背後的數學核心，適合連續細胞軌跡建模。

Networks as continuous dynamical systems — backbone of scTour, Dynamo; perfect for continuous cell trajectories.
🌊
Ch 17

Flow Matching

2022 後 diffusion 的更簡單對手，AlphaFlow、FrameFlow 在蛋白質結構設計大放異彩。

Simpler post-2022 alternative to diffusion. AlphaFlow, FrameFlow shine in protein structure design.
📜
Ch 18

State Space / Mamba

Transformer 的長序列接班者。HyenaDNA、Mamba-DNA 處理 1M bp 基因組序列，效率遠超 Transformer。

Transformer's long-sequence successor. HyenaDNA, Mamba-DNA handle 1M-bp genome sequences far more efficiently.

08 不確定性、實驗設計、表徵原理

🎯
Ch 19

保適預測

分布無關的不確定性量化——對臨床決策、藥物反應預測、cell type annotation 有覆蓋率保證的關鍵工具。

Distribution-free uncertainty with coverage guarantees — critical for clinical decisions, drug response, cell-type annotation.
🔬
Ch 20

主動學習 / 貝氏最佳化

指導實驗選擇——下一個 CRISPR perturbation 要打哪？下一個藥物 candidate 試哪個劑量？

Guide experiment selection — which CRISPR target next? Which drug dose to try? Bayesian optimization rules.
🌐
Ch 21

資訊瓶頸

Tishby 表徵學習原理——讓 latent 只保留「與任務相關」訊息。IB-VAE 給 disentangled 與可解釋表徵。

Tishby's representation principle — latents keep only "task-relevant" info. IB-VAE delivers disentangled, interpretable embeddings.
🧊
Ch 22

張量分解

CP / Tucker / TT——個人 × 細胞 × 基因 × 時間 4D 資料的原生語言，MOFA、LIGER、Tensor-cell2cell 都是。

CP / Tucker / TT — native language for 4D data (person × cell × gene × time). MOFA, LIGER, Tensor-cell2cell.

09 前沿表徵與幾何

🔀
Ch 23

自監督對比學習

SimCLR / MoCo / BYOL / VICReg / CLIP——scLLM 預訓練的關鍵 trick，scCLIP、Concerto 等用來學跨模態表徵。

SimCLR / MoCo / BYOL / VICReg / CLIP — key trick behind scLLM pretraining. scCLIP, Concerto for cross-modal embeddings.
🔣
Ch 24

符號回歸

從資料反推「方程式」——SINDy、PySR 從 scRNA-seq 軌跡發現 ODE，從 GWAS 反推遺傳互動式。

Reverse-engineer "equations" from data — SINDy, PySR discover ODEs from trajectories, infer epistasis from GWAS.
📐
Ch 25

Sheaf 與細胞 sheaf

廣義拓撲——處理「多模態間不一致對應」的數學工具，sheaf Laplacian 在 multi-omics 與空間整合中興起。

Generalized topology — math for "inconsistent multi-modal correspondences." Sheaf Laplacian rising in multi-omics and spatial.
🧬
Ch 26

幾何深度學習

SE(3)-equivariant、Riemannian、Lie group 神經網路——蛋白質、分子、3D 結構設計的中心。AlphaFold3 的根。

SE(3)-equivariant, Riemannian, Lie group nets — center of protein, molecule, 3D structure design. The root of AlphaFold3.

Ramsauer 2021 showed attention ≡ modern Hopfield. HopfieldRepertoire, DeepRC use it for TCR/BCR immune repertoire retrieval.

Direct Coupling Analysis is the root of AlphaFold's MSA-based contact prediction. Forecast protein contacts, binding sites.

Ch 29

Hawkes 點過程

自激發點過程——體細胞突變時序、神經元 spike、免疫激活、CRISPR 變異事件微規。

Self-exciting point processes — somatic mutation timing, neural spikes, immune activation, CRISPR variant micro-events.

Ch 30

RL for Bio

REINVENT 藥物設計、蛋白質設計、藥物雞金優化、多步實驗認接。

REINVENT for drug design, protein design, drug dose optimization, multi-step experiment guidance.

11 幾何、多尺度、稀疏

🎲
Ch 31

Random Matrix Theory

Marchenko-Pastur 定理——GRN 推論中的「噪訊 vs 真信號」門檻。Eigengene、MEFISTO、CovEdit 背後數學。

Marchenko-Pastur theorem — the "noise vs signal" threshold in GRN inference. Math behind Eigengene, MEFISTO, CovEdit.
🚄
Ch 32

Sliced-W / Fast OT

「只算一維投影的 OT」——比 Sinkhorn 快 100×，適合 atlas-scale single-cell。SW-AE、SW-PD 都是。

"OT via 1D projections" — 100× faster than Sinkhorn, fits atlas-scale single-cell. SW-AE, SW-PD.
📡
Ch 33

壓縮感測

L1 稀疏恢復。Cell painting、MERFISH、CITE-seq 以少量探針重建完整表現裝照。LASSO-genetic 也是。

L1 sparse recovery. Cell painting, MERFISH, CITE-seq reconstruct full profiles from few probes. LASSO-genetic too.
〰️
Ch 34

小波與多尺度

多尺度時頻分析。細胞週期、晝夜週心、信號學、spatial multi-scale hot 結構偏移。

Multi-scale time-frequency analysis. Cell cycle, circadian, neuroscience, spatial multi-scale hot structures.

Ch 35

隱式神經表徵

Continuous coordinate→value functions. cryo-EM reconstruction, continuous spatial transcriptomics upscaling.

Sample "diverse and non-redundant" subsets — marker selection, multi-modal panel design, active-learning sampling.

Ch 37

聯邦學習

Privacy-preserving multi-hospital prediction (no raw data sharing). BiogenoFL has gained traction recently.
🕸️
Ch 38

超圖學習

「一條邊接多個點」的推廣 — 適合 multi-omics、藥物 × 規復× 基因、L-R complex (multimer signaling)。

"One edge connects many nodes" generalization — fits multi-omics, drug × pathway × gene, L-R complex (multimer signaling).
