Normalizing Flows 正規化流

用一連串可逆 (invertible) 神經網路把簡單分布（如 Gaussian）變換成複雜的目標分布——一個能「精確算出 likelihood」的生成模型。從 RealNVP、Glow、MAF 到連續流 FFJORD，再到 Boltzmann Generators 在分子物理的革命性應用。

Use a stack of invertible neural networks to morph a simple distribution (e.g. Gaussian) into a complex target — a generative model with exact likelihood. From RealNVP, Glow, MAF to continuous FFJORD, and Boltzmann Generators revolutionising molecular physics.

核心概念

變數變換與可逆網路

基本想法：給定一個簡單分布 z ~ p_Z（通常是 N(0, I)），透過一個 可逆函數 x = f(z) 把它推到目標分布 p_X。透過變數變換公式（change of variables）：

p_X(x) = p_Z(f−1(x)) · |det(∂f−1/∂x)|

或反過來寫：log p_X(x) = log p_Z(z) − log |det(∂f/∂z)|，其中 z = f−1(x)。

關鍵：f 必須是 可逆 (bijective) 且 Jacobian determinant 可以高效計算。一般神經網路不滿足這兩個條件，所以 normalizing flows 的所有研究都圍繞「如何設計能滿足 invertibility 且 Jacobian 易算的網路」展開。

訓練：直接做 maximum likelihood——對訓練資料 x，計算 log p_X(x) = log p_Z(f−1(x)) − log|det J|，然後梯度上升即可。不像 VAE 那樣需要 ELBO bound，也不像 GAN 那樣需要 discriminator。

採樣：z ~ N(0, I) 然後 x = f(z)。一次前向就 OK。

Core idea: given a simple base p_Z (typically N(0, I)), push it through an invertible function x = f(z) to land at the target p_X. By change of variables:

p_X(x) = p_Z(f−1(x)) · |det(∂f−1/∂x)|

Equivalently log p_X(x) = log p_Z(z) − log|det(∂f/∂z)| with z = f−1(x).

The catch: f must be bijective and its Jacobian determinant must be efficiently computable. Vanilla NNs satisfy neither — hence all normalizing-flow research designs networks that do.

Training: direct maximum likelihood — for data x, compute log p_X(x) = log p_Z(f−1(x)) − log|det J|, then gradient ascent. No ELBO bound (unlike VAE), no discriminator (unlike GAN).

Sampling: z ~ N(0, I), then x = f(z). One forward pass.

🎯

精確 likelihood

不像 VAE 只有 ELBO 下界、也不像 diffusion 算間接 score——NF 直接給出精確 log p(x)。

Unlike VAE (ELBO bound) or diffusion (indirect score) — NF gives exact log p(x) directly.

🔄

雙向都快

訓練（密度估計）與採樣兩個方向都用同一個網路，設計得當兩邊都 O(N)。

Training (density estimation) and sampling use the same network — both O(N) when designed well.

📐

可逆 Jacobian

所有 flow 設計的本質：找出 Jacobian determinant 可在 O(d) 或 O(d²) 內算出的可逆變換。

The essence of flow design: invertible transforms whose Jacobian determinant computes in O(d) or O(d²).

演算法

主要流模型架構

① RealNVP (Dinh et al. 2017, ICLR) — Coupling Layers：把輸入 x 切成兩半 (x_A, x_B)：

y_A = x_A,   y_B = x_B ⊙ exp(s(x_A)) + t(x_A)

其中 s, t 是任意神經網路（不需可逆）。Jacobian 是 三角矩陣，determinant = exp(Σ s(x_A))——O(d) 算出！堆疊多層並交替哪一半被變換。

② Glow (Kingma & Dhariwal 2018, NeurIPS)：RealNVP + 可逆 1×1 convolution 取代固定的 channel-shuffle，讓「哪些 channel 進 s/t」是可學的。配上 ActNorm（data-dependent affine）。圖像生成達到 GAN 級品質且 likelihood 精確。

③ MAF (Masked Autoregressive Flow, Papamakarios et al. 2017, NeurIPS)：用 autoregressive 結構：

x_i = z_i · exp(α_i(x_<i)) + μ_i(x_<i)

Jacobian 是三角，determinant easy。密度估計快（一次 forward 用 MADE 即可），但採樣慢（需 d 步串行）。

④ IAF (Inverse Autoregressive Flow, Kingma et al. 2016, NeurIPS)：把 MAF 的方向反過來——z_i 是 condition 變數：

x_i = z_i · exp(α_i(z_<i)) + μ_i(z_<i)

採樣快、密度估計慢。常作為 VAE 的 posterior 改良。

⑤ Neural Spline Flows (Durkan et al. 2019, NeurIPS)：把 coupling 的 affine 變換換成 monotonic rational-quadratic spline——表達力大幅提升，仍保持可逆與 tractable Jacobian。目前 tabular density estimation 的 SOTA 之一。

⑥ FFJORD (Grathwohl et al. 2019, ICLR) — 連續流：把離散 flow 推廣為 Neural ODE：dx/dt = v_θ(x, t)。Jacobian determinant 用 Hutchinson trace estimator + adjoint method 估計。連續、無 coupling 限制、表達力最強，但訓練慢（要解 ODE）。Flow Matching 後續就是 FFJORD 的 score-based 簡化版。

① RealNVP (Dinh et al. 2017, ICLR) — Coupling Layers: split input x = (x_A, x_B):

y_A = x_A,   y_B = x_B ⊙ exp(s(x_A)) + t(x_A)

where s, t are arbitrary NNs (no invertibility needed). Jacobian is triangular, determinant = exp(Σ s(x_A)) — O(d). Stack many layers, alternating which half is transformed.

② Glow (Kingma & Dhariwal 2018, NeurIPS): RealNVP + invertible 1×1 convolution replaces fixed channel shuffle, making "which channels feed s/t" learnable. Plus ActNorm (data-dependent affine). Reaches GAN-level image quality with exact likelihood.

③ MAF (Masked Autoregressive Flow, Papamakarios et al. 2017, NeurIPS): autoregressive structure:

x_i = z_i · exp(α_i(x_<i)) + μ_i(x_<i)

Triangular Jacobian. Fast density estimation (one MADE pass), slow sampling (d serial steps).

④ IAF (Inverse Autoregressive Flow, Kingma et al. 2016, NeurIPS): reverse MAF — z_i as conditioning:

x_i = z_i · exp(α_i(z_<i)) + μ_i(z_<i)

Fast sampling, slow density. Often used to improve VAE posteriors.

⑤ Neural Spline Flows (Durkan et al. 2019, NeurIPS): replace coupling's affine map with monotonic rational-quadratic splines — far higher expressivity, still invertible with tractable Jacobian. State of the art for tabular density.

⑥ FFJORD (Grathwohl et al. 2019, ICLR) — Continuous flow: generalise discrete flow to a Neural ODE: dx/dt = v_θ(x, t). Jacobian determinant via Hutchinson trace estimator + adjoint method. Continuous, no coupling restriction, maximum expressivity — but slow (ODE solver). Flow Matching is the score-free successor.

方向性權衡：MAF 訓練快採樣慢；IAF 採樣快訓練慢；coupling (RealNVP/Glow) 兩邊都對稱；FFJORD 兩邊都慢但最有表達力。實務選擇取決於主要任務（生成？密度估計？兩者？）。
Direction trade-off: MAF — train fast, sample slow. IAF — sample fast, train slow. Coupling (RealNVP/Glow) — both symmetric. FFJORD — both slow but most expressive. Choice depends on the main task (generate? estimate density? both?).

生物應用

Normalizing Flows 在生物資訊與分子科學

Boltzmann Generators

Noé et al. (2019, Science) 用 normalizing flows 直接從 Boltzmann 分布 p(x) ∝ exp(−U(x)/kT) 取樣——避免 MD/MCMC 的 ergodicity 瓶頸。應用於蛋白質 conformational sampling，可在毫秒級得到 MD 需要毫秒~秒級的 ensemble。

Noé et al. (2019, Science) sample directly from the Boltzmann distribution p(x) ∝ exp(−U(x)/kT) using normalizing flows — bypassing the ergodicity bottleneck of MD/MCMC. Applied to protein conformational sampling, milliseconds to obtain what MD needs ms–s for.

scVI-flow / 後驗改良

原始 scVI (Lopez 2018, Nat Methods) 用 mean-field VI；後續工作把 IAF 接在 encoder 後做更靈活的 posterior q(z|x)，提升 scRNA-seq 雜訊建模精度，特別是 batch correction 的不確定性估計。

Original scVI (Lopez 2018, Nat Methods) used mean-field VI; later work stacks IAF on the encoder for richer q(z|x), improving scRNA-seq noise modelling and uncertainty estimates in batch correction.

細胞流形學習

Sokoła et al. (2020) 等工作將 NF 用於估計 single-cell 表達空間的真實 manifold 密度——比 KDE 與 GMM 更有彈性，可用來偵測 rare cell state（低密度區域）。

Sokoła et al. (2020) and others apply NF to estimate the true manifold density of single-cell expression — more flexible than KDE/GMM. Useful for detecting rare cell states (low-density regions).

分子生成

MoFlow (Zang & Wang 2020, KDD)、GraphNVP (Madhawa 2019) 把 normalizing flows 用於分子圖生成，同時生成 atom types 與 bond structures。後續被 diffusion 與 flow matching 部分取代，但在「小資料、需要精確 likelihood」場景仍很常用。

MoFlow (Zang & Wang 2020, KDD), GraphNVP (Madhawa 2019) apply NF to molecular graph generation — atom types and bonds jointly. Largely succeeded by diffusion / flow matching, but still useful where small data and exact likelihood matter.

蛋白質構象 sampling

FlowSite (2023)、Timewarp (Klein 2023, NeurIPS) 等用 NF 替 MD 加速採樣 protein/peptide ensemble。對 IDR、cryptic pocket、allosteric site 的構象變化特別有用。

FlowSite (2023), Timewarp (Klein 2023, NeurIPS) accelerate sampling of protein/peptide ensembles. Especially useful for IDRs, cryptic pockets, allosteric sites where conformational change matters.

罕見變異偵測

用 NF 在 large cohort 中學「正常 variant 分布」，然後在新樣本上計算 log p——低密度區即為 anomaly。比 GMM-based 方法更能捕捉高維非高斯結構，已用於 cancer rare variant calling。

NF learns the "normal variant distribution" across a large cohort; new samples with low log p are anomalies. More expressive than GMM for high-dimensional non-Gaussian structure. Applied to cancer rare-variant calling.

質譜光譜校準

MS data 跨批次有複雜的非線性 shift；用 conditional NF 學「parent batch → reference」的映射做 normalization，比傳統 ComBat / loess 更精準保留生物變異。

MS data has complex non-linear batch shifts; conditional NF learns "batch → reference" mappings for normalization, preserving biological variance better than ComBat / loess.

SE(3) NF

EquiFlow (2024) 等用 SE(3)-equivariant normalizing flow 設計蛋白質 backbone——保留旋轉/平移等變性，採樣分布始終物理上合理。是 FrameFlow / RFdiffusion 的 likelihood-based 替代。

EquiFlow (2024) and others use SE(3)-equivariant NF for protein backbone — preserves rotational/translational equivariance, samples are always physically valid. A likelihood-based alternative to FrameFlow / RFdiffusion.

深入解析

Normalizing Flows 進階主題

① Jacobian determinant 計算的關鍵

三角 Jacobian → determinant = 對角線連乘（O(d)）；coupling 的「上三角分塊」也是 O(d)。FFJORD 用 Hutchinson trace estimator：tr(A) ≈ E[v^T A v]，把 trace 換成 vector-Jacobian product，O(d) 完成。所有 flow 設計都圍繞「讓 Jacobian determinant 不變成 O(d³)」。

② 表達力 (expressivity) 的限制

RealNVP/Glow coupling 是局部 affine，理論上可逼近任意密度但需要極多層。Neural Spline Flows 用 spline 把每層表達力大幅提升。Universal approximation: Huang et al. (2018) 證明 NAF (Neural Autoregressive Flow) 可逼近任意連續密度。

③ 連續流的數值穩定性

FFJORD 訓練要解 ODE，且 Jacobian 需積分 ∫₀¹ tr(∂v/∂x) dt。常見問題：(a) trajectory 太彎（採樣慢），(b) Hutchinson estimator 高方差。Flow Matching (Lipman 2022) 用 conditional regression 直接學 velocity，避開這些問題——但代價是 likelihood 不再精確。

④ NF vs Diffusion vs Flow Matching

  • NF：精確 likelihood、可逆網路、表達力受架構限制。
  • Diffusion：score-based、無限制網路、likelihood 是 ELBO 下界。
  • Flow Matching：regression on velocity、無限制網路、likelihood 需積分得到（仍精確但要算）。

當你需要「給定 x 算精確 log p(x)」的場景（如 Bayes factor、anomaly detection、physics constraint），NF 仍是首選。

⑤ 離散資料的 NF

Categorical NF (Tran 2019)、Argmax flows (Hoogeboom 2021) 處理離散資料：先 dequantize 為連續變數、用連續 flow、再離散化（或 argmax）。對 DNA/protein sequence、SMILES 都適用。

⑥ Conditional NF

把條件 c 喂進 coupling 的 s/t 網路：y_B = x_B ⊙ exp(s(x_A, c)) + t(x_A, c)。可做 conditional generation（給標籤生成）、conditional density estimation（p(x|c)）、imputation 等。

⑦ 訓練實務

  • 初始化：ActNorm 用 data-dependent init 把初始輸出方差化為 1。
  • 梯度 clipping：Jacobian determinant 的 log 可能爆炸，clip 必要。
  • 架構深度：2D toy 6–12 層；圖像 ~30 層 (Glow)；分子 8–16 層 coupling。
  • Mixed precision：NF 對 fp16 比 diffusion 敏感（Jacobian 數值穩定性），通常用 bf16 或 fp32。

① The key: Jacobian determinant computation

Triangular Jacobian → determinant = product of diagonals (O(d)); coupling's block-triangular form is also O(d). FFJORD uses Hutchinson trace estimator: tr(A) ≈ E[v^T A v] — trace via vector-Jacobian product in O(d). All NF design centres on "keep Jacobian determinant out of O(d³) territory".

② Expressivity limits

RealNVP/Glow coupling is locally affine — theoretically universal but needs many layers. Neural Spline Flows boost per-layer expressivity dramatically. Universal approximation: Huang et al. (2018) proved NAF (Neural Autoregressive Flow) is a universal density approximator.

③ Numerical stability of continuous flow

FFJORD training solves an ODE plus integrates ∫₀¹ tr(∂v/∂x) dt. Common issues: (a) trajectory too curved (slow sampling), (b) high-variance Hutchinson estimator. Flow Matching (Lipman 2022) avoids both with direct regression — at the cost of losing exact likelihood.

④ NF vs Diffusion vs Flow Matching

  • NF: exact likelihood, invertible network, expressivity bound by architecture.
  • Diffusion: score-based, unrestricted network, likelihood is an ELBO lower bound.
  • Flow Matching: velocity regression, unrestricted network, likelihood obtained via integration (still exact, but to be computed).

When you genuinely need exact log p(x) (Bayes factors, anomaly detection, physics constraints), NF remains the first choice.

⑤ Discrete data via NF

Categorical NF (Tran 2019), Argmax flows (Hoogeboom 2021) handle discrete data: dequantize to continuous, apply continuous flow, then discretize (or argmax). Works for DNA/protein sequences, SMILES.

⑥ Conditional NF

Feed condition c into the coupling s/t nets: y_B = x_B ⊙ exp(s(x_A, c)) + t(x_A, c). Enables conditional generation (label→sample), conditional density estimation p(x|c), imputation, etc.

⑦ Training tips

  • Init: ActNorm uses data-dependent init so the initial output has unit variance.
  • Gradient clipping: log|det J| can explode; clip generously.
  • Depth: 6–12 layers for 2D toys, ~30 for images (Glow), 8–16 coupling layers for molecules.
  • Mixed precision: NF is more sensitive to fp16 than diffusion (Jacobian stability). Prefer bf16 or fp32.
潛在改良應用

還能改良哪些任務？

🎯 1. Bayesian variant calling 的精確 posterior

多數 variant caller (GATK, DeepVariant) 用 EM 或粗略的 calibration。提案：用 NF 學每個 locus 的 read-count posterior，能給出精確的 variant probability，特別有助於 mosaic / low-VAF variant。

🎯 2. 蛋白質 conformational ensemble

Boltzmann Generators 已開先例，但只在 small peptide。提案：把 SE(3)-equivariant NF + AlphaFold prior 整合，做 protein ensemble 採樣，目標是 IDR、allosteric pocket、cryptic site 的構象多樣性。

🎯 3. 單細胞 anomaly detection

scRNA-seq 中的「異常細胞」（doublet、感染、應激）通常在密度低區。提案：用 NF 對全部 atlas 學密度，新樣本中 log p 最低的 1% 標為候選 anomaly，比 doublet detection method 更全面。

🎯 4. 多體學 joint density

用 conditional NF 學 p(RNA | ATAC) 或 p(protein | RNA)——精確 likelihood 讓我們可做 modality imputation 與 missingness reasoning。提案：OmniNF 多體學整合框架。

🎯 5. 罕見疾病 phenotype 模型

罕病患者表型分布通常極窄；常規 supervised learning 容易過擬合。提案：用 conditional NF 學 p(phenotype | genotype)，給每個變異一個 phenotype 不確定性區間，特別有助 trio 分析。

🎯 6. CRISPR off-target 精確 likelihood

off-target probability 目前用 MIT score 或 CFD score。提案：用 NF 學 guide-target binding energy 的條件分布，給出精確 off-target 機率而非啟發式 score。

🎯 7. Mass spec 跨平台校準

Orbitrap、TOF、Q-Exactive 等 MS 平台的光譜形狀差異複雜。提案：conditional NF 學「source platform → target platform」mapping，比 ComBat / harmonization 保留更多原生峰結構。

🎯 8. Hi-C contact map 密度模型

Hi-C 的接觸頻率有強尾分布（power law）。提案：NF 模擬 contact frequency distribution，用於 differential interaction detection 與 rare contact identification。

🎯 1. Exact Bayesian posterior for variant calling

Most callers (GATK, DeepVariant) rely on EM or coarse calibration. Proposal: NF learns per-locus read-count posterior — exact variant probability, especially helpful for mosaic / low-VAF variants.

🎯 2. Protein conformational ensembles

Boltzmann Generators set the precedent but only for small peptides. Proposal: combine SE(3)-equivariant NF + AlphaFold prior for protein ensemble sampling — targeting IDR, allosteric, cryptic conformational diversity.

🎯 3. Single-cell anomaly detection

"Aberrant cells" (doublets, infected, stressed) often live in low-density regions. Proposal: NF density over the whole atlas; flag bottom-1% log p as anomalies — more general than doublet-specific methods.

🎯 4. Multi-omics joint density

Conditional NF for p(RNA | ATAC) or p(protein | RNA) — exact likelihood enables modality imputation and missingness reasoning. Proposal: OmniNF, a multi-omics integration framework.

🎯 5. Rare disease phenotype modelling

Phenotype distributions are extremely narrow for rare diseases; supervised models overfit. Proposal: conditional NF for p(phenotype | genotype) — phenotype uncertainty intervals per variant, especially valuable for trios.

🎯 6. Exact CRISPR off-target probability

Off-target scores (MIT, CFD) are heuristic. Proposal: NF on guide-target binding energy distributions — exact off-target probability instead of a heuristic score.

🎯 7. Cross-platform mass-spec calibration

Orbitrap, TOF, Q-Exactive spectra differ non-linearly. Proposal: conditional NF learns "source platform → target" mapping — preserves more native peak structure than ComBat / harmonization.

🎯 8. Hi-C contact-map density

Hi-C contact frequencies follow power-law tails. Proposal: NF for contact frequency distribution — for differential interaction detection and rare-contact identification.

import torch, torch.nn as nn

class Coupling(nn.Module):
    def __init__(self, d=2, h=64, mask_type=0):
        super().__init__()
        self.mask = torch.tensor([1,0] if mask_type==0 else [0,1],
                                 dtype=torch.float32)
        self.net = nn.Sequential(nn.Linear(d, h), nn.ReLU(),
                                 nn.Linear(h, h), nn.ReLU(),
                                 nn.Linear(h, 2*d))    # outputs s, t
    def forward(self, x):
        x_a = x * self.mask
        s, t = self.net(x_a).chunk(2, dim=-1)
        s = s * (1-self.mask)
        t = t * (1-self.mask)
        y = x_a + (1-self.mask) * (x*torch.exp(s) + t)
        log_det = s.sum(-1)
        return y, log_det

    def inverse(self, y):
        y_a = y * self.mask
        s, t = self.net(y_a).chunk(2, dim=-1)
        s = s * (1-self.mask); t = t * (1-self.mask)
        x = y_a + (1-self.mask) * ((y - t) * torch.exp(-s))
        return x, -s.sum(-1)

📝 自我檢測
