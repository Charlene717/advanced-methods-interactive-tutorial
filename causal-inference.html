單細胞因果推論

相關不等於因果。Pearl 的 do-calculus、DAG 學習、Perturb-seq、CausalCell——從觀察走向干預。

Correlation ≠ causation. Pearl's do-calculus, DAG learning, Perturb-seq, CausalCell — moving from observation to intervention.

動機

為什麼觀察性 GRN 推論不夠？

傳統 GRN 推論（Pearson、MI、Chatterjee）找的是「相關」——兩基因表現有關聯。但相關有三種來源：

  • 真因果：A → B
  • 反向因果：B → A
  • 共同原因（confounder）：C → A, C → B（A 與 B 共變但無直接調控）

純觀察資料無法區分這三種。我們需要 干預——主動敲除/活化某基因，觀察其他基因如何改變。這就是 Pearl 的 do(·) 符號表達的「干預機率」p(B | do(A))，與「條件機率」p(B | A) 不同。

Traditional GRN inference (Pearson, MI, Chatterjee) finds correlation. But correlation has three sources:

  • True cause: A → B
  • Reverse cause: B → A
  • Common cause (confounder): C → A, C → B (A, B covary but neither regulates the other)

Pure observational data can't distinguish them. We need intervention — actively perturb a gene and watch others change. Pearl's do(·) notation captures intervention probability p(B | do(A)), distinct from conditional probability p(B | A).

CausalCell (Wen 2023), CINEMA-OT, etc.: use limited interventions as anchors to direct observational data — more reliable than pure obs, cheaper than pure intervention.

深入解析

深入解析因果推論：理論基礎、演算法、單細胞特有問題

① Pearl 的 Structural Causal Model (SCM) 與 do-calculus 三條規則

Judea Pearl 的因果框架建立在 SCM 上：每個變數 Xi 由 structural equation Xi = fi(PAi, Ui) 決定，其中 PAi 是 Xi 的因父節點，Ui 是外生噪音。do(·) 算子定義「干預」：do(X = x) 等於把該 equation 改為 X = x（切斷 PAi → Xi 的所有箭頭）。

Pearl 證明了三條 do-calculus 規則，能把 p(Y | do(X), Z) 改寫為純觀察 p(Y | X, Z, ...) 的條件（若可識別）：

  • Rule 1（插入/刪除觀察）：在 graph G 中切斷 X 的入邊後，若 Y ⊥⊥ Z | X，則 p(Y | do(X), Z, W) = p(Y | do(X), W)。
  • Rule 2（觀察 ↔ 干預）：若在 graph G 中切斷 Z 的出邊後 Y ⊥⊥ Z | X，則 p(Y | do(X), do(Z), W) = p(Y | do(X), Z, W)。
  • Rule 3（插入/刪除干預）：若在 graph G 中切斷 X 與 Z 的入邊後 Y ⊥⊥ Z | X，則 p(Y | do(X), do(Z), W) = p(Y | do(X), W)。

這三條規則完備 (complete) — Shpitser & Pearl (2006) 證明任何可識別的 do-query 都能透過有限次套用這三條規則化簡。

② d-separation：DAG 上 conditional independence 的 graphical criterion

給定 DAG G，路徑 p 從 X 到 Y 被 Z 阻擋 (blocked) 若：(a) p 含 chain X → M → Y 或 fork X ← M → Y 且 M ∈ Z，或 (b) p 含 collider X → C ← Y 且 C 及其後代 ∉ Z。X ⊥⊥d Y | Z 表示「Z 阻擋所有 X-Y 路徑」。

關鍵定理：若 SCM 對應 DAG 為 G，則 X ⊥⊥d Y | Z 在 G 上 ⇒ X ⊥⊥ Y | Z 在所有 distribution 上。逆向不一定成立 (有 faithfulness assumption)。

實務上：collider 是因果推論最常見的陷阱。「在 collider 上條件」反而會引入虛假相關（Berkson's paradox），這在單細胞中對應「對 cell cycle 做 condition 反而引入新偽訊號」。

③ Backdoor criterion 與 frontdoor criterion

從觀察資料估計 do(·) 的兩個基本工具：

  • Backdoor criterion：若 Z 阻擋所有從 X 出發、最後指向 Y 的「後門路徑」（含 X 的入邊路徑），則 p(Y | do(X)) = Σz p(Y | X, Z=z) p(Z=z)。實務上 Z 即為「所有 confounder」的足夠集合。
  • Frontdoor criterion：當 confounder 無法觀察時，若存在 mediator M 滿足 (a) M 截斷 X 到 Y 的所有有向路徑、(b) X 到 M 沒有 backdoor、(c) Y 到 M 的所有 backdoor 都被 X 阻擋，則 p(Y | do(X)) = Σm p(M=m | X) Σx' p(Y | M=m, X=x') p(X=x')。

單細胞應用：基因 A → 蛋白 P → 基因 B 是典型 frontdoor 結構。即使有未觀察 confounder (如轉錄因子)，仍可透過 P 識別 A 對 B 的因果效應。

④ Instrumental Variable (IV) 方法：當 confounder 無法觀察

當所有可能 confounder 都無法觀察，backdoor 不可行。IV 是替代方案：找一個變數 Z 滿足 (a) Z 影響 X、(b) Z 只透過 X 影響 Y（exclusion restriction）、(c) Z 與 (Y 的) confounder 獨立。

因果效應估計：β = Cov(Z, Y) / Cov(Z, X)（two-stage least squares）。

基因組學的 IV：Mendelian randomization (MR)——以 SNP 作為 IV，因為 SNP 在 meiosis 中隨機分配（滿足 exclusion 與 independence，需檢驗 pleiotropy）。MR 在 large GWAS + scRNA-seq 整合中極受重視。

⑤ PC algorithm 細節

PC (Spirtes-Glymour-Scheines 1991) 是最經典的 constraint-based causal discovery：

  1. Skeleton phase：從完全圖出發，對每對 (X, Y) 找 separating set S ⊂ Adj(X) \ {Y}，使 X ⊥⊥ Y | S。若找到則刪邊。Conditional independence 用 Fisher's z-test 配 partial correlation 檢驗（連續資料）或 χ² test（離散）。
  2. Orientation phase：對每個 unshielded triple X – Y – Z (X 與 Z 不相鄰)，若 Y ∉ SXZ，則 orient 為 collider X → Y ← Z。其他邊用 Meek's rules (avoid new colliders, avoid cycles) 反覆 propagate。

輸出為 CPDAG (completed partially directed acyclic graph)——含未定向邊代表「等價類」，可能對應多個 DAG。

單細胞特有問題：細胞數雖大但「相依」（共享 batch、technical factor），會違反 PC 的 i.i.d. 假設，導致 false positives。CausalCell (Wen 2023) 用 resampling-based calibration 緩解。

⑥ NOTEARS (Zheng et al. 2018, NeurIPS)：把 DAG 約束變連續最佳化

傳統 DAG 學習是組合最佳化（NP-hard）。NOTEARS 的核心 trick：把「acyclicity」寫成連續可微的 trace constraint：

h(W) = tr(eW ⊙ W) − d = 0

其中 W 是 d×d 加權鄰接矩陣，⊙ 是 element-wise 平方，e· 是矩陣指數。h(W) = 0 當且僅當 W 對應 DAG（無環）。整體 loss：

minW ½ ||X − XW||² + λ ||W||1 s.t. h(W) = 0

用 augmented Lagrangian 解。優點：對中等規模 (d ≤ 100) graphs 比 PC 快且穩定。缺點：對 large d (基因數 > 1000) 仍 expensive；scNotears (2023) 提出 mini-batch 變體。

⑦ LiNGAM：利用非高斯性定向

Linear Non-Gaussian Acyclic Model (Shimizu 2006) 利用一個關鍵事實：若 X = AY + e，且 e 非高斯，則「方向」是可識別的（vs 高斯時方向 ambiguous）。

演算法：(1) 對資料做 ICA (Independent Component Analysis) 找出 mixing matrix；(2) permute matrix rows 使對角線元素非零；(3) 從 lower triangular 結構讀出 causal order。

scRNA-seq 中 gene expression 因 dropout、bursty transcription 多呈非高斯，是 LiNGAM 合適的應用場景。

⑧ Additive Noise Model (ANM) 與其他 SEM-based 方法

更廣的 SEM 模型：Xi = fi(PAi) + Ni，其中 Ni 獨立。Hoyer et al. (2009) 證明若 f 非線性，方向可識別：對「正確方向」做 regression 後 residual 與 cause 獨立；錯誤方向則不獨立。實作上用 HSIC test 檢驗 residual independence。

⑨ 反事實 (counterfactual)：Pearl's three-step procedure

反事實問「假如當初做了不同的選擇，會怎樣？」（給定觀察到的 evidence E，問 do(X = x') 的結果）。Pearl 的三步驟：

  1. Abduction：從觀察 E 更新 exogenous variable U 的後驗 p(U | E)。
  2. Action：在 SCM 中執行 do(X = x')——把 X 的 equation 換成 X = x'。
  3. Prediction：從更新後 model 計算 Y 的分布。

單細胞中，反事實 = 「這個被擾動的細胞如果沒被擾動會怎樣？」CINEMA-OT (Dong 2023) 用 optimal transport 對每個 perturbed cell 找對應的 counterfactual 控制細胞。

⑩ Causal discovery on time-series：Granger vs full causal

Granger causality：X Granger-causes Y 若 p(Yt | Xpast, Ypast) ≠ p(Yt | Ypast)。優點：簡單。缺點：(a) 只能處理 stationary time series，(b) 不能處理 unobserved confounder，(c) 不是真因果（Pearl 與 Granger 不等價）。

Full causal discovery on time-series：PCMCI (Runge 2019, Nat Comm) 把 PC 擴展到時序，能處理 lagged + contemporaneous 兩種因果關係。

單細胞中：pseudotime 不是真時間，直接套 Granger 有風險。RNA velocity 提供細胞個體的「短時間」變化，是更直接的因果訊號來源。

⑪ Mendelian randomization in single-cell QTL

單細胞 eQTL (e.g. OneK1K, sceQTL-Gen) 提供「SNP → cell-type-specific expression」的關聯。用 SNP 作為 IV，可估計：

  • 基因表現 → 細胞表型 (e.g. ageing, apoptosis) 的因果效應
  • SNP → 中介基因 → 疾病的 mediation analysis

挑戰：scRNA-seq 雜訊大、cell-type 內樣本數小，需要 large cohort (> 1000 個體)。

⑫ DR (doubly-robust) estimation

結合 propensity score model 與 outcome model：若兩者至少一個正確，DR estimator 仍 consistent。對於 Perturb-seq：

τ = E[(W − π(X))/(π(X)(1 − π(X))) · (Y − μ̂(X, W))] + μ̂(X, 1) − μ̂(X, 0)

其中 W 是擾動 indicator，π(X) 是 propensity，μ̂ 是 outcome model。DR-Learner (Kennedy 2020) 是進階版本。

⑬ Perturb-seq 中的模型：potential outcomes vs DAG

兩個並行的因果框架：

  • Potential outcomes (Rubin)：每個 cell 有兩種潛在 outcome Y(0)、Y(1)，因果效應 = Y(1) − Y(0)。但「同一細胞」只能觀察一種。Perturb-seq 把不同細胞分到不同擾動，估計 ATE = E[Y(1) − Y(0)]。
  • DAG (Pearl)：明確畫出 perturbation → gene → downstream gene 的 graph，估計 path-specific effects。

兩者數學上等價但思維方式不同。Perturb-seq 通常用 Rubin 框架做 ATE 估計，做 mediation/path analysis 時切到 Pearl 框架。

⑭ Direct effect vs total effect (path-specific)

對 A → M → B 的結構，A 對 B 有：

  • Total effect (TE)：所有路徑加總 = p(B | do(A=1)) − p(B | do(A=0))
  • Natural Direct Effect (NDE)：固定 M 在「沒擾動 A 時」的分布，看 A 對 B 的影響
  • Natural Indirect Effect (NIE)：固定 A=0 但讓 M 跟隨 A=1 時的分布，看 B 變化

VanderWeele (2015) 的 mediation analysis 提供嚴格的識別條件。單細胞中：TF → chromatin → gene 的 mediation 是典型 NDE/NIE 應用。

① Pearl's Structural Causal Model (SCM) and the three do-calculus rules

SCM: each variable Xi obeys a structural equation Xi = fi(PAi, Ui), where PAi are causal parents and Ui is exogenous noise. The do(·) operator defines intervention: do(X = x) replaces that equation with X = x (severing all incoming arrows to X).

Pearl's do-calculus consists of three rules that rewrite p(Y | do(X), Z) into purely observational p(Y | X, Z, ...) when identifiable:

  • Rule 1 (insert/delete observations): after removing incoming edges of X in G, if Y ⊥⊥ Z | X, then p(Y | do(X), Z, W) = p(Y | do(X), W).
  • Rule 2 (observation ↔ intervention): after removing outgoing edges of Z in G, if Y ⊥⊥ Z | X, then p(Y | do(X), do(Z), W) = p(Y | do(X), Z, W).
  • Rule 3 (insert/delete interventions): after removing incoming edges of X and Z, if Y ⊥⊥ Z | X, then p(Y | do(X), do(Z), W) = p(Y | do(X), W).

Shpitser & Pearl (2006) proved these three rules are complete — any identifiable do-query reduces via finite applications.

② d-separation: graphical criterion for conditional independence

In DAG G, a path p from X to Y is blocked by Z if (a) p contains chain X → M → Y or fork X ← M → Y with M ∈ Z, or (b) p contains a collider X → C ← Y and neither C nor its descendants are in Z. We write X ⊥⊥d Y | Z if Z blocks all X-Y paths.

Key theorem: X ⊥⊥d Y | Z in G ⇒ X ⊥⊥ Y | Z in all distributions consistent with G. The converse requires faithfulness.

Practical caveat: colliders are the most common pitfall. Conditioning on a collider creates spurious correlation (Berkson's paradox) — in single-cell, "conditioning on cell cycle" can fabricate fake signal.

③ Backdoor and frontdoor criteria

  • Backdoor: if Z blocks all "backdoor" paths (paths starting with an arrow into X) from X to Y, then p(Y | do(X)) = Σz p(Y | X, Z=z) p(Z=z). In practice Z is "all confounders."
  • Frontdoor: when confounders are unobserved, find mediator M such that (a) M intercepts all directed X→Y paths, (b) no backdoor from X to M, (c) all M-to-Y backdoors are blocked by X. Then p(Y | do(X)) = Σm p(M=m | X) Σx' p(Y | M=m, X=x') p(X=x').

Single-cell example: gene A → protein P → gene B is a frontdoor. Even with unobserved confounders (e.g. TFs), the causal effect A → B is identifiable through P.

④ Instrumental Variable (IV): when confounders are unobserved

Find Z satisfying (a) Z affects X, (b) Z affects Y only through X (exclusion restriction), (c) Z independent of Y's confounders. Estimate β = Cov(Z, Y) / Cov(Z, X) via 2SLS.

Genomic IV: Mendelian randomization (MR) — SNPs as instruments (random at meiosis). Pleiotropy must be tested. Increasingly used to integrate GWAS + scRNA-seq.

⑤ PC algorithm in detail

PC (Spirtes-Glymour-Scheines 1991): the classic constraint-based discovery algorithm.

  1. Skeleton: start from complete graph; for each pair (X, Y), find separating set S ⊂ Adj(X) \ {Y} with X ⊥⊥ Y | S. Test via Fisher's z on partial correlation (continuous) or χ² (discrete).
  2. Orientation: for unshielded triple X – Y – Z, if Y ∉ SXZ, orient as collider X → Y ← Z. Propagate via Meek's rules (avoid new colliders, no cycles).

Output is a CPDAG (completed partially directed acyclic graph) — undirected edges represent equivalence classes.

Single-cell pitfall: cells are not i.i.d. (shared batch, tech factors), violating PC's assumption → false positives. CausalCell (Wen 2023) uses resampling-based calibration.

⑥ NOTEARS (Zheng et al. 2018, NeurIPS): DAG constraint as continuous optimization

DAG learning is NP-hard combinatorial. NOTEARS' core trick: express acyclicity as a smooth trace constraint:

h(W) = tr(eW ⊙ W) − d = 0

Combined loss: minW ½ ||X − XW||² + λ ||W||1 s.t. h(W) = 0, solved via augmented Lagrangian. Faster and more stable than PC for moderate d ≤ 100; scNotears (2023) extends with mini-batches for genes > 1000.

⑦ LiNGAM: orientability via non-Gaussianity

Linear Non-Gaussian Acyclic Model (Shimizu 2006) exploits: if X = AY + e and e is non-Gaussian, direction is identifiable (vs ambiguous in Gaussian). Algorithm: (1) ICA finds mixing matrix; (2) permute rows so diagonal is nonzero; (3) read causal order from the lower triangular pattern.

scRNA-seq expression is non-Gaussian (dropouts, bursts) — good for LiNGAM.

⑧ Additive Noise Model (ANM) and other SEM methods

Broader SEM: Xi = fi(PAi) + Ni. Hoyer et al. (2009) show: if f is non-linear, direction is identifiable — regressing in the "correct" direction yields residuals independent of cause; wrong direction does not. Test via HSIC.

⑨ Counterfactuals: Pearl's three-step procedure

Counterfactuals ask "what if a different choice had been made?" (compute outcome of do(X = x') given observed evidence E):

  1. Abduction: update exogenous variable posterior p(U | E).
  2. Action: apply do(X = x') in the SCM.
  3. Prediction: compute Y from the modified model.

Single-cell counterfactual: "what would this perturbed cell express if NOT perturbed?" CINEMA-OT (Dong 2023) uses optimal transport to match each perturbed cell to a counterfactual control.

⑩ Time-series causal discovery: Granger vs full causal

Granger: X Granger-causes Y if p(Yt | Xpast, Ypast) ≠ p(Yt | Ypast). Simple but (a) stationarity required, (b) can't handle unobserved confounders, (c) not Pearl-equivalent.

Full: PCMCI (Runge 2019, Nat Comm) extends PC to time-series, handling both lagged and contemporaneous causality.

Single-cell: pseudotime isn't real time — naive Granger is risky. RNA velocity offers per-cell "short-time" change, a more direct causal signal.

⑪ Mendelian randomization in single-cell QTL

Single-cell eQTL (OneK1K, sceQTL-Gen) provides SNP → cell-type-specific expression associations. Using SNPs as IVs:

  • Estimate gene → phenotype (aging, apoptosis) causal effects.
  • Mediation: SNP → mediator gene → disease.

Challenge: noisy scRNA-seq, small per-cell-type sample size — needs large cohorts (> 1000 donors).

⑫ Doubly-robust (DR) estimation

Combine propensity-score and outcome models; if at least one is correct, DR is consistent. For Perturb-seq:

τ = E[(W − π(X)) / (π(X)(1 − π(X))) · (Y − μ̂(X, W))] + μ̂(X, 1) − μ̂(X, 0)

W is perturbation indicator, π is propensity, μ̂ is outcome model. DR-Learner (Kennedy 2020) is the advanced variant.

⑬ Perturb-seq frameworks: potential outcomes vs DAG

  • Potential outcomes (Rubin): each cell has Y(0) and Y(1); causal effect = Y(1) − Y(0). One cell observes only one. Perturb-seq assigns cells to perturbations → estimate ATE.
  • DAG (Pearl): draw perturbation → gene → downstream graph; estimate path-specific effects.

Mathematically equivalent. Perturb-seq typically uses Rubin for ATE; switches to Pearl for mediation/path analysis.

⑭ Direct effect vs total effect (path-specific)

For A → M → B:

  • Total effect (TE): p(B | do(A=1)) − p(B | do(A=0)) — summed across all paths.
  • Natural Direct Effect (NDE): fix M at its A=0 distribution; measure A's effect on B.
  • Natural Indirect Effect (NIE): hold A=0 but let M follow A=1 distribution.

VanderWeele (2015) gives identification conditions. Single-cell: TF → chromatin → gene mediation is a canonical NDE/NIE application.

詳細已知應用

因果推論在單細胞與多體學的具體案例

🧬 案例 1：Dixit et al. (2016, Cell) — Perturb-seq 開創

把 CRISPR sgRNA 條碼整合進 scRNA-seq droplet——每個細胞被指定一個 sgRNA 知識，再讀全 transcriptome。第一個論文證明可用 ~200,000 細胞同時擾動 24 個轉錄因子，得到 cell-type-specific 反應圖譜。意義：把「scRNA-seq + CRISPR screen」變成標準工具，因果推論在單細胞的可行性被建立。

🧬 案例 2：Adamson et al. (2016, Cell) — UPR perturbation

用 Perturb-seq 解析 unfolded protein response (UPR) 的因果結構，擾動 ATF6、IRE1、PERK 等 master regulator。發現 UPR 三條 arm 比過去認知更獨立——這個 finding 推翻了「三 arm 高度交織」的舊模型。

🧬 案例 3：Replogle et al. (2022, Cell) — genome-wide Perturb-seq

2.5M 細胞、9867 個 essential genes 的 CRISPRi 干預——目前最大規模的單細胞干預資料。關鍵 contribution：(a) 證明 essentiality 的 transcriptional readout 可揭示「為何 essential」（哪些下游 pathway 被破壞），(b) 建立 day-1 reference for in-silico perturbation 模型的訓練/評估。

🧬 案例 4：GEARS (Roohani et al. 2023, Nature Biotechnology)

用 GNN 預測未測過的 perturbation 效應——把基因表示為 GNN node、訓練時學「擾動 X 後 transcriptome 的變化方向」，inference 對新 X 預測。在 Norman 2019 dual-perturbation 上 r = 0.55。關鍵 insight：用 perturbation-perturbation 相似度而非 cell-cell 相似度作為 GNN edge，效果更好。

🧬 案例 5：CINEMA-OT (Dong et al. 2023, Nature Methods)

用 optimal transport (OT) 對 perturbed vs control cells 做配對，估計每個 perturbed cell 的「反事實 expression」（如果這個細胞沒被擾動會怎樣）。創新：(a) 用 entropy-regularized OT 處理 scale 問題、(b) 配對後計算 individual treatment effect (ITE) 而非僅 ATE。對藥物治療異質性研究有重大用途。

🧬 案例 6：CausalCell (Wen et al. 2023, NAR)

整合觀察 + 干預資料學 causal DAG：(a) 觀察資料用 modified PC algorithm，(b) 干預資料用 do-calculus 校準方向。在 mouse embryonic stem cell 資料上找回已知 OCT4 → NANOG → SOX2 環，並預測新 edges 經 Perturb-seq 驗證。

🧬 案例 7：Mixscape (Papalexi et al. 2021, Nature Methods)

Perturb-seq 一大問題：CRISPR 效率不完美，有些細胞「逃脫」（gRNA 存在但基因未被有效敲除）。Mixscape 用 perturbation signature 將 cells 分為「真擾動」與「逃脫」，前者用於 downstream causal analysis，後者作為內部 control。影響：Perturb-seq pipeline 標準化。

🧬 案例 8：scGEN (Lotfollahi et al. 2019, Nature Methods)

用 VAE 學「擾動方向 vector」——在 latent space 把 control cell 沿這個 vector 移動就能模擬擾動效應。雖然不是嚴格因果，但提供「跨 cell type 的擾動 transfer」框架。後續發展為 scGen 系列。

🧬 案例 9：Fröhlich et al. (2018, NAR) — Boolean causal models

把 GRN 建為 Boolean network (gene = ON/OFF)，用 perturb data 推估每個基因的 logical function (AND/OR/NOT)。對 small networks (10–30 基因) 可窮舉搜索，是經典的 mechanistic causal inference。

🧬 案例 10：Mendelian randomization in single-cell eQTL

OneK1K (Yazar 2022, Science) 在 982 individuals × scRNA-seq 上做 sceQTL mapping，識別 cell-type-specific eQTL。後續多個論文用這些 SNP 作 IV 估計「gene → trait」因果效應，例如 IL6R 表現對 COVID-19 嚴重度的因果影響。

🧬 案例 11：Lopez et al. (2022) — causal scRNA-seq testing

正式化「scRNA-seq 上做 hypothesis test 因果效應」的統計框架，提出 conditional randomization test (CRT) 結合 Mixscape，得到 valid p-values，控制 FDR。

🧬 案例 12：DECIPHER (2024)

偵測 Perturb-seq 中「functional」擾動效應 (區分真實 biological effect vs 細胞 stress response)。用 contrastive learning 學「擾動相關」與「擾動無關」的 latent space，再用後者作為 nuisance variable 控制。

🧬 Case 1: Dixit et al. (2016, Cell) — Perturb-seq inaugural paper

Integrated CRISPR sgRNA barcodes into scRNA-seq droplets — each cell tagged with a known sgRNA, then full transcriptome read. First paper to perturb 24 TFs simultaneously in ~200,000 cells, generating cell-type-specific response maps. Significance: made "scRNA-seq + CRISPR screen" a standard tool, established feasibility of single-cell causal inference.

🧬 Case 2: Adamson et al. (2016, Cell) — UPR perturbation

Perturb-seq dissection of the unfolded protein response — perturbing ATF6, IRE1, PERK master regulators. Showed UPR's three arms are more independent than previously thought, overturning the old "highly intertwined" model.

🧬 Case 3: Replogle et al. (2022, Cell) — genome-wide Perturb-seq

2.5M cells, 9867 essential genes, CRISPRi — largest single-cell intervention map to date. Key contributions: (a) transcriptional readout of essentiality reveals "why" (which downstream pathways disrupt), (b) day-1 reference for in-silico perturbation model training/eval.

🧬 Case 4: GEARS (Roohani et al. 2023, Nature Biotechnology)

GNN predicts unseen perturbation effects — genes as GNN nodes; learns direction of transcriptome change post-perturbation. On Norman 2019 dual perturbations, r = 0.55. Key insight: using perturbation-perturbation similarity (not cell-cell) as GNN edges works better.

🧬 Case 5: CINEMA-OT (Dong et al. 2023, Nature Methods)

Optimal transport between perturbed and control cells → counterfactual expression per cell ("what would this cell express if not perturbed?"). Innovation: (a) entropy-regularized OT for scale, (b) individual treatment effects (ITE) beyond ATE. Crucial for treatment-heterogeneity studies.

🧬 Case 6: CausalCell (Wen et al. 2023, NAR)

Joint observational + interventional causal DAG learning: (a) modified PC on observation, (b) do-calculus orientation from interventions. On mouse ESC data, recovers OCT4 → NANOG → SOX2 cycle; predicts new edges validated by Perturb-seq.

🧬 Case 7: Mixscape (Papalexi et al. 2021, Nature Methods)

A core Perturb-seq problem: CRISPR is imperfect; some cells "escape" (sgRNA present but gene not effectively knocked down). Mixscape separates true perturbed vs escaped via perturbation signatures; former for downstream causal analysis, latter as internal control. Impact: standardized Perturb-seq pipeline.

🧬 Case 8: scGEN (Lotfollahi et al. 2019, Nature Methods)

VAE learns a "perturbation direction" vector — moving control cells along it in latent space simulates perturbation effect. Not strictly causal, but enables cross-cell-type perturbation transfer. Spawned the scGen family.

🧬 Case 9: Fröhlich et al. (2018, NAR) — Boolean causal models

GRN as a Boolean network (gene = ON/OFF); infer per-gene logical function (AND/OR/NOT) from perturb data. Exhaustive search feasible for small networks (10–30 genes) — classical mechanistic causal inference.

🧬 Case 10: Mendelian randomization with single-cell eQTL

OneK1K (Yazar 2022, Science): 982 individuals × scRNA-seq → cell-type-specific eQTLs. Follow-ups use these SNPs as IVs to estimate gene → trait causal effects, e.g. IL6R expression's effect on COVID-19 severity.

🧬 Case 11: Lopez et al. (2022) — causal scRNA-seq testing

Formalized "hypothesis testing of causal effects on scRNA-seq" — conditional randomization test (CRT) combined with Mixscape yields valid p-values with FDR control.

🧬 Case 12: DECIPHER (2024)

Detects "functional" Perturb-seq effects vs cell-stress responses. Contrastive learning separates "perturbation-related" from "perturbation-agnostic" latent spaces; latter used as nuisance covariate.

潛在改良應用

因果推論還能改良/新解決哪些任務？

🎯 1. 多步因果發現：藥物 → 基因 → 蛋白 → 表型

現有限制：多數因果分析只看「擾動 → 直接 mRNA 改變」，無法解釋藥物效應的完整 mechanism。

新提案：整合 Perturb-seq + CITE-seq (protein) + 表型 (例如 high-content imaging) 在同一 cell，建立多層 SCM。用 path-specific effect (NDE/NIE) 分解每一步貢獻。

改良：對藥物 mode of action 提供 mechanism-level 解釋，加速 lead optimization。

🎯 2. 因果整合多體學

現有限制：多體學整合 (scGLUE、Seurat WNN) 求 cross-modal alignment，但未表達「ATAC peak → RNA → protein」的因果方向。

新提案：把多體學整合改寫為 layered SCM：ATAC accessibility 為上游、RNA 為中游、protein 為下游。用 Perturb-ATAC、Perturb-seq 等干預資料定向，並用 Mendelian randomization 補充未觀察 confounder。

改良：能回答「染色質開放是因為 TF binding 或是 TF 結果」這類因果問題。

🎯 3. Causal Perturb-spatial：擾動空間影響

現有限制：Perturb-seq 用 droplet 分離 cell，丟失空間 context；空間轉錄體 (Visium、MERFISH) 又無 perturbation 標記。

新提案：結合 in-situ perturbation (e.g. CRISPR-display) + 空間轉錄體，分析擾動細胞對「鄰近細胞」的 paracrine 因果效應。需要新的 spatial SCM 表示形式（細胞為 node、空間鄰接為 edge）。

改良：對 cell-cell communication 與 niche signaling 提供首次 do(·) 級的證據。

🎯 4. 反事實治療選擇（precision medicine）

現有限制：當前精準醫療多靠 biomarker stratification，未直接估計「這個病人用 A vs B 藥的反事實 outcome」。

新提案：用 patient-level scRNA-seq + clinical outcome 訓練 causal forest (Wager-Athey) 或 BART (Bayesian Additive Regression Trees)，估計個別治療效應 (ITE)。對腫瘤異質性特別有用。

改良：從「平均最佳」轉為「個人最佳」治療決策。

🎯 5. 預測抗藥性演化的因果模型

現有限制：癌症抗藥性研究多用基因組學 (mutation profiling)，無法解釋「為何特定 escape pathway 出現」。

新提案：結合 longitudinal scRNA-seq + Perturb-seq，建立「藥物壓力 → 細胞狀態轉換 → 抗藥性」的時序因果模型。用 PCMCI on time-series 找 lagged causality。

改良：預測哪些 escape pathway 會出現，預先設計組合療法。

🎯 6. 用 IV 方法整合 GWAS + scRNA-seq

現有限制：GWAS 給出 SNP-trait 關聯但未解釋 mechanism；scRNA-seq eQTL 給出 SNP-gene 但難連到 disease。

新提案：大規模 Mendelian randomization——對每個 (SNP, cell-type, gene, disease) 四元組做 MR 分析，建立「SNP → cell-type-specific expression → disease」的因果鏈。需要 1000+ individual cohort 與 standardized cell-type 對應。

改良：從 GWAS hits 直接連到 cell-type-specific 機制，加速 drug target 發掘。

🎯 7. Causal scLLM：給模型加因果結構先驗

現有限制：當前 scLLM (scGPT、Geneformer) 學「相關性」而非「因果」。其 attention 在 perturbation prediction benchmark 上未勝過 linear baseline。

新提案：把 known causal edges (來自 KEGG、Reactome、Perturb-seq) 編入 scLLM 的 attention prior——對已知 causal pair 給高 attention weight 初始化，並加入「causal consistency」loss（擾動 A 後 B 的預測變化應符合已知方向）。

改良：scLLM 從「pattern learner」升級為「causal learner」，在 perturbation 任務勝過 baseline。

🎯 8. 偵測「Simpson's paradox」型細胞型別誤導

現有限制：整體 cell population 上的 gene-gene relationship 可能在每個 sub-population 內部反向（Simpson 悖論）。傳統 DE/GRN 推論常忽略這個。

新提案：系統性掃描每對 gene，在 cluster 內 vs 全部 sample 上計算相關，flag「方向反轉」的 pair。這些 pair 提示存在 confounding cell type effect。

改良：避免 spurious gene module；提示需要進一步 sub-cluster 與 stratified analysis。

🎯 9. 病人特異性因果發現

現有限制：因果發現通常從 population 推估 average DAG，無法捕捉病人個別結構差異。

新提案：對單一病人的 longitudinal scRNA-seq + treatment response 做 personalized causal discovery，用 Bayesian causal forest 或 patient-specific GP-based SCM。

改良：對罕見疾病、N-of-1 試驗提供新工具。

🎯 10. 從 single-cell 資料建構 mechanistic 動力學模型

現有限制：當前 RNA velocity (Bergen 2020) 假設 splicing-degradation simple kinetics，無法捕捉 TF binding 與多階段 regulation。

新提案：用 Perturb-seq + causal discovery 重建 mechanistic ODE 系統 (dGene/dt = f(TF, miRNA, ...))，再用 sparse identification of nonlinear dynamics (SINDy) 估計係數。

改良：可預測未測 perturbation 的時序軌跡，是真正的「mechanistic prediction」。

🎯 11. Causal inference 用於 senescence trajectories

現有限制：細胞衰老 (senescence) 是 ageing 與癌症的核心，但「哪個 cell state 決定 fate (senescence vs apoptosis)」未解。

新提案：longitudinal scRNA-seq + Perturb-seq on senescence-related genes，用 counterfactual analysis 找出「fate-deciding moment」。

改良：對 senolytic drug development 提供因果靶標。

🎯 12. 預測 combination perturbation 效應

現有限制：GEARS 等預測 dual perturbation 效應 r ≈ 0.55，對 3+ gene 組合幾乎完全無法預測。

新提案：用 path-specific causal effects 分解 combination effect (additive + synergistic + antagonistic)，每個 component 用不同模型估計，再加總。需要 large multi-gene Perturb-seq 資料 (e.g. Norman + Replogle combined)。

改良：為 combination therapy 設計提供更精準預測。

🎯 1. Multi-step causal discovery: drug → gene → protein → phenotype

Current limit: most causal analyses cover only "perturbation → direct mRNA change," missing full drug mechanisms.

Proposal: integrate Perturb-seq + CITE-seq + phenotype (e.g. high-content imaging) in one cell; build multi-layer SCM. Decompose contributions via path-specific effects (NDE/NIE).

Improvement: mechanism-level explanation of drug mode-of-action; accelerates lead optimization.

🎯 2. Causal multi-omics integration

Current limit: scGLUE, Seurat WNN find cross-modal alignment but ignore "ATAC → RNA → protein" causal direction.

Proposal: rewrite as layered SCM — ATAC upstream, RNA middle, protein downstream. Use Perturb-ATAC, Perturb-seq for orientation; Mendelian randomization for unobserved confounders.

Improvement: answers "is chromatin opening cause or consequence of TF binding?"

🎯 3. Causal Perturb-spatial: perturbation effects in space

Current limit: Perturb-seq loses spatial context; spatial transcriptomics lacks perturbation labels.

Proposal: in-situ perturbation (CRISPR-display) + spatial transcriptomics — measure paracrine causal effects on neighboring cells. Requires a new spatial SCM (cells as nodes, spatial adjacency as edges).

Improvement: first do(·)-level evidence for cell-cell communication and niche signaling.

🎯 4. Counterfactual treatment selection (precision medicine)

Current limit: precision medicine relies on biomarker stratification, not counterfactual "drug A vs B for this patient" estimation.

Proposal: train causal forests (Wager-Athey) or BART on patient-level scRNA-seq + clinical outcomes to estimate individual treatment effects (ITE).

Improvement: shifts from "best-on-average" to "best-for-this-patient" decisions.

🎯 5. Causal models for drug-resistance evolution

Current limit: cancer resistance mostly studied via genomics (mutations), can't explain "why this escape pathway."

Proposal: longitudinal scRNA-seq + Perturb-seq → time-series causal model "drug pressure → cell-state transition → resistance." Use PCMCI for lagged causality.

Improvement: predict which escape pathways emerge; pre-design combination therapies.

🎯 6. IV methods integrating GWAS + scRNA-seq

Current limit: GWAS gives SNP-trait but no mechanism; sc-eQTL gives SNP-gene but rarely links to disease.

Proposal: large-scale Mendelian randomization on (SNP, cell-type, gene, disease) — build SNP → cell-type-specific expression → disease causal chains. Requires 1000+ donor cohorts and standardized cell-type vocab.

Improvement: directly link GWAS hits to cell-type-specific mechanisms; accelerate target discovery.

🎯 7. Causal scLLM: causal priors in the model

Current limit: current scLLMs learn correlation, not causation; attention does not beat linear baselines on perturbation benchmarks.

Proposal: embed known causal edges (KEGG, Reactome, Perturb-seq) as attention priors; add "causal consistency" loss — direction of B's predicted change after perturbing A must match known direction.

Improvement: upgrade scLLM from pattern learner to causal learner; surpass baselines on perturbation tasks.

🎯 8. Detect Simpson's-paradox-type cell-type confounding

Current limit: gene-gene relationships in full population may reverse within sub-populations (Simpson). Traditional DE/GRN ignores this.

Proposal: systematically scan gene pairs; compute correlation per cluster vs full sample; flag direction reversals. These pairs hint at confounding cell-type effects.

Improvement: avoid spurious gene modules; flag pairs needing stratified analysis.

🎯 9. Patient-specific causal discovery

Current limit: causal discovery typically infers a population-average DAG, missing patient-level variability.

Proposal: personalized causal discovery on longitudinal per-patient scRNA-seq + treatment response — Bayesian causal forests, patient-specific GP-based SCM.

Improvement: tool for rare diseases and N-of-1 trials.

🎯 10. Build mechanistic dynamics from single-cell data

Current limit: RNA velocity (Bergen 2020) assumes simple splicing-degradation kinetics, missing TF binding and multi-stage regulation.

Proposal: Perturb-seq + causal discovery → mechanistic ODE (dGene/dt = f(TF, miRNA, ...)) with SINDy estimating coefficients.

Improvement: predict trajectories under unseen perturbations — true mechanistic prediction.

🎯 11. Causal inference for senescence trajectories

Current limit: senescence is central to ageing and cancer, but "which cell state decides fate (senescence vs apoptosis)" is unresolved.

Proposal: longitudinal scRNA-seq + Perturb-seq on senescence genes; counterfactual analysis to find "fate-deciding moments."

Improvement: causal targets for senolytic drug development.

🎯 12. Predict combination perturbation effects

Current limit: GEARS predicts dual perturbations at r ≈ 0.55; 3+ gene combos are nearly unpredictable.

Proposal: decompose combination effects into additive + synergistic + antagonistic via path-specific causal effects, estimate each separately and sum. Needs large multi-gene Perturb-seq (Norman + Replogle merged).

Improvement: more accurate predictions for combination therapy design.
