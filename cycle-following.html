循環追蹤 (Cycle-following)

不是所有軌跡都有起點與終點——細胞週期、晝夜節律、振盪基因網路都是「圓環」。如何在 360° 上定位細胞？

Not all trajectories have a start and an end — cell cycle, circadian rhythm, oscillating gene networks are loops. How to localize cells on a 360° circle?

動機

線性 pseudotime 在循環過程中崩潰

標準軌跡推論方法（Monocle、Slingshot、PAGA）預設「樹狀」或「線性」結構——指定一個起點，計算到每個細胞的距離當 pseudotime。但細胞週期沒有起點：G1 → S → G2 → M → G1 是個圓。如果硬塞進線性 pseudotime，G1 起點與 G1 終點會被當成不同狀態。

循環追蹤方法把細胞的「相位」(phase) 定義在 [0, 2π) 圓上，明確處理週期性。對細胞週期、晝夜節律、神經元振盪、發炎基因週期都至關重要。

Standard trajectory tools (Monocle, Slingshot, PAGA) assume tree or linear structure — pick a root and compute distances. But the cell cycle has no root: G1 → S → G2 → M → G1 is a loop. Forcing it into linear pseudotime makes start-G1 and end-G1 look like different states.

Cycle-following methods define cell phase on the circle [0, 2π), explicitly handling periodicity — essential for cell cycle, circadian rhythm, neuronal oscillation, and inflammatory cycles.

🔁

不需起點

圓上沒有「第一個」細胞——只有相對相位。

No "first" cell on a circle — only relative phase.

📐

連續值

相位是 [0, 2π) 連續值，比 G1/S/G2/M 離散分類更精細。

Phase is continuous on [0, 2π) — finer than G1/S/G2/M discrete classes.

🧪

效應控制

下游分析時把細胞週期相位當共變量回歸掉，可看純粹的生物變異。

Regress out phase in downstream analysis to reveal pure biological variation.

主要方法

五種代表性方法

Tricycle (Zheng 2022, Genome Biol)

用「Go transfer learning」概念——預訓練一個「circular embedding」於 human/mouse 標準資料，新樣本投影上去即得相位。最簡單、最穩健。

Uses transfer learning — a circular embedding pretrained on human/mouse reference; new samples project onto it. Simplest and most robust.

Cyclum (Liang 2020)

非線性自編碼器把資料投影到「螺旋線」上，分離「圓周成分」與「線性成分」。適合分化過程中疊加細胞週期。

Non-linear autoencoder projects data onto a helix, separating circular and linear components. Good when cell cycle overlays differentiation.

reCAT (Liu 2017)

先 PCA 取 marker 基因，再用 TSP（旅行推銷員問題）找最佳「閉合路徑」——把細胞排成一個圓。

PCA on marker genes, then TSP (traveling salesman) to find the optimal closed path — orders cells into a loop.

CYCLOPS (Anafi 2017)

最早的圓嵌入方法之一，autoencoder 強制 bottleneck 為 2D 單位圓——專為晝夜節律設計。

One of the earliest — autoencoder forces a 2D unit circle bottleneck. Designed for circadian rhythm.

CCPE (2022)

用 discriminative helix 同時建模圓周與時間軸，提供 pseudotime 與離散 phase 雙重輸出。

Discriminative helix models both circle and time axis; outputs both continuous pseudotime and discrete phase.

基於拓撲的方法

直接用 persistent H₁ 偵測迴圈，計算 「circular coordinates」（Perea & Harer 2015）— 完全不依賴 marker 基因。

Detect loops via persistent H₁ and compute circular coordinates (Perea & Harer 2015) — no marker genes required.

互動模擬

用兩個振盪基因定位細胞相位

細胞週期通常用兩組基因量化：S-phase markers 與 G2/M markers。它們的相對水準在圓周上「轉」一圈，arctan2(G2M, S) 即可給出相位。調整噪音觀察恢復品質。

Cell cycle is often quantified by S-phase and G2/M marker scores. Their relative levels trace a circle; arctan2(G2M, S) gives the phase. Adjust noise to see recovery.

生物應用

實務情境

細胞週期效應控制

在 DE 分析或 clustering 前，用 tricycle 相位 ψ 當共變量：lm(expr ~ condition + sin(ψ) + cos(ψ))。比 Seurat::CellCycleScoring 的離散分類更精細。

Before DE or clustering, use tricycle phase ψ as covariate: lm(expr ~ condition + sin(ψ) + cos(ψ)). Finer than Seurat's discrete CellCycleScoring.

晝夜節律

CYCLOPS 用在無時間標記的樣本（如人類大腦死後組織），仍能還原 24 小時節律的相位。

CYCLOPS recovers 24-hour phases even in untimed samples (e.g. post-mortem human brain).

振盪基因偵測

給定圓周相位，做 cosinor 回歸 expr = M + A·cos(ψ − φ)，A 大且顯著 = 振盪基因。MetaCycle、RAIN 等工具是經典做法。

With phase, cosinor regression expr = M + A·cos(ψ − φ) identifies oscillators by significant amplitude A. MetaCycle, RAIN are classic tools.

癌細胞異常週期

許多腫瘤的細胞週期相位分布偏離正常組織（特定相位停滯）——可作為惡性程度指標。

Tumor cell phase distributions deviate from normal (stalled at specific phases) — a potential malignancy marker.

深入解析

深入解析 Cycle-following：拓撲、座標、振盪估計

① 為何「圓」是合適的拓撲？從 H₁ 說起

細胞週期是封閉迴路 G1 → S → G2 → M → G1，這種「回到起點」的軌跡無法用線性 pseudotime 表示——任何單調 t ∈ [0, 1] 都會在 G1 處出現「跳躍」。拓撲學告訴我們：軌跡是迴圈時，其同調群 H₀ = ℤ 與 H₁ = ℤ（一個非平凡 1-cycle）。當 persistent homology 偵測到 H₁ ≠ 0，正確的座標就應該是圓上的點 ψ ∈ S¹ = [0, 2π)，而非線段。這是「圓座標」的拓撲學基礎，由 Perea & Harer (2015, Found Comput Math) 提出。

② 從 H₁ 到 circular coordinates（TDA-based 方法）

Perea–Harer 的 SCOOP (Sliding-Window persistence + Cohomology Optimization for Periodic data) 流程：

  • (1) 計算 persistent cohomology：找出 H¹ 的 representative cocycle α（即偵測哪個 1-cycle 主導）。
  • (2) Lift 到 ℝ-valued cocycle：把模 p 表示的 cocycle 提升到實值。
  • (3) 解 harmonic representative：最小化 ‖dθ − α‖² 求 circular function θ : data → S¹。
  • θ 即為每個資料點的「圓相位」，是 topologically-justified 的座標。

這個方法的優點是不需要預設「circular gene set」——拓撲直接告訴你資料有無圓結構。缺點是計算量大 (n > 5000 慢)，且需要密集軌跡。

③ Tricycle 演算法（Zheng et al. 2022, Nat Commun）

Tricycle 用更實用的「reference-based projection」：

  • (1) Reference embedding：用 mouse neural progenitor 已知 cycle phase 資料，對 GO cell-cycle 基因（500 個）做 PCA，前 2 個 PC 自然形成圓。
  • (2) 投影新樣本：把新資料的 cycle gene expression 用同一旋轉矩陣投到 reference PC1–PC2 平面。
  • (3) 取 angular coordinate：ψᵢ = atan2(PC2ᵢ, PC1ᵢ) ∈ [−π, π]，即為「tricycle position」。

優點：(i) 連續，比 discrete G1/S/G2M 細緻；(ii) 跨物種轉移（reference 是 mouse，可用於 human）；(iii) 對 amplitude 不敏感（投影後標準化）。已成 Bioconductor 標準工具。

④ Discrete vs continuous resolution

不同方法的「解析度」差異實際後果：

  • Seurat::CellCycleScoring：把細胞分成 G1 / S / G2M 三類，使用 Tirosh et al. (2016) 的兩組 marker score。對「DE 控制」夠用，但無法分析 S 中期 vs S 晚期。
  • Schwabe et al. (2020) stages：5 stage 細分（G1, G1/S, S, G2, G2/M），中度解析。
  • Tricycle continuous ψ：連續角度——可做「ψ 對 expression」的 cosinor regression，找出在特定 narrow window 振盪的基因。從 Schwabe stages 到 tricycle 的轉換經 Zheng 2022 表 S3 提供查表。

⑤ 處理多週期疊加（cycle + circadian + ultradian）

細胞同時受多週期影響：~24 hr 細胞週期、~24 hr circadian、~12 hr ultradian、~90 min ATP 振盪。要分離需要：

  • (1) Tricycle ψ_cc 校正細胞週期後，殘差仍含其他週期；
  • (2) 對殘差做 second-pass circular coordinate（用 circadian gene set 如 BMAL1, PER1, CRY1, NR1D1 重做 PCA）；
  • (3) 多週期 cosinor model：expr = M + A₁·cos(ψ_cc − φ₁) + A₂·cos(ψ_cir − φ₂) + ε。R 套件 circacompare、discoRhythm 可做這類分析。

⑥ Cosinor regression 與 harmonic regression 的數學

對連續相位 ψ，振盪基因模型：expr(ψ) = M + A·cos(ψ − φ) = M + β₁·cos(ψ) + β₂·sin(ψ)。線性 fit 後：

  • Amplitude A = √(β₁² + β₂²)
  • Phase φ = atan2(β₂, β₁)
  • F-test on (β₁, β₂) 同為 0 → 「無振盪」H₀。

Harmonic regression 把 1st、2nd 諧波同列：expr = M + Σₖ Aₖ·cos(kψ − φₖ)，可偵測「雙峰」週期（如 M 與 S 都有 peak 的基因）。

⑦ 與 deep learning 結合：CycleAVE、scCycle、Cyclum 學 latent circular embedding

  • Cyclum (Liang et al. 2020, Nat Commun)：用 autoencoder + sinusoidal bottleneck（latent 為 sin(ψ), cos(ψ)）強迫嵌入是圓。比 PCA-based 對非線性 cycle gene 更穩。
  • CCPE (Liu et al. 2022, NAR)：helix 嵌入——允許「螺旋」進展，捕捉週期 + 漸進變化（如 senescence 的累積）。
  • CycleAVE：variational autoencoder + von Mises prior on latent ψ，提供 phase 的 posterior uncertainty。

⑧ 振盪基因 amplitude estimation（sparse 觀察）

單細胞資料有 dropout、count noise，sparse 觀察下估 A 困難。經典工具：

  • JTK_CYCLE (Hughes 2010)：nonparametric rank-based test。
  • ARSER (Yang & Su 2010)：autoregressive spectral residual。
  • RAIN (Thaben & Westermark 2014)：支援不對稱波形。
  • MetaCycle (Wu 2016)：整合上述三者投票，FDR 控制較嚴。

適用於 bulk 多時點，scRNA-seq 需先 phase-sort 細胞（tricycle 給 ψ），再「pseudo-bulk」每 phase bin 求 mean expression，再丟給 MetaCycle。

⑨ Cell cycle vs cell state 解耦

很多 DE 與 trajectory 分析受 cell cycle 干擾——同 cell type 不同 phase 細胞被誤分群。標準做法：

  • Seurat：RegressOut "S.Score" + "G2M.Score"（離散）。
  • tricycle 改良：RegressOut sin(ψ) + cos(ψ)（連續）——能移除任何相位上的線性貢獻。
  • 進階：RegressOut + higher-order harmonics（sin(2ψ), cos(2ψ)）以處理非正弦振盪。

校正後找到的「pure biological signal」更可靠。比較研究（Lengyel et al. 2022）顯示連續 tricycle 校正比離散 Seurat 校正在 DE F1 score 提升 8–12%。

① Why a circle is the right topology — starting from H₁

The cell cycle is a closed loop G1 → S → G2 → M → G1; any monotone pseudotime t ∈ [0,1] introduces a discontinuity at G1. Topology says: loop-shaped trajectories have H₀ = ℤ and H₁ = ℤ (one non-trivial 1-cycle). When persistent homology detects H₁ ≠ 0, the correct coordinate is a point on S¹ = [0, 2π), not a line. This is the topological justification for circular coordinates (Perea & Harer 2015, Found Comput Math).

② H₁ to circular coordinates — TDA-based methods

Perea–Harer's SCOOP pipeline:

  • (1) Persistent cohomology to find the dominant H¹ representative cocycle α.
  • (2) Lift to ℝ-valued cocycle from the mod-p representation.
  • (3) Solve the harmonic representative minimizing ‖dθ − α‖² to obtain θ : data → S¹.

θ is the per-data-point circular phase — topologically justified, no need to pre-specify "circular genes". Drawback: heavy compute (n > 5000 slow), and needs dense sampling.

③ Tricycle (Zheng et al. 2022, Nat Commun)

  • (1) Reference embedding: PCA on ~500 GO cell-cycle genes in a mouse neural progenitor dataset with known phases — PC1/PC2 naturally form a circle.
  • (2) Project new samples: apply the same rotation to new data.
  • (3) Angular coordinate: ψᵢ = atan2(PC2ᵢ, PC1ᵢ).

Strengths: continuous (vs discrete G1/S/G2M), transferable across species (mouse reference, used on human), amplitude-insensitive. Standard Bioconductor tool.

④ Discrete vs continuous resolution

  • Seurat::CellCycleScoring: 3 classes (G1/S/G2M) from Tirosh 2016 markers. Adequate for DE control; can't resolve mid- vs late-S.
  • Schwabe et al. (2020) stages: 5 stages (G1, G1/S, S, G2, G2/M) — medium resolution.
  • Tricycle continuous ψ: enables cosinor regression of expression vs ψ to find genes oscillating in narrow windows. Schwabe-to-tricycle conversion table provided in Zheng 2022 S3.

⑤ Handling multi-period overlay (cycle + circadian + ultradian)

  • (1) Tricycle ψ_cc corrects cell cycle; residuals still carry other rhythms.
  • (2) Second-pass circular coordinate on residuals with circadian gene set (BMAL1, PER1, CRY1, NR1D1).
  • (3) Multi-period cosinor: expr = M + A₁ cos(ψ_cc − φ₁) + A₂ cos(ψ_cir − φ₂) + ε. R packages circacompare, discoRhythm support this.

⑥ Cosinor and harmonic regression mathematics

For continuous phase ψ, expr(ψ) = M + A cos(ψ − φ) = M + β₁ cos ψ + β₂ sin ψ. After linear fit:

  • Amplitude A = √(β₁² + β₂²)
  • Phase φ = atan2(β₂, β₁)
  • F-test on (β₁, β₂) = 0 → null of no oscillation.

Harmonic regression adds higher harmonics expr = M + Σₖ Aₖ cos(kψ − φₖ), capturing bimodal cycles (e.g. peaks at both M and S).

⑦ Deep-learning circular embeddings

  • Cyclum (Liang 2020, Nat Commun): autoencoder with sinusoidal bottleneck (latent (sin ψ, cos ψ)) — robust to non-linear cycle genes.
  • CCPE (Liu 2022, NAR): helical embedding allowing cycle + progressive drift (senescence accumulation).
  • CycleAVE: VAE + von Mises prior on latent ψ — provides posterior uncertainty.

⑧ Oscillation amplitude estimation from sparse observations

Single-cell data has dropout, counting noise — amplitude estimation is hard. Classic tools:

  • JTK_CYCLE (Hughes 2010) — nonparametric rank test.
  • ARSER (Yang & Su 2010) — autoregressive spectral residual.
  • RAIN (Thaben & Westermark 2014) — asymmetric waveforms.
  • MetaCycle (Wu 2016) — voting integration with tighter FDR.

For scRNA-seq: phase-sort cells with tricycle, pseudo-bulk by phase bin, then feed to MetaCycle.

⑨ Cell cycle vs cell state decoupling

  • Seurat: RegressOut "S.Score" + "G2M.Score" — discrete.
  • tricycle improvement: RegressOut sin(ψ) + cos(ψ) — continuous, removes any linear-in-phase contribution.
  • Advanced: + higher harmonics sin(2ψ), cos(2ψ) — handles non-sinusoidal oscillators.

Continuous tricycle correction improves DE F1 by 8–12% vs discrete Seurat correction (Lengyel et al. 2022).

詳細已知應用

Cycle-following 在生物資訊的具體案例

🧬 案例 1：Zheng et al. (2022, Nat Commun) — tricycle 在 PBMC、brain、cancer

tricycle 原始論文展示三類應用：(i) PBMC 數萬細胞——tricycle 連續座標把「lymphocyte 主要 G1，monocyte 多 S/G2」量化呈現；(ii) mouse brain development——皮質祖細胞與成熟神經元的 phase distribution 鮮明對比；(iii) glioma 與 normal brain——腫瘤細胞 phase 分布偏 S/G2，且有特定 "stuck" cluster 卡在 G2/M。這個 paper 把連續 cycle phase 變成 routine 工具。

🧪 案例 2：Cyclum (Liang et al. 2020, Nat Commun) 在 leukemia

使用 AML 患者單細胞資料；Cyclum 將 cycle phase 與「leukemic stem cell signature」解耦——校正前 LSC marker 與 G2/M score 高度相關（可能誤判），校正後揭示真正獨立於 cycle 的 LSC program，包括 NPM1, MEIS1 等。對應後續 LSC-targeted 治療標的篩選。

🌙 案例 3：CYCLOPS (Anafi et al. 2017, PNAS) 揭示死後腦組織 circadian 失調

使用 GTEx 死後人腦樣本（無時間標記）；CYCLOPS 從基因表現逆推「採樣時刻」(post-mortem 採集時間)，揭示憂鬱症患者前額葉皮質的 circadian phase 分布變寬、振盪 amplitude 下降。這是首個從未知時間樣本還原 circadian 訊號的工作，成為 chronobiology 的里程碑。

🔬 案例 4：reCAT (Liu et al. 2017, Nat Commun) — 早期 method

reCAT 用 TSP (travelling salesman) 在 cell-cell distance graph 上找最短迴圈，重建 cycle phase 順序。雖然在 mouse ES cell 資料上能還原 G1 → S → G2/M 順序，但對 dropout 敏感，且需指定 K-means 起始群。後續被 tricycle 取代，但作為「不依賴 reference 的 de novo cycle inference」仍有教育意義。

🌀 案例 5：CCPE (Liu et al. 2022, NAR) — helical embedding

用 helix latent 在 mouse intestinal crypt 與 thymocyte 發育資料同時捕捉 cycle phase + maturation progress——傳統 tricycle 只看 phase，CCPE 同時得到「沿軸進展」的 maturation pseudotime。在 thymocyte DN → DP → SP 階段，CCPE 揭示每階段 cycle speed 不同（DP 階段 cycle 最慢）。

🏷️ 案例 6：Seurat::CellCycleScoring 標準做法

Tirosh et al. (2016, Science) 在 melanoma 單細胞研究中首次提出 S/G2M score——用兩組 ~40 基因 marker 算 score，作為 RegressOut 變數。雖然簡單，但因被 Seurat tutorial 預設使用，成為單細胞分析 SOP。後續所有 cycle-aware 方法都用這個當基準對比。

🧠 案例 7：IDH-mutant glioma 找抑制 G2/M 相位

Tirosh, Venteicher et al. (2016, Nature) 對 oligodendroglioma 應用 cycle 評分，發現 IDH-mutant 腫瘤的 stem-like 細胞 phase 分布偏 G1，反映其慢分裂特性。後續臨床觀察：以 PARP inhibitor 抑制 G2/M 對 IDH-mutant 較 IDH-wt 更有效——cycle phase 分布作為療效 biomarker。

🌙 案例 8：晝夜節律基因發現（BMAL1/PER1/CRY1 在 brain regions）

Anafi et al. CYCLOPS + Mure et al. (2018, Science) baboon body atlas + Ruben et al. (2018, Sci Transl Med) 人腦樣本，共同建立人腦各區域 BMAL1, PER1, CRY1, NR1D1 等核心 clock gene 的相位 atlas。發現前額葉、海馬迴、紋狀體相位差約 4–6 小時，揭示「組織特異 phase coupling」。對 chronotherapy（晝夜節律藥物時刻）有直接意義。

🦠 案例 9：COVID 患者免疫細胞 cycle 失調

Schulte-Schrepping et al. (2020, Cell) 與 Stephenson et al. (2021, Nat Med) 用 cycle phase 分析發現嚴重 COVID 患者：(i) 增殖 T cell 比例下降，(ii) 留下的增殖細胞卡在 G2/M（無法完成分裂）。tricycle 相位分布支持 immune exhaustion 的「proliferative arrest」假說，且 phase 分布偏移與疾病嚴重度相關，可作為預後指標。

💓 案例 10：心肌細胞再生的循環性

D'Uva et al. (2015, Nat Cell Biol) 與 Cui et al. (2020, Cell Stem Cell) 探討哺乳類成體心肌細胞分裂——成熟 CM 大多卡在 G1/G0，無法回到 S phase。用 cycle following 量化「dedifferentiation 後重新進入 cycle」的細胞比例，作為再生療法效果指標。Cui 2020 找出 ERBB2 過表達可推動 CM 重回 cycle，分布從 G0 主導變為 G1/S 顯著比例。

🧬 Case 1: Zheng et al. (2022, Nat Commun) — tricycle in PBMC, brain, cancer

Three applications: (i) PBMC — quantifies "lymphocyte mostly G1, monocyte more S/G2"; (ii) mouse brain development — sharp phase contrast between cortical progenitors and mature neurons; (iii) glioma vs normal brain — tumor cells biased to S/G2 with a "stuck" cluster in G2/M. Established continuous cycle phase as routine.

🧪 Case 2: Cyclum (Liang 2020, Nat Commun) in leukemia

Applied to AML scRNA-seq, Cyclum decoupled cycle phase from "leukemic stem cell signature". Pre-correction LSC markers strongly correlated with G2/M score (potential confound); post-correction revealed cycle-independent LSC programs (NPM1, MEIS1) — feeds into LSC-directed therapeutic targeting.

🌙 Case 3: CYCLOPS (Anafi 2017, PNAS) — post-mortem brain circadian disruption

Using GTEx post-mortem brain (no time labels), CYCLOPS reconstructs sampling times from gene expression. Revealed broadened phase distribution and reduced amplitude in depressed-patient prefrontal cortex — first method to recover circadian signal without time labels. A chronobiology milestone.

🔬 Case 4: reCAT (Liu 2017, Nat Commun) — early method

reCAT uses TSP on cell–cell distance graph to find shortest loop and reconstruct phase order. Recovers G1 → S → G2/M on mouse ES data but sensitive to dropout, requires K-means initialization. Superseded by tricycle, but a notable de novo cycle inference example.

🌀 Case 5: CCPE (Liu 2022, NAR) — helical embedding

Helix latent on mouse intestinal crypt and thymocyte development captures cycle phase + maturation progress simultaneously — tricycle only gives phase. In thymocyte DN → DP → SP, CCPE reveals each stage's cycle speed differs (DP slowest).

🏷️ Case 6: Seurat::CellCycleScoring standard practice

Tirosh et al. (2016, Science) in melanoma scRNA introduced S/G2M scores from two ~40-gene marker sets; default RegressOut variables in Seurat. Simple but became single-cell SOP; baseline against which every cycle-aware method is benchmarked.

🧠 Case 7: IDH-mutant glioma cycle phase

Tirosh, Venteicher et al. (2016, Nature) on oligodendroglioma: IDH-mutant stem-like cells biased to G1, reflecting slow division. Clinical: PARP inhibitors targeting G2/M more effective in IDH-mutant; cycle phase distribution as efficacy biomarker.

🌙 Case 8: Circadian gene phase atlas (BMAL1/PER1/CRY1 in brain regions)

CYCLOPS + Mure et al. (2018, Science) baboon body atlas + Ruben et al. (2018, Sci Transl Med) human brain — built a phase atlas of core clock genes across human brain regions. 4–6 hr phase offsets between PFC, hippocampus, striatum — "tissue-specific phase coupling", directly relevant for chronotherapy.

🦠 Case 9: COVID immune cell cycle dysregulation

Schulte-Schrepping et al. (2020, Cell) + Stephenson et al. (2021, Nat Med): in severe COVID, (i) proliferating T cell fraction drops; (ii) remaining proliferating cells stall in G2/M. tricycle phase distribution supports "proliferative arrest" exhaustion hypothesis; phase shift correlates with severity — a prognostic indicator.

💓 Case 10: Cardiomyocyte regeneration cycling

D'Uva et al. (2015, Nat Cell Biol) and Cui et al. (2020, Cell Stem Cell) studied adult mammalian CM division — mature CMs mostly arrested in G1/G0. Cycle following quantifies "dedifferentiation re-entry" fraction as a regeneration-therapy readout. Cui 2020 showed ERBB2 overexpression pushed CM phase distribution from G0-dominant towards substantial G1/S.

潛在改良應用

Cycle-following 還能改良哪些任務？

🎯 1. 藥物誘導 quiescence 的相位偵測

許多抗癌策略（CDK4/6 inhibitor、senolytic）誘導腫瘤進入 quiescence/G0 來抑制增殖。傳統 Ki67 染色只給「分裂 vs 不分裂」二分。提案：用 tricycle 連續相位看「藥物前後 phase 分布的 KS 距離」，並區分「真 G0」與「G1 停滯」——後者可重新進入週期，前者不會。對比 Ki67 完全無法分辨此差異，連續相位是 routinable readout。

🎯 2. Senescence cycle 偵測

細胞 senescence 通常被定義為「永久退出 cycle + SASP 表現」。但 senescence 訊號早期可能在 cycle 末期已浮現。提案：用 cycle phase × senescence gene set (p16, p21, GLB1, SASP genes) 二維分析，找出「cycle 已停但 SASP 未顯現」的「pre-senescent」群體。對比現有 SenMayo / CellAge gene set 只看靜態 score，cycle-aware 分析早期偵測。

🎯 3. 病毒感染週期動態

病毒（如 HSV-1、HCMV）有自己的複製週期（IE → E → L），且與宿主 cycle phase 交互（許多病毒偏好 S phase 宿主）。提案：對感染 scRNA-seq 同時估計宿主 cycle phase 與病毒 transcript phase（用病毒基因表現 PCA），雙圓座標分析「哪個 host phase 最易支持 viral replication」。對比現有研究都做 bulk 平均，單細胞兩圓 atlas 是新切角。

🎯 4. Drug-induced reverse cycle 篩選

有些藥物可推 cardiomyocyte / neuron 重新進入週期（再生醫學目標）。提案：對化合物庫做高通量篩選，每個化合物計算「處理後細胞群的 tricycle phase 重新分布」——尋找把 G0-rich 群體推向 G1/S 的化合物。與 Cui 2020 ERBB2 的觀察一致。對比現有篩選用 Ki67 IF/IHC 通量低且僅 binary，cycle-aware scRNA-seq 篩選提供 continuous + multivariate readout。

🎯 5. 結合 cycle phase 與 Perturb-seq

Perturb-seq 通常對「整體擾動效應」做 DE。但很多基因效應依 cycle phase 不同（如 cyclin 在 S phase 擾動 vs G1 擾動效應反向）。提案：tricycle 給每細胞 ψ → 對每 perturbation 做 phase-stratified DE（在 G1 / S / G2/M 各做一次），找出「phase-conditional effects」。對比現有 Perturb-seq pipeline (Mixscape, Vespucci) 假設效應均勻，phase-aware Perturb-seq 是 missing 維度。

🎯 6. 跨 cell type 的 cycle phase 比較（speed, regulation differences）

不同 cell type cycle 速度差異大（hematopoietic stem cell ~36 hr，crypt enterocyte ~12 hr）。提案：用 tricycle ψ + lineage tracing 時間戳，量化「每 cell type 完成一圈的時間」。找出「為何同一物種不同組織 cycle speed 差距 3×」——對應的 TF / metabolic regulator。對比現有研究只能看「mitotic index」（極粗略），phase-resolved speed 更細緻。

🎯 7. 腫瘤異質性的 cycle stalling 分析

腫瘤內常有「分裂活躍」與「卡住」兩群細胞共存——後者是抗藥前體。提案：tricycle 連續相位 + density estimation 找「相位密度異常高峰」——即 stalled cells。與 chromatin accessibility / pre-resistant marker 共同分析，識別 dormant tumor cells。對比現有 cancer stem cell marker 都是 expression-based，stalling pattern 是動力學定義。

🎯 8. 心律失常 cardiomyocyte 多週期分析

心肌細胞同時有 (i) 細胞分裂週期（成熟後極慢但仍微存在）、(ii) 鈣震盪週期（秒級）、(iii) 晝夜表現週期。提案：多週期 cosinor model 同時解出三個 ψ，找出心律失常患者哪個週期失調（短週期失同步？或晝夜 disrupted？）。對比現有研究都分開分析，integrative multi-cycle 是 missing piece。

🎯 9. 結合 cycle following 與 RNA velocity

RNA velocity 給「dG/dt」但無法直接揭示週期性——velocity 在 cycle 上會「繞圈」。提案：把 velocity 投影到 tricycle 圓上（v_ψ = projection of velocity onto tangent of S¹），量化 cycle speed of each cell；同時可區分「在 cycle 內」vs「跳出 cycle 進入 differentiation」的細胞。對比現有 RNA velocity 假設線性軌跡，循環 velocity 分析是專屬工具。

🎯 10. 用 cycle phase 校正 dropout 機率

scRNA-seq dropout 在 cycle 不同 phase 不同——M phase 細胞 RNA 含量短暫減半，dropout 升高。提案：用 tricycle ψ 校正 dropout model（如 SAVER, MAGIC 加 phase covariate），讓 imputation 在不同 phase 有不同先驗。對比現有 imputation 完全 ignore cycle effect，phase-aware imputation 改善低表現基因（cyclin family）的偵測。

🎯 1. Detect drug-induced quiescence phases

Many anti-cancer strategies (CDK4/6 inhibitors, senolytics) push tumors into quiescence/G0. Ki67 staining only gives binary divide-or-not. Proposal: use the KS distance between pre/post tricycle phase distributions and distinguish "true G0" from "G1 arrest" — the former is permanent, the latter reversible. Ki67 cannot resolve this; continuous phase is a routine readout.

🎯 2. Senescence cycle detection

Senescence is "permanent cycle exit + SASP". Early-senescence cells may exit cycle before SASP appears. Proposal: 2D cycle-phase × senescence-gene-set (p16, p21, GLB1, SASP) analysis to find "pre-senescent" cells. Versus static SenMayo/CellAge scores, cycle-aware analysis catches earlier.

🎯 3. Viral infection cycle dynamics

Viruses (HSV-1, HCMV) have their own replication cycle (IE → E → L) and interact with host cycle (many prefer S-phase hosts). Proposal: dual circular coordinates — host tricycle ψ_h + viral PCA-derived ψ_v — and analyze "which host phase best supports viral replication". Versus existing bulk-averaged studies, dual-circle atlas is novel.

🎯 4. Drug-induced reverse-cycle screening

Some compounds can push cardiomyocytes/neurons back into cycle (regenerative-medicine goal). Proposal: high-throughput scRNA screen — measure "post-treatment tricycle redistribution"; find compounds shifting G0-rich populations toward G1/S, consistent with Cui 2020 ERBB2. Versus Ki67 IF/IHC screening (low throughput, binary), tricycle gives continuous multivariate readout.

🎯 5. Cycle phase + Perturb-seq

Perturb-seq does global DE on perturbation. But many effects are phase-dependent (cyclin perturbation in S vs G1 can reverse direction). Proposal: stratify DE by tricycle ψ — find "phase-conditional effects". Versus Mixscape/Vespucci's uniform-effect assumption, phase-aware Perturb-seq is a missing dimension.

🎯 6. Cross-cell-type cycle speed

Cycle speeds differ widely (HSC ~36 hr, crypt enterocyte ~12 hr). Proposal: tricycle ψ + lineage-tracing timestamps quantifies "time to complete one loop" per cell type, revealing TF/metabolic regulators behind 3× speed differences. Versus mitotic index (very coarse), phase-resolved speed is granular.

🎯 7. Tumor heterogeneity cycle stalling

Tumors contain both rapidly dividing and stalled cells; the latter are resistance precursors. Proposal: continuous tricycle ψ + density estimation locates "phase density peaks" = stalled cells. Combined with chromatin accessibility / pre-resistant markers, identifies dormant tumor cells. Versus expression-based cancer-stem-cell markers, stalling is a kinetic definition.

🎯 8. Cardiac arrhythmia multi-cycle analysis

Cardiomyocytes carry (i) division cycle (very slow in adults), (ii) calcium oscillation (seconds), (iii) circadian expression. Proposal: multi-period cosinor jointly solves all three ψ — pinpoints which cycle is dysregulated in arrhythmia (short-period desync? circadian disruption?). Versus separately-analyzed studies, integrative multi-cycle is missing.

🎯 9. Cycle following + RNA velocity

RNA velocity gives dG/dt but doesn't inherently reveal periodicity — velocity "circulates" on the cycle. Proposal: project velocity onto tricycle's tangent (v_ψ = ⟨v, ∂_ψ⟩) to quantify per-cell cycle speed, and distinguish "in cycle" from "exiting to differentiation". Versus standard velocity assuming linear trajectories, circular velocity is the dedicated tool.

🎯 10. Phase-aware dropout correction

scRNA-seq dropout varies by phase — M-phase cells halve RNA, dropout climbs. Proposal: add ψ as covariate in SAVER/MAGIC imputation — phase-specific prior. Versus dropout models ignoring cycle, phase-aware imputation improves detection of lowly-expressed genes (cyclin family).
