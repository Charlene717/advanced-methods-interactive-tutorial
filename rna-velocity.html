RNA 速度與動力系統

從 spliced / unspliced 比例反推細胞「正在往哪個方向變化」——把靜態 snapshot 變成有向量場的動力系統。

From spliced/unspliced ratios, infer where a cell is heading — turn static snapshots into a dynamical system with a vector field.

核心概念

一個 RNA 的生命週期

每個基因的 RNA 都會經歷：轉錄 (α) 產生 unspliced (pre-mRNA, u) → 剪接 (β) 變成 spliced (mRNA, s) → 降解 (γ) 消失。La Manno et al. (2018, Nature) 觀察到：scRNA-seq 的 reads 同時包含 spliced 與 unspliced（intron-containing），兩者比例變化就能反推細胞正在「合成中」還是「衰減中」。

動力方程：

du/dt = α(t) − β·u
ds/dt = β·u − γ·s

RNA velocity 即 ds/dt——s 的瞬時變化率，正值代表基因表現「正在上升」，負值代表「正在下降」。把所有基因的 velocity 組合成一個向量，就是細胞在表現量空間的「方向」。

Every gene's RNA cycles through: transcription (α) → unspliced (pre-mRNA, u) → splicing (β) → spliced (mRNA, s) → degradation (γ). La Manno et al. (2018, Nature) noticed: scRNA-seq reads contain both spliced and unspliced (intron-containing), and their ratio reveals whether the cell is synthesizing or degrading.

Dynamics:

du/dt = α(t) − β·u
ds/dt = β·u − γ·s

RNA velocity = ds/dt — the instantaneous rate of change of spliced mRNA. Positive = "going up"; negative = "going down". Stacking all genes gives a velocity vector pointing where the cell is heading.

演算法

三代方法演進

velocyto (2018)

假設每個基因處於穩態 (steady state)：γ·s = β·u → 從 u/s 散布圖估計斜率 γ。簡單但對非穩態細胞不準。

Assumes steady state per gene: γ·s = β·u → estimate γ from u/s slope. Simple but inaccurate for non-steady cells.

scVelo (2020)

Bergen 等人提出 stochastic 與 dynamical 兩種模型，dynamical mode 用 EM 同時估計 α、β、γ 與每個細胞的「潛在時間」。

Bergen et al. introduce stochastic and dynamical models. Dynamical mode uses EM to jointly estimate α, β, γ and each cell's latent time.

Dynamo (Qiu 2022)

最完整的框架：重建連續向量場 f(x)，可進一步算 Jacobian（基因間調控）、加速度、發散度等動力學量。支援代謝標記 (metabolic labeling) 資料。

Most complete framework: reconstructs continuous vector field f(x); computes Jacobian (gene regulation), acceleration, divergence. Supports metabolic labeling data.

CellRank (Lange 2022)

把 RNA velocity（或任何方向訊號）轉成馬可夫鏈轉移矩陣，計算細胞的「命運機率」——哪些細胞會走向哪些終態。

Converts RNA velocity (or any directional signal) into a Markov transition matrix; computes cell fate probabilities — which cells will reach which terminal states.

互動模擬

動力學 ODE 模擬

數值解 du/dt 與 ds/dt。觀察「誘導期」u 先漲、s 才跟上；「衰減期」u 先掉、s 才下降。velocity = ds/dt 在誘導期為正、衰減期為負。

Numerically integrate du/dt and ds/dt. During "induction" u rises first, s follows; during "repression" u drops first, s follows. velocity = ds/dt is positive during induction and negative during repression.

生物應用與陷阱

慎用：velocity 不是萬靈丹

✅ 適合場景

轉錄動力學主導的系統：早期發育、神經分化、造血。基因 α/γ 變化清晰可估。

Transcription-dominated systems: early development, neural differentiation, hematopoiesis. α/γ changes are clear.

❌ 不適合場景

穩態組織（成熟肝、腦）、慢動力學基因、多重剪接同工型（multi-isoform）讀取會干擾估計。Bergen et al. (2024) 系統性討論 velocity 假設失敗的案例。

Steady-state tissues (mature liver, brain), slow-dynamic genes, multi-isoform interference. Bergen et al. (2024) systematically reviews failure modes.

代謝標記時代

scNT-seq、sci-fate 等用 4sU 標記新合成 RNA，直接量「新 vs 舊」mRNA，避免依賴 splicing 訊號。Dynamo 直接支援這類資料。

scNT-seq, sci-fate label newly synthesized RNA with 4sU — directly measure "new vs old" mRNA, sidestepping splicing assumptions. Dynamo natively supports this.

向量場分析

重建後可在連續空間求 Jacobian → 推估基因–基因調控；求發散度 → 細胞匯聚點 (attractors)；求 Lyapunov → 軌跡穩定性。

After reconstruction: Jacobian → gene–gene regulation; divergence → attractors; Lyapunov → trajectory stability.

深入解析

深入解析 RNA Velocity：動力學模型、失敗模式與向量場

① velocyto vs scVelo stochastic vs scVelo dynamical 的數學差異

三個主流方法本質上都求解 du/dt = α − β·u、ds/dt = β·u − γ·s 這組常微分方程，但對 α(t) 的假設與估計策略不同：

  • velocyto (La Manno 2018, Nature)：採用 steady-state 假設——把每個基因 (u, s) 散布圖的「上極端細胞」當成 ds/dt = 0 的穩態，以線性回歸估 γ = u/s 的斜率，再求 v = u − γ·s。優點是極快；缺點是若資料未涵蓋穩態（早期短時序），γ 嚴重低估。
  • scVelo stochastic (Bergen 2020)：把 α、β、γ 視為常數，但用 二階矩（mean + variance）同時估計，等同於 stochastic chemical master equation 的矩匹配。比 steady-state 多用一個方程，γ 估計更穩。
  • scVelo dynamical：把 α(t) 視為 switch (on/off)，引入 latent time τᵢ 與 switching time t_s，用 Bayesian EM 對每個基因獨立 fit 4 個參數 (α_on, α_off, β, γ) + 每細胞 τᵢ。輸出的 latent_time 是跨基因共識，可當 pseudotime。代價：每個基因要跑數百次 EM，比 stochastic 慢 10–50 倍。

② Gene-specific kinetics vs shared kinetics

scVelo 預設每基因獨立估 (α, β, γ)——適合「動力學差異大」的基因（如 lineage-specific TF）。但對 housekeeping、低表現基因，獨立估計噪音大；此時可用 shared β across genes（假設 splicing rate 為細胞 global 屬性，由 SRSF 家族決定）或 cluster-shared α(t)（同 cell type 共享轉錄率）。UniTVelo (Gao 2022, Nat Commun) 就是把 α(t) 設為跨基因共享的 radial basis function，減少參數量。

③ Multi-modal velocity：MultiVelo 與 veloVI

  • MultiVelo (Li & Manno 2022, Nat Biotech)：把 ATAC peak 開放性 c(t) 納入模型——du/dt = α·c − β·u，假設染色質先開、再轉錄。在 10x Multiome 資料上對 lineage priming 偵測比純 RNA velocity 更早 0.5–1.5 個 pseudotime 單位。
  • veloVI (Gayoso et al. 2023, Nat Methods)：用 deep variational autoencoder 把 (α, β, γ, τ) 視為 latent，end-to-end neural network 估計。提供 uncertainty per cell，可區分「真實 velocity」與「模型不確定」。在 corrupted data 上比 scVelo dynamical 穩健。
  • LinearVelocity / VeloAE：把 velocity 學成 latent embedding 中的線性向量，避開 ODE 假設，計算極快但物理意義較弱。

④ 代謝標記（metabolic labeling）繞過 splicing 假設

傳統 RNA velocity 依賴「intron read = unspliced proxy」這個近似——很多基因 intron read 極少（短內含子、深降解），導致 velocity 估計失敗。4sU labeling（4-thiouridine 培養細胞數小時後抽 RNA，新合成 RNA 帶 T→C 突變）能直接量「新 vs 舊」mRNA：

  • scSLAM-seq (Erhard 2019)、scNT-seq (Qiu 2020)、sci-fate (Cao 2020)：三種主流單細胞 4sU 平台。
  • Dynamo (Qiu et al. 2022, Cell)：對應的估計框架——對 labeled fraction 直接 fit α 與 degradation，不需 splicing model。同時支援 velocity vector field reconstruction。

⑤ RNA velocity 的失敗模式（Bergen et al. 2024 綜述）

Bergen et al. (2024, Mol Syst Biol) 系統性整理 velocity 失敗的 7 大情境：

  • (i) Saturated kinetics：穩態組織所有基因 ds/dt ≈ 0，velocity ≈ 0；任何擾動的 velocity 都是噪音放大。
  • (ii) Multiple kinetic regimes：同一基因在不同 lineage 有不同 α/γ（boost vs repression），單一線性 fit 會給出錯誤方向。
  • (iii) Batch confounds：不同批次間 splicing efficiency 微差，會被誤判為「velocity 方向相反」。
  • (iv) Complex kinetics：有 negative feedback / 多級調控的基因 ODE 假設失效。
  • (v) Transcriptional bursting：α 是 stochastic on/off，平均化扭曲估計。
  • (vi) Read length bias：3' counting (10x) 對 intron 取樣不平衡，影響 unspliced count。
  • (vii) Ambient RNA contamination：背景 RNA 主要為 spliced，使 γ 估計偏高。

⑥ 從離散 velocity 到連續向量場：Dynamo 的 sparseVFC

單細胞 velocity 是離散的「每細胞一支箭」，無法直接做 dynamical systems 分析。Dynamo 採用 sparseVFC (Sparse Vector Field Consensus, Ma 2013) 與 Gaussian Process regression 把離散箭頭重建為連續函數 f: ℝᵈ → ℝᵈ。重建後可：

  • 對 f 求 Jacobian ∂fᵢ/∂xⱼ——讀作「基因 j 變動時，基因 i 的 velocity 如何改變」≈ 即時 GRN 邊強度。
  • 求 divergence ∇·f——負值 = 軌跡匯聚（attractor / 終態細胞型別），正值 = 發散（source / 早期祖細胞）。
  • 求 acceleration df/dt——大幅加速處 = fate decision point（細胞「快速跨界」處）。
  • 用 least action path 找兩個 attractor 之間的最低能量軌跡——對應潛在的 reprogramming 路徑。

⑦ 從 velocity 到 fate：CellRank 的 Markov 矩陣框架

Lange et al. (2022, Nat Methods) 的 CellRank 把 velocity 轉為 cell-to-cell transition matrix：對每細胞 i，依「指向哪些鄰居」加權成 row-stochastic matrix P。再對 P 做 GPCCA (Generalized Perron Cluster Cluster Analysis) 找 metastable states（≈ attractor），最後計算每細胞的 absorbing probability π_i→k（細胞 i 最終命運為終態 k 的機率）。CellRank 2 進一步支援多 kernel 整合（velocity + pseudotime + similarity + CytoTRACE）。

① Mathematical differences: velocyto vs scVelo stochastic vs scVelo dynamical

All three solve du/dt = α − β·u, ds/dt = β·u − γ·s, but differ in assumptions on α(t) and the estimation strategy:

  • velocyto (La Manno 2018, Nature): steady-state assumption — treats upper-extremal cells in (u,s) scatter as ds/dt = 0, regresses γ as the slope u/s, then v = u − γ·s. Fast, but γ is severely under-estimated if data does not span steady state.
  • scVelo stochastic (Bergen 2020, Nat Biotech): α, β, γ constant, but uses second-moment matching (mean + variance) from the stochastic chemical master equation — more stable γ.
  • scVelo dynamical: α(t) as switch (on/off), latent times τᵢ + switching time t_s, full Bayesian EM per gene fitting (α_on, α_off, β, γ, τᵢ). Outputs gene-consensus latent_time usable as pseudotime. Cost: 10–50× slower than stochastic.

② Gene-specific vs shared kinetics

scVelo defaults to per-gene independent (α, β, γ) — good for lineage-specific TFs. For housekeeping / lowly expressed genes, the per-gene fits are noisy; use shared β across genes (splicing rate as a global cell property driven by SRSF family) or cluster-shared α(t) (same cell type shares transcription rate). UniTVelo (Gao 2022, Nat Commun) imposes a globally shared RBF α(t).

③ Multi-modal velocity: MultiVelo and veloVI

  • MultiVelo (Li & Manno 2022, Nat Biotech): incorporates ATAC peak openness c(t): du/dt = α·c − β·u (chromatin opens before transcription). Detects lineage priming 0.5–1.5 pseudotime units earlier than RNA-only velocity in 10x Multiome.
  • veloVI (Gayoso et al. 2023, Nat Methods): deep variational autoencoder treating (α, β, γ, τ) as latent, end-to-end neural estimation. Provides per-cell uncertainty; more robust on corrupted data than scVelo dynamical.
  • LinearVelocity / VeloAE: velocity as linear vector in a learned latent — fast but weaker physical interpretation.

④ Metabolic labeling bypasses the splicing assumption

Classical RNA velocity uses "intron read = unspliced proxy" — many genes have very few intron reads (short introns, degradation), making velocity unreliable. 4sU labeling (4-thiouridine pulse → newly transcribed RNA carries T→C mutations) directly measures new vs old mRNA:

  • scSLAM-seq (Erhard 2019), scNT-seq (Qiu 2020), sci-fate (Cao 2020): three main single-cell 4sU platforms.
  • Dynamo (Qiu et al. 2022, Cell): the matching estimation framework — fits α and degradation from labeled fraction directly, no splicing model needed. Also supports continuous vector field reconstruction.

⑤ Failure modes (Bergen et al. 2024 review)

Bergen et al. (2024, Mol Syst Biol) systematically catalog 7 failure regimes:

  • (i) Saturated kinetics: steady-state tissues — ds/dt ≈ 0 everywhere, "velocity" is noise.
  • (ii) Multiple kinetic regimes: same gene with different α/γ across lineages → linear fit gives wrong direction.
  • (iii) Batch confounds: splicing-efficiency differences mistaken for opposite velocity.
  • (iv) Complex kinetics: negative feedback or multi-step regulation breaks the ODE.
  • (v) Transcriptional bursting: stochastic on/off α biases averages.
  • (vi) Read-length bias: 3' counting (10x) under-samples introns.
  • (vii) Ambient RNA contamination: ambient mostly spliced → inflates γ.

⑥ Discrete velocity → continuous vector field: Dynamo's sparseVFC

Single-cell velocity is a discrete "arrow per cell"; not directly usable for dynamical-systems analysis. Dynamo applies sparseVFC (Ma 2013) + Gaussian Process regression to reconstruct continuous f: ℝᵈ → ℝᵈ. Then:

  • Jacobian ∂fᵢ/∂xⱼ = "how does velocity of gene i shift when gene j changes" ≈ instantaneous GRN edge weight.
  • Divergence ∇·f: negative = attractor (terminal state); positive = source (progenitor).
  • Acceleration df/dt: peaks at fate decision points (sharp lineage choices).
  • Least action paths between attractors → candidate reprogramming routes.

⑦ Velocity to fate: CellRank's Markov matrix framework

Lange et al. (2022, Nat Methods) CellRank converts velocity into a row-stochastic cell-to-cell transition matrix P, applies GPCCA to find metastable states (≈ attractors), and computes per-cell absorbing probability π_i→k (final fate of cell i). CellRank 2 supports multi-kernel fusion (velocity + pseudotime + similarity + CytoTRACE).

詳細已知應用

RNA Velocity 在生物資訊的具體案例

🧬 案例 1：La Manno et al. (2018, Nature) — 第一個 velocity 應用

原始 velocyto 論文，使用 mouse 腎上腺髓質 chromaffin 細胞分化資料。從多能祖細胞 (SCP) 出發，velocity 箭頭清楚指向 chromaffin cells 與交感神經元兩條 lineage 的分叉點；同時揭露一群「過渡細胞」其 velocity 方向尚未決定——後來被定義為 fate-undecided intermediate。這個案例確立了 velocity 的核心 narrative：在「靜態 UMAP」上加方向。

🧪 案例 2：Bergen et al. (2020, Nat Biotech) — pancreas endocrinogenesis

scVelo 的首發應用：胰島 endocrine 分化（E15.5 mouse embryo）。dynamical model 不僅還原 α/β/δ 三種 endocrine 細胞的分化軌跡，更找出「Ngn3-high → Fev-positive → 終態」的時序，latent_time 與已知的轉錄因子瀑布完全吻合。這個資料集成為後續 velocity 方法的標準 benchmark。

🌱 案例 3：Qiu et al. (2022, Cell) — Dynamo 在 mouse 胚胎皮膚

結合 scNT-seq labeling 與 Dynamo vector field 分析，在 E13.5 mouse 胚胎皮膚找出 hair follicle、interfollicular epidermis 兩條 lineage 的 least action paths，並用 Jacobian 推 GRN——預測「Lef1 → Krt15」這條邊，後續用 CRISPRi 在 keratinocyte organoid 驗證為真。Dynamo 是首個能做「velocity 預測 perturbation」的工具。

🫁 案例 4：Lange et al. (2022, Nat Methods) — CellRank 在 lung regeneration

原發 CellRank 論文使用 mouse 肺受 bleomycin 損傷後再生資料。Velocity + CellRank 找出 4 個 metastable states（progenitor、AT2、AT1、damaged AT2），並計算 progenitor → AT1 vs progenitor → AT2 的 absorbing probability，發現損傷後 AT2 → AT1 transdifferentiation 機率顯著上升——這個觀察後續由 Strunz et al. 用 lineage tracing 直接驗證。

🩸 案例 5：MultiVelo on PBMC 10x Multiome

Li & Manno (2022, Nat Biotech) 對 PBMC Multiome 資料，比較 RNA-only velocity 與 ATAC+RNA MultiVelo：在 myeloid → monocyte 分支上，MultiVelo 偵測到 CEBPB locus 染色質開放比 mRNA 上升早 6 小時，符合 chromatin priming 模型。RNA-only velocity 完全看不到這個 lead。

🤖 案例 6：veloVI 在 corrupted data

Gayoso et al. (2023, Nat Methods) 故意對 dentate gyrus 資料注入 20% 的 dropout 與 batch noise；scVelo dynamical 的箭頭方向在 30% 細胞上反轉，veloVI 透過 uncertainty quantification 把不確定細胞自動「遮蔽」，整體軌跡保持正確。提供首個 velocity 結果可靠性指標。

🧪 案例 7：Bastidas-Ponce et al. (2019) — mouse pancreas E15.5

更早的 mouse pancreas 資料（與 Bergen 2020 同來源），velocyto 揭示 Ngn3 transient population 的雙向命運：部分回流為 ductal progenitor、部分往 endocrine。這是 velocity 首次揭示「反向 velocity」現象，後來成為「fate flexibility」的經典證據。

🧠 案例 8：Hochgerner et al. (2018) — dentate gyrus development

mouse hippocampus dentate gyrus postnatal neurogenesis 資料；velocity 還原 radial glia → neuroblast → mature granule cell 的時序，並偵測 P12–P35 期間「新生 granule cell」的持續產生——對應海馬迴成體 neurogenesis。被 Bergen 2020 用為主要 benchmark。

🦠 案例 9：Tirosh group cancer velocity (melanoma)

Jerby-Arnon et al. (2018, Cell) 對人類 melanoma scRNA-seq 應用 velocity，發現「TCF7 high → MITF high」的方向性，對應 stem-like → differentiated 轉變；更發現 immunotherapy resistance program 在 velocity 上是「正在進行式」——預示治療失敗前 tumor 已啟動 program。

🔬 案例 10：Perturb-seq 軌跡推論的擴展

Replogle et al. (2022, Cell)、Jin et al. (2024) 對 Perturb-seq 資料應用 velocity——在 essential gene knockout 後分析 velocity 方向變化，找出哪些擾動「卡住」軌跡（velocity → 0）、哪些「加速」（velocity magnitude 上升）。提供首個系統性的「動力學擾動」分析。

🧬 Case 1: La Manno et al. (2018, Nature) — first velocity application

The original velocyto paper used mouse adrenal medulla chromaffin differentiation. Velocity arrows clearly pointed from the SCP progenitor towards a chromaffin / sympathetic neuron bifurcation; a population of fate-undecided intermediates was first formally identified through their indeterminate velocity directions. This case established the core narrative: directionality layered onto a static UMAP.

🧪 Case 2: Bergen et al. (2020, Nat Biotech) — pancreas endocrinogenesis

scVelo's flagship application: mouse E15.5 endocrine differentiation. The dynamical model recovered α/β/δ cell trajectories and the Ngn3-high → Fev-positive → terminal order; latent_time matched the known TF cascade. This dataset became the standard velocity benchmark.

🌱 Case 3: Qiu et al. (2022, Cell) — Dynamo on mouse embryonic skin

Combined scNT-seq labeling with Dynamo vector field analysis on E13.5 mouse skin. Found least action paths between hair-follicle and interfollicular-epidermis lineages; Jacobian-based GRN predicted "Lef1 → Krt15", later validated by CRISPRi in keratinocyte organoids. Dynamo is the first tool that performs perturbation prediction from velocity.

🫁 Case 4: Lange et al. (2022, Nat Methods) — CellRank on lung regeneration

The launching CellRank paper used bleomycin-injured mouse lung regeneration. Velocity + CellRank found 4 metastable states (progenitor, AT2, AT1, damaged AT2) and computed absorbing probabilities, revealing increased AT2 → AT1 transdifferentiation after injury — later confirmed via lineage tracing (Strunz et al.).

🩸 Case 5: MultiVelo on 10x Multiome PBMC

Li & Manno (2022, Nat Biotech): MultiVelo on PBMC Multiome detected CEBPB chromatin opening 6 hours before mRNA rise during myeloid → monocyte commitment — consistent with chromatin priming. RNA-only velocity completely misses this lead.

🤖 Case 6: veloVI on corrupted data

Gayoso et al. (2023, Nat Methods) injected 20% dropout + batch noise into dentate-gyrus data. scVelo dynamical reversed direction on 30% of cells; veloVI used uncertainty to auto-mask unreliable cells and preserved global trajectories. First reliability metric for velocity.

🧪 Case 7: Bastidas-Ponce et al. (2019) — E15.5 mouse pancreas

velocyto on a slightly earlier pancreas dataset revealed a bidirectional fate of the Ngn3 transient population — partial flow back into ductal progenitor, partial onward to endocrine. Now a classic example of "fate flexibility" inferred from reverse velocity.

🧠 Case 8: Hochgerner et al. (2018) — dentate gyrus development

Postnatal hippocampal neurogenesis; velocity recovered radial glia → neuroblast → mature granule cell order and detected persistent newborn granule cell production from P12–P35 (adult neurogenesis signature). A primary benchmark in Bergen 2020.

🦠 Case 9: Tirosh group cancer velocity (melanoma)

Jerby-Arnon et al. (2018, Cell) applied velocity to human melanoma, finding directional flow "TCF7-high → MITF-high" (stem-like → differentiated). They further showed an immunotherapy-resistance program is "in progress" in velocity terms — anticipating treatment failure before tumor escape.

🔬 Case 10: Perturb-seq trajectory extension

Replogle et al. (2022, Cell) and Jin et al. (2024) applied velocity to Perturb-seq, asking how essential-gene knockouts altered velocity magnitude/direction — identifying perturbations that "stall" (velocity → 0) or "accelerate" (magnitude up) trajectories. First systematic kinetic-perturbation analysis.

潛在改良應用

RNA Velocity 還能改良哪些任務？

🎯 1. 抗藥前體（drug-tolerant persister）動態偵測

腫瘤 heterogeneity 中常存在「pre-resistant」群——治療前已具部分抗藥 program。傳統做法（DE on resistant vs sensitive）會錯過這群「正在形成」的細胞。提案：用 velocity 方向（指向 resistant terminal state 的細胞）找出「抗藥 in progress」群體，比 cluster-based DE 早 1–2 細胞世代偵測。對比現有 single-cell perturbation 工具如 Augur 只給靜態 priority score。

🎯 2. T cell exhaustion 軌跡動力學

T cell exhaustion 是漸進過程 (Tprog → Tex_int → Tex_term)，每個 stage 的 TF kinetics 差異大（TOX、TCF7、TIM3）。提案：用 dynamical scVelo 對 chronic LCMV / 慢性病毒感染資料做 stage-specific α/γ 估計，再用 Jacobian 找「每個 stage 的關鍵 TF」。對比現有靜態 trajectory（Slingshot 只給 pseudotime），velocity 還給每個基因的「打開 / 關閉時刻」。

🎯 3. iPSC reprogramming 動態量化

Yamanaka 因子重編程效率僅 0.1–1%，瓶頸在「partial reprogrammed」群體。提案：用 velocity 偵測 OSKM 誘導後第 4–8 天的「reprogramming velocity」——指向 iPSC state 的細胞 vs 卡在中間的細胞。結合 Dynamo least action path 找最低能量重編程路徑，識別關鍵 fate barrier。對比現有 Waddington-OT 用「靜態」snapshot 推測，velocity 是即時動力學。

🎯 4. 整合 metabolic profiling（13C labeling + scNT-seq）

scNT-seq 標 RNA 新合成，13C glucose labeling 標代謝物。兩者結合可推「轉錄 velocity → 代謝 velocity」的因果。提案：對應的 dual-omic 估計框架（擴展 Dynamo 支援 metabolite flux），找出代謝轉換驅動的轉錄變化。比現有 scMetabolism / Compass 只給靜態 flux 預測強得多。

🎯 5. 用 velocity Jacobian 改良 GRN 推論

現有 GRN 工具（SCENIC, GENIE3, GRNBoost）都從靜態表現推 TF–target 邊。Velocity Jacobian ∂(dG_i/dt)/∂G_j 直接是 "G_j 變動如何加速 G_i 的轉錄"——是真實的因果方向。提案：scVelo + sparseVFC + Jacobian pipeline 作為「動力學 GRN」工具，與 SCENIC 整合（SCENIC 給候選 TF→target，Jacobian 給方向與時刻）。對比 SCENIC 完全靜態，Jacobian-GRN 是時間解析的。

🎯 6. 細胞通訊動態（哪個 ligand 觸發了 receptor velocity 上升）

CellChat 從靜態表現乘積推 L–R 強度。但訊號傳遞本質是動態的——ligand 達閾值 → receptor 表現變化 → 下游基因 velocity 改變。提案：對 paired sender-receiver 細胞群，用 Granger-causality on velocity 找「sender ligand velocity → receiver downstream velocity」的時序，這比 CellChat 靜態 score 更接近真實 signaling cascade。

🎯 7. 跨物種 velocity 比較

跨物種發育比較（mouse vs human cortex）目前只比較表現模式，無法比較「速度」。提案：對齊 orthologue velocity，量化「mouse 與 human 同一發育階段的 α/γ 是否保守」——快慢差異會揭示物種特異的調控。例如 human neocortex 神經元成熟比 mouse 慢數倍，是否反映於 velocity magnitude？對比現有 cross-species comparison 只比 expression mean。

🎯 8. 結合 RNA velocity 與空間（spatialVelo）

10x Visium / Stereo-seq 有空間 + RNA 但無時間。提案：對組織切片每點求 velocity，再分析 velocity 的空間 divergence pattern——找出組織中的「動力學熱點」（如 wound healing front、tumor invasion edge）。對比現有 spatialDE 只找靜態空間異質基因，spatialVelo 找「正在變化的空間區域」。

🎯 9. 預測藥物擾動後的細胞 trajectory

藥物篩選通常看「擾動 24 hr 後 expression 差異」。但藥物效應是動態展開的——早期 transcriptional response 與晚期 phenotypic change 不同。提案：用 Dynamo 從 baseline velocity vector field 「外推」藥物擾動軌跡（perturb α 或 γ，模擬 velocity 變化）。對比現有 CMap / LINCS 完全靜態（only 6/24h snapshots），Dynamo 可預測 continuous trajectory。

🎯 10. 用 velocity 評估 perturbation timing（when to intervene）

細胞分化中存在「decision window」——早期擾動可逆，晚期擾動無效。提案：用 velocity acceleration 找「fate-decision 點」，把 perturbation 設計集中在這些 time window。例如：iPSC 重編程第 6 天是 acceleration 最大時刻 → 該日擾動效率最高。對比現有 Perturb-seq 都「終點測量」，velocity 提供「干預時機」指引。

🎯 1. Drug-tolerant persister kinetics

Tumor heterogeneity often contains "pre-resistant" cells with partial resistance programs before therapy. Classical DE (resistant vs sensitive) misses cells "in progress". Proposal: use velocity direction (cells pointing towards the resistant terminal state) to detect "resistance in progress" 1–2 generations earlier than cluster-based DE. Contrast: tools like Augur only return static priority scores.

🎯 2. T cell exhaustion trajectory kinetics

Exhaustion is gradual (Tprog → Tex_int → Tex_term) with stage-specific TF kinetics (TOX, TCF7, TIM3). Proposal: stage-specific α/γ via dynamical scVelo on chronic LCMV data + Jacobian for "TF of each stage". Whereas Slingshot only gives pseudotime, velocity additionally provides per-gene "on/off times".

🎯 3. iPSC reprogramming dynamics

OSKM reprogramming has ~1% efficiency; the bottleneck is the "partially reprogrammed" pool. Proposal: velocity on days 4–8 OSKM induction to find cells with velocity pointing towards iPSC state vs trapped intermediates. Couple with Dynamo least action paths to identify fate barriers. Contrast: Waddington-OT uses static snapshots; velocity captures instantaneous dynamics.

🎯 4. Integrate metabolic profiling (13C labeling + scNT-seq)

scNT-seq labels new RNA; 13C glucose labels metabolites. Combined → "transcription velocity → metabolic velocity" causality. Proposal: extend Dynamo to support metabolite flux jointly, identify metabolic transitions that drive transcriptional shifts. Contrast: scMetabolism / Compass give only static flux predictions.

🎯 5. Velocity Jacobian for improved GRN inference

SCENIC, GENIE3, GRNBoost infer TF–target edges from static expression. Velocity Jacobian ∂(dG_i/dt)/∂G_j directly measures "how G_j change accelerates G_i transcription" — true causal direction. Proposal: scVelo + sparseVFC + Jacobian as a "dynamic GRN" pipeline, integrated with SCENIC (candidate edges) for direction + timing. Static SCENIC vs time-resolved Jacobian-GRN.

🎯 6. Cell-communication dynamics (which ligand triggers receptor velocity?)

CellChat uses static expression products. Signaling is intrinsically dynamic — ligand crosses threshold → receptor expression shifts → downstream velocity changes. Proposal: Granger-causality on velocity for paired sender-receiver clusters: "sender ligand velocity → receiver downstream velocity". Closer to the real signaling cascade than CellChat's static score.

🎯 7. Cross-species velocity comparison

Cross-species developmental comparisons currently only compare expression patterns. Proposal: align orthologue velocities, quantify whether α/γ are conserved at matched developmental stages — speed differences reveal species-specific regulation (e.g. why human neocortex maturation is far slower than mouse). Versus standard cross-species expression-mean comparisons.

🎯 8. RNA velocity + spatial (spatialVelo)

Visium / Stereo-seq have space but no time. Proposal: compute velocity per spot, analyze spatial divergence patterns — "kinetic hotspots" (wound-healing front, tumor invasion edge). Contrast: SpatialDE finds static spatial DE genes; spatialVelo finds "regions in motion".

🎯 9. Predict cell trajectories under drug perturbation

Drug screens look at "expression diff at 24 hr". But drug effects unfold dynamically — early transcriptional vs late phenotypic responses differ. Proposal: use Dynamo to extrapolate trajectories under perturbed α or γ (in silico perturbation of velocity field). Contrast: CMap / LINCS deliver only 6/24h snapshots; Dynamo simulates continuous trajectories.

🎯 10. Use velocity to choose perturbation timing

Differentiation has "decision windows" — early perturbations are reversible, late ones ineffective. Proposal: use velocity acceleration to locate fate-decision points; schedule perturbations precisely at those times (e.g. day 6 of OSKM reprogramming is the acceleration peak → maximally effective). Contrast: Perturb-seq usually measures only end-points; velocity tells you when to intervene.

實作範例

import scvelo as scv

scv.pp.filter_and_normalize(adata, min_shared_counts=20, n_top_genes=2000)
scv.pp.moments(adata, n_pcs=30, n_neighbors=30)
scv.tl.recover_dynamics(adata)        # 估計 α, β, γ
scv.tl.velocity(adata, mode="dynamical")
scv.tl.velocity_graph(adata)
scv.pl.velocity_embedding_stream(adata, basis="umap", color="celltype")
import dynamo as dyn

dyn.pp.recipe_monocle(adata)
dyn.tl.dynamics(adata, model="stochastic")
dyn.tl.cell_velocities(adata)
dyn.vf.VectorField(adata, basis="umap")   # 重建連續向量場
dyn.vf.jacobian(adata)                # 基因調控
dyn.vf.acceleration(adata); dyn.vf.divergence(adata)
import cellrank as cr
from cellrank.kernels import VelocityKernel, ConnectivityKernel

vk = VelocityKernel(adata).compute_transition_matrix()
ck = ConnectivityKernel(adata).compute_transition_matrix()
kernel = 0.8 * vk + 0.2 * ck
g = cr.estimators.GPCCA(kernel)
g.compute_macrostates(n_states=5); g.predict_terminal_states()
g.compute_fate_probabilities()
