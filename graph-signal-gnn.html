圖訊號處理與 GNN

把單細胞與空間資料看成「圖上的訊號」——Laplacian 譜分析、GCN、GAT 提供了一個統一框架，串起 kNN graph、空間鄰近圖、PPI 網路。

Treat single-cell and spatial data as "signals on graphs" — Laplacian spectral analysis, GCN, GAT unify kNN graphs, spatial neighborhood graphs, and PPI networks.

核心概念

圖的 Laplacian 與譜

給定圖 G = (V, E)，鄰接矩陣 A、度矩陣 D，圖 Laplacian 定義為 L = D − A（或對稱歸一化 Lsym = I − D⁻¹ᐟ² A D⁻¹ᐟ²）。L 的特徵分解 L = U Λ Uᵀ 是「圖傅立葉變換」——特徵向量 = 頻率，特徵值 λ = 頻率高低。

  • λ ≈ 0 的特徵向量 = 低頻 = 連通分支、平滑訊號
  • λ 大的特徵向量 = 高頻 = 鄰居間劇烈變化

降維（Laplacian eigenmaps）、聚類（spectral clustering）、降噪（low-pass filtering）統統都是 L 譜分析的應用。

Given graph G = (V, E), adjacency A, degree D; graph Laplacian L = D − A (or symmetric Lsym = I − D⁻¹ᐟ² A D⁻¹ᐟ²). The eigendecomposition L = U Λ Uᵀ is the "graph Fourier transform" — eigenvectors = frequencies, eigenvalues λ = pitch.

  • λ ≈ 0 eigenvectors = low frequency = connected components, smooth signals
  • Large λ eigenvectors = high frequency = wild variation between neighbors

Dim reduction (Laplacian eigenmaps), clustering (spectral clustering), denoising (low-pass filtering) — all spectral applications.

從 GSP 到 GNN

GCN / GAT：把圖卷積學起來

GCN (Kipf & Welling 2017)：每一層 H(l+1) = σ(Ã H(l) W(l))，其中 Ã 是加自環、對稱歸一化的鄰接矩陣。直覺：每個節點把鄰居特徵取平均，再經過線性變換+非線性。

GAT (Veličković 2018)：用注意力加權鄰居——αij = softmax(a([Whi ‖ Whj]))。每個鄰居的重要性可學習，對異質鄰居（不同細胞型別）特別有用。

單細胞與空間轉錄體的關鍵：圖怎麼建 決定一切。

GCN (Kipf & Welling 2017): Each layer H(l+1) = σ(Ã H(l) W(l)), where Ã is adjacency with self-loops and symmetric normalization. Intuition: average neighbor features, then linear + non-linearity.

GAT (Veličković 2018): Weight neighbors with attention: αij = softmax(a([Whi ‖ Whj])). Learnable importance — useful for heterogeneous neighbors (different cell types).

For single-cell and spatial: how the graph is built decides everything.

kNN 圖（細胞之間）

在 PCA 空間找每個細胞的 k 個最近鄰——這是 Seurat/Scanpy 聚類的底層結構。

k nearest neighbors in PCA space — the backbone of Seurat/Scanpy clustering.

空間圖（空間鄰近）

在 spatial transcriptomics 中用 Delaunay 三角剖分或半徑鄰近建圖——空間鄰居的訊息整合到特徵裡。

Delaunay triangulation or radius-based neighbors in spatial transcriptomics — neighbors' info integrated into features.

混合圖

SpaGCN、STAGATE 等把「表現相似」與「空間相鄰」混合成一張圖——同時考慮基因表現與位置。

SpaGCN, STAGATE blend "expression similarity" + "spatial proximity" into one graph — both modalities at once.

基因–基因圖

用 PPI、共表達或 KEGG pathway 當作圖結構——Geneformer 等模型其實是「在基因圖上做 attention」。

PPI, co-expression, or KEGG pathways as the graph — Geneformer-like models effectively do "attention on gene graphs."

互動模擬

GCN 鄰居平均（1 層）

幾何上 GCN 一層 = 把每個節點的訊號替換成「自身 + 鄰居」的平均。多次平均會把訊號越來越「平滑」——這就是 over-smoothing 問題。拖動「迭代次數」觀察。

One GCN layer = replace each node's signal with average of itself + neighbors. Many iterations over-smooth — the well-known over-smoothing problem. Drag iterations to observe.

生物應用

代表性方法

SpaGCN (Hu 2021)

在空間鄰近圖上跑 GCN，識別「空間域」(spatial domains)——比單純表現分群更貼近組織學切片。

GCN on spatial neighbor graph to identify spatial domains — closer to histological structure than expression-only clustering.

STAGATE (Dong 2022)

圖注意力自編碼器，在空間轉錄體上學「降噪 + 嵌入」——保留空間結構的同時補回 dropout。

Graph-attention autoencoder; learns denoising + embedding on spatial data — fills dropouts while preserving structure.

Cellcharter / CellNeighborEx

把細胞層次的鄰居組成轉換成新特徵——「同類聚集」vs「異類交界」可區分腫瘤內部生態。

Convert cell-level neighbor composition into features — homotypic vs heterotypic neighborhoods reveal tumor ecosystems.

Decipher / GraphST

最新一代：用對比學習 (contrastive) + 圖神經網路，對齊多切片或多模態 spatial 資料。

Latest gen: contrastive + GNN to align multiple slices or modalities in spatial data.

細胞–細胞通訊

把 ligand–receptor 對作為「邊」、細胞作為「節點」——GNN 學習通訊強度。比 CellChat 等統計方法更可擴展。

Ligand–receptor pairs as edges, cells as nodes — GNN learns communication strength. More scalable than CellChat-style statistics.

基因網路推論

DeepSEM、GENELink 等用 GNN 直接從 scRNA-seq 推 GRN，可整合先驗知識（PPI、motif）。

DeepSEM, GENELink use GNN to infer GRNs from scRNA-seq, integrating prior knowledge (PPI, motifs).

深入解析

深入解析：頻譜、過平滑、表達力與最新進展

① Graph Fourier Transform 的數學內涵

對無向圖，Laplacian L = D − A 是對稱半正定矩陣，可分解為 L = U Λ Uᵀ，其中 Λ = diag(λ₀, ..., λ_{n−1})、0 = λ₀ ≤ λ₁ ≤ ... ≤ λ_{n−1}。U 的每一行 u_k 就是「圖頻率 k」的基底，類比於傳統 Fourier 的 sin/cos。給定圖訊號 x ∈ ℝⁿ（每節點一個值，如基因表現），其 Graph Fourier Transform 為 x̂ = Uᵀ x。低 λ 對應「平滑」訊號（連通分支內幾乎常數）；高 λ 對應「劇變」訊號（鄰居間正負交錯）。圖卷積本質是 g(L) x = U g(Λ) Uᵀ x——在頻譜域對每個頻率做 element-wise filter。

② 對稱歸一化 vs Random Walk 歸一化

原始 L = D − A 的特徵值依節點度數而異。在實作中常用兩種歸一化：

  • 對稱歸一化：L_sym = I − D−1/2 A D−1/2，特徵值 ∈ [0, 2]，被 Kipf & Welling (2017) 用於 GCN，數學性質乾淨且保留對稱性，但對「異質度數圖」（hub 與 leaf 共存）資訊有偏移。
  • Random Walk 歸一化：L_rw = I − D−1 A，對應隨機漫步機率，可詮釋為「從鄰居取平均」，但矩陣非對稱、無法直接特徵分解（需用左/右特徵向量）。在 PageRank 系列、APPNP (Klicpera 2019) 中常用。

實作經驗：對 spatial transcriptomics 的 spot graph 通常 L_sym 較穩定；對 cell-cell heterogeneity 強的圖（如腫瘤微環境），L_rw 配合 personalized PageRank propagation 更佳。

③ GCN 的頻譜解讀

Defferrard et al. (2016) 提出 ChebNet：用 Chebyshev 多項式 T_k(L̃) 近似任意 graph filter g(L)，避免 O(n³) 的特徵分解。Kipf-Welling 的 GCN 進一步將 k 限定為 1 並做近似，得到 H(l+1) = σ(D̂−1/2 Â D̂−1/2 H(l) W(l))（其中 Â = A + I）。從頻譜角度看，每一層 GCN 等價於一個 low-pass filter g(λ) ≈ 1 − λ/2——只保留低頻、抑制高頻。多層堆疊 = 反覆 low-pass = 「平滑」。

④ Over-smoothing 的數學原因與緩解

Li et al. (2018, AAAI) 證明：當 GCN 層數 → ∞，所有節點特徵會收斂到 Laplacian 的 zero eigenspace（連通分支的指示向量），失去判別力。這就是 over-smoothing。緩解方法：

  • Residual GCN (Li 2019): H(l+1) = α H(0) + (1−α) GCN(H(l))，保留初始特徵。
  • JK-Net (Xu 2018, ICML): Jumping Knowledge，每層輸出 concat / max-pool，由模型自選最佳深度。
  • GCNII (Chen 2020, ICML): 結合 identity mapping 與 initial residual，理論證明可避免 over-smoothing 至任意深度，64 層仍能訓練。
  • DropEdge (Rong 2020): 隨機丟邊增加感受野多樣性。
  • PairNorm (Zhao 2020): 正規化讓節點對之間的 total pairwise distance 保持常數。
  • Attention-based: GAT 用 attention 動態決定鄰居權重，部分緩解（但深層仍會 collapse）。

⑤ Graph Attention：GAT 數學

Veličković et al. (2018, ICLR) 提出 GAT：每條邊 (i, j) 的權重不再固定，而由 attention 學出：α_{ij} = softmax_j( LeakyReLU( aᵀ [W h_i ‖ W h_j] ) )，其中 a 是可學參數向量、‖ 表示 concat。Multi-head attention K 個並行平均/concat。在 spatial transcriptomics 場景，GAT 能自動學會「哪些鄰居 spot 同屬一個 niche」，比固定 weight 的 GCN 更靈活。STAGATE (Dong 2022) 就是 spatial domain 用 GAT 的代表。

⑥ Subgraph-based / Inductive GNN

傳統 GCN 是 transductive——訓練時需要完整圖。GraphSAGE (Hamilton 2017, NeurIPS) 提出 inductive 學習：每節點隨機抽樣固定數量鄰居（如 K=10），用 mean/LSTM/pool aggregator 聚合。優點：(1) 可處理 1M+ 節點；(2) 新節點不需重新訓練；(3) 適合 streaming 資料。在 single-cell atlas 整合（樣本數百萬細胞）必用 GraphSAGE-like 架構，否則記憶體爆。

⑦ GNN 的表達力：Weisfeiler-Lehman 等價性

Xu et al. (2019, ICLR) 在 GIN (Graph Isomorphism Network) 論文證明：標準 message-passing GNN 的判別力上限等價於 1-WL test（無法區分某些非同構圖，如不同的 regular graph）。GIN 用 sum aggregator + MLP 達到 1-WL 上限。更強的表達力需要：(1) higher-order GNN (k-WL, Morris 2019)；(2) subgraph-based GNN (ESAN, GNN-AK)；(3) 加 positional / structural encoding (RWPE, Laplacian PE)。實作上 spatial transcriptomics 多用 1-WL 等價架構就夠，但藥物分子設計需要更強模型。

⑧ Heterogeneous GNN：節點與邊有多種型別

真實生物網路常有多型別：基因、蛋白、藥物、疾病；邊有 PPI、調控、共表達等。處理方法：

  • metapath2vec (Dong 2017, KDD): 沿著預定 metapath 做 random walk + Skip-gram。
  • R-GCN (Schlichtkrull 2018, ESWC): 對每種邊型別獨立 weight matrix W_r，aggregate 時依 relation 分組。
  • HGT (Hu 2020, WWW): Heterogeneous Graph Transformer，type-aware attention，可處理百萬節點異質網路（如 OpenAlex 學術圖）。

⑨ Graph Contrastive Learning

無標註資料時的自監督學習：

  • DGI (Veličković 2019, ICLR): Deep Graph Infomax，最大化 node embedding 與全圖 summary 的 mutual information。
  • GRACE (Zhu 2020): 用兩種圖擾動（edge drop + feature mask）生成正樣本對，contrastive loss。
  • BGRL (Thakoor 2022, ICLR): Bootstrapped Graph Latents，無需負樣本（仿 BYOL），對大圖更省記憶體。

GraphST (Long 2023, Nat Commun) 就是用 contrastive learning 整合多 ST 切片的代表。

① Mathematics of the Graph Fourier Transform

For an undirected graph, the Laplacian L = D − A is symmetric PSD and decomposes as L = U Λ Uᵀ with Λ = diag(λ₀, ..., λ_{n−1}), 0 = λ₀ ≤ λ₁ ≤ ... ≤ λ_{n−1}. Each column u_k of U is the basis for "graph frequency k", analogous to classical Fourier sin/cos. For a graph signal x ∈ ℝⁿ (one value per node, e.g. gene expression), the Graph Fourier Transform is x̂ = Uᵀ x. Low λ ⇒ smooth signal (nearly constant within connected components); high λ ⇒ rapidly oscillating signal (alternating signs across neighbors). Graph convolution is g(L) x = U g(Λ) Uᵀ x — element-wise filtering in the spectral domain.

② Symmetric vs Random-Walk Normalization

The raw L = D − A has eigenvalues that scale with node degree. Two common normalizations:

  • Symmetric: L_sym = I − D−1/2 A D−1/2, eigenvalues ∈ [0, 2], used in Kipf & Welling's GCN (2017). Clean math, preserves symmetry, but biased on degree-heterogeneous graphs.
  • Random walk: L_rw = I − D−1 A. Corresponds to a random walk; interpretable as "average over neighbors", but non-symmetric (needs left/right eigenvectors). Used in PageRank-family methods like APPNP (Klicpera 2019).

Empirically, L_sym is more stable on ST spot graphs; L_rw with personalized PageRank works better on heterogeneous tumor-microenvironment graphs.

③ Spectral Interpretation of GCN

Defferrard et al. (2016) proposed ChebNet, using Chebyshev polynomials T_k(L̃) to approximate any graph filter g(L), avoiding O(n³) eigendecomposition. Kipf-Welling's GCN restricts k=1 and approximates further: H(l+1) = σ(D̂−1/2 Â D̂−1/2 H(l) W(l)) with Â = A + I. Spectrally, each GCN layer is a low-pass filter g(λ) ≈ 1 − λ/2. Stacking many layers = repeated low-pass = smoothing.

④ Why Over-smoothing Happens, and How to Fix It

Li et al. (2018, AAAI) proved that as GCN depth → ∞, all node features converge to the Laplacian's zero eigenspace (indicator vectors of connected components) — losing discriminative power. Fixes:

  • Residual GCN (Li 2019): H(l+1) = α H(0) + (1−α) GCN(H(l)) preserves the initial signal.
  • JK-Net (Xu 2018, ICML): Jumping Knowledge — each layer feeds into a final concat/max-pool, model picks best depth.
  • GCNII (Chen 2020, ICML): identity mapping + initial residual; provably avoids over-smoothing at arbitrary depth, 64 layers trainable.
  • DropEdge (Rong 2020): randomly drop edges to diversify receptive fields.
  • PairNorm (Zhao 2020): normalize so total pairwise distance is preserved.
  • Attention: GAT learns neighbor weights — partially helps but still collapses very deep.

⑤ Graph Attention (GAT) Math

Veličković et al. (2018, ICLR) introduced GAT: each edge weight is learned by attention α_{ij} = softmax_j( LeakyReLU( aᵀ [W h_i ‖ W h_j] ) ) where a is a learnable vector, ‖ denotes concat. Multi-head attention runs K heads in parallel and averages/concats. In spatial transcriptomics, GAT automatically learns "which neighbor spots belong to the same niche" — more flexible than fixed-weight GCN. STAGATE (Dong 2022) is the canonical spatial-domain example.

⑥ Subgraph-based / Inductive GNN

Plain GCN is transductive — needs the full graph at training. GraphSAGE (Hamilton 2017, NeurIPS) introduced inductive learning: per node sample a fixed number of neighbors (e.g. K=10), then mean/LSTM/pool aggregate. Benefits: (1) scales to 1M+ nodes; (2) new nodes need no retraining; (3) supports streaming data. Single-cell atlas integration (millions of cells) needs GraphSAGE-style sampling — otherwise OOM.

⑦ GNN Expressivity: WL Equivalence

Xu et al. (2019, ICLR) in the GIN paper proved that standard message-passing GNNs are at most as expressive as the 1-WL test (cannot distinguish certain non-isomorphic graphs, e.g. some regular graphs). GIN uses sum + MLP to reach the 1-WL bound. Beyond 1-WL: (1) higher-order GNN (k-WL, Morris 2019); (2) subgraph GNN (ESAN, GNN-AK); (3) positional/structural encodings (RWPE, Laplacian PE). For ST, 1-WL-equivalent architectures usually suffice, but molecular design needs stronger models.

⑧ Heterogeneous GNNs

Biological networks contain multiple node and edge types — genes, proteins, drugs, diseases; PPI, regulation, co-expression. Methods:

  • metapath2vec (Dong 2017, KDD): random walks along predefined metapaths + Skip-gram.
  • R-GCN (Schlichtkrull 2018, ESWC): per-relation weight matrices, aggregate grouped by relation type.
  • HGT (Hu 2020, WWW): Heterogeneous Graph Transformer with type-aware attention; scales to million-node heterogeneous networks (e.g. OpenAlex).

⑨ Graph Contrastive Learning

Self-supervised learning without labels:

  • DGI (Veličković 2019, ICLR): Deep Graph Infomax, max MI between node embedding and global graph summary.
  • GRACE (Zhu 2020): two augmentations (edge drop + feature mask) form positives, contrastive loss.
  • BGRL (Thakoor 2022, ICLR): Bootstrapped Graph Latents, no negatives needed (BYOL-style), more memory-efficient on large graphs.

GraphST (Long 2023, Nat Commun) exemplifies contrastive learning for multi-slice ST integration.

詳細已知應用

GNN / GSP 在單細胞與空間體學的具體案例

🧬 案例 1：Hu et al. 2021, Nature Methods — SpaGCN

第一個把 GCN 系統性應用於 Visium spatial transcriptomics 的工作。核心：把 spot 視為節點，邊由「空間距離 + 組織影像 (H&E) 顏色相似度」加權，再以 GCN 學每個 spot 的低維表徵；最後用 mclust 做 spatial domain 聚類。在 DLPFC、breast cancer、mouse brain 上的 spatial domain 都比純 expression 聚類好。延伸還能找 spatial domain 特異基因。

🧬 案例 2：Dong & Zhang 2022, Nature Communications — STAGATE

用 graph attention auto-encoder：encoder 把 spot expression 編碼到 latent，decoder 重建；attention 讓模型自動降低「跨 domain 邊界邊」的權重。結果：在多種 ST 資料（Visium, Slide-seq, Stereo-seq）的 domain identification 上勝過 SpaGCN、BayesSpace。還能用 attention map 量化「哪些 spot pair 屬同一 domain」，做 cross-modal 驗證。

🧬 案例 3：Long et al. 2023, Nature Communications — GraphST

把 graph contrastive learning（augmentation = edge drop + feature mask）應用於 ST，特別擅長「多切片整合」——一次處理多個病人/多個時間點的切片，避免 batch effect 主導 latent。在 10x Visium、Slide-seqV2、Stereo-seq 上都有 SOTA 表現，且整合後可直接做跨切片的 spatial domain 對齊。

🧬 案例 4：Li & Zhou 2022, Nature Communications — CCST

Cell Clustering using Spatial Transcriptomics graph (CCST)：用 DGI (Deep Graph Infomax) 做無監督學習，並設計專門針對 cell-cell communication 的圖結構（節點是 cell，邊是 ligand-receptor 共表達 + 空間鄰近）。可推斷哪對細胞型別之間有顯著 paracrine 訊號，已用於分析腫瘤微環境免疫互動。

🧬 案例 5：Ren et al. 2022, Genome Biology — CellNeighborEx

關注 spatial neighborhood 對基因表現的因果影響。用 GNN 預測「某 cell 周圍的 cell type composition 是否影響其 gene expression」，並做 counterfactual：「如果改變鄰居組成，目標基因會怎樣變？」。在乳癌 ST 資料找到多個「鄰居依賴」基因，多為免疫–腫瘤交互作用 marker。

🧬 案例 6：Shao et al. 2022, Nature Communications — DeepST

整合 H&E image (用 CNN encode) + gene expression + spatial location 的 GNN 框架。優於僅用單一模態的方法，且對「組織學差異大但分子差異小」的 domain（如腫瘤邊界）特別有效。

🧬 案例 7：Wang et al. 2022, Briefings in Bioinformatics — DeepSEM / GENELink

用 GNN 推 gene regulatory network：把基因當節點，先用 prior PPI 或 ChIP-seq 建初始圖，再用 GNN refine。GENELink 進一步把 link prediction 框架化為 supervised 學習，比 GENIE3 等傳統方法 AUPR 高 15–30%。

🧬 案例 8：Jumper et al. 2021, Nature — AlphaFold2 Evoformer

AlphaFold2 的 Evoformer 模組本質是一種特殊的 graph attention（在 MSA 與 pair representation 上交替做 attention）。雖然名稱不叫 GNN，但操作完全是 graph-based message passing。這是 GNN 在結構生物學最重要的成功。

🧬 案例 9：Roohani et al. 2024, Nature Biotechnology — GEARS

用 GNN 預測 Perturb-seq 中「同時擾動多個基因」的效應。GEARS 用 GNN 把基因 prior knowledge graph (GO + PPI) 整合進模型，能 zero-shot 預測未見過的 perturbation combination。

🧬 案例 10：StellarGraph & PyG 在 biological networks

PyTorch Geometric 與 StellarGraph 已成為 GNN 標準工具，在藥物–靶點預測（DTI）、PPI 預測、單細胞分群、藥物副作用預測（Decagon, Zitnik 2018）等多個任務上廣泛應用。

🧬 Case 1: Hu et al. 2021, Nature Methods — SpaGCN

The first systematic application of GCN to Visium ST. Spots are nodes; edges weighted by spatial distance + H&E color similarity. GCN learns spot embeddings, then mclust clusters to identify spatial domains. Beats expression-only clustering on DLPFC, breast cancer, mouse brain. Extension also identifies domain-specific genes.

🧬 Case 2: Dong & Zhang 2022, Nature Communications — STAGATE

Graph attention auto-encoder: encoder maps spot expression to latent, decoder reconstructs; attention downweights cross-domain boundary edges. Outperforms SpaGCN / BayesSpace on Visium, Slide-seq, Stereo-seq. Attention maps quantify "which spot pairs belong to same domain" — useful for cross-modal validation.

🧬 Case 3: Long et al. 2023, Nature Communications — GraphST

Graph contrastive learning (augmentation = edge drop + feature mask) for ST, particularly strong at multi-slice integration — handles many patients/timepoints at once, avoids batch effects dominating the latent. SOTA on 10x Visium, Slide-seqV2, Stereo-seq.

🧬 Case 4: Li & Zhou 2022, Nature Communications — CCST

Cell Clustering using Spatial Transcriptomics graph: unsupervised DGI on a custom graph (nodes = cells, edges = L-R co-expression + spatial proximity). Infers which cell-type pairs show significant paracrine signaling; applied to tumor microenvironment immune crosstalk.

🧬 Case 5: Ren et al. 2022, Genome Biology — CellNeighborEx

Focuses on the causal effect of spatial neighborhood on expression. GNN predicts whether neighbor cell-type composition influences a cell's expression, with counterfactual: "if neighbors changed, how would the target gene change?" Identified neighborhood-dependent genes in breast cancer ST, many being immune-tumor interaction markers.

🧬 Case 6: Shao et al. 2022, Nature Communications — DeepST

Integrates H&E imaging (CNN-encoded) + gene expression + spatial coords in a GNN. Outperforms single-modality methods, especially on histologically distinct but molecularly subtle domains (e.g. tumor margins).

🧬 Case 7: Wang et al. 2022, Briefings in Bioinformatics — DeepSEM / GENELink

GNN for GRN inference. Genes are nodes; initialize graph with PPI / ChIP-seq priors, then GNN refines. GENELink frames link prediction as supervised learning, gains 15–30% AUPR over GENIE3.

🧬 Case 8: Jumper et al. 2021, Nature — AlphaFold2 Evoformer

AlphaFold2's Evoformer is essentially graph attention (alternating attention over MSA and pair representations). Though not named GNN, the operations are graph-based message passing — the most important GNN success in structural biology.

🧬 Case 9: Roohani et al. 2024, Nature Biotechnology — GEARS

GNN predicts effects of multi-gene perturbations in Perturb-seq. GEARS encodes GO + PPI knowledge graphs into the model, enabling zero-shot prediction of unseen perturbation combinations.

🧬 Case 10: StellarGraph & PyG in biological networks

PyTorch Geometric and StellarGraph are now standard tools — used in drug-target interaction prediction, PPI prediction, single-cell clustering, drug side-effect prediction (Decagon, Zitnik 2018), and more.

潛在改良應用

GNN / GSP 還能改良哪些任務？

🎯 1. 整合 Spatial GNN + RNA Velocity 的時空動態建模

現有限制：STAGATE、SpaGCN 只用 static expression 學 spatial domain，看不到「動態」資訊；scVelo 只看 unspliced/spliced 不用空間。新提案：把 RNA velocity (ds/dt) 當作節點屬性，spatial graph 當作圖結構，用 graph neural ODE (PDE on graph) 學習「在空間圖上的轉錄速率場」。預期改進：能預測「未來 6 小時這個 spot 的細胞型別組成會如何變」，對發育、傷口癒合、腫瘤侵襲的時間外推有重大意義。

🎯 2. 跨切片 Graph Contrastive Learning（Patient-aware ST 整合）

現有限制：GraphST 整合多切片但假設「相同 spatial domain 在不同切片應該對齊」。但 patient-specific 疾病異質性會破壞這假設。新提案：用 hierarchical contrastive learning：第一層 within-slice、第二層 patient-aware（同病人 vs 不同病人）、第三層 disease-state-aware。預期改進：在 100+ 病人的 ST cohort 上能找到「patient-shared 疾病模式 vs patient-unique 模式」，對 precision medicine 有用。

🎯 3. Patient-specific Disease Network

現有限制：傳統 GRN 是 population-level，無法反映個體差異。新提案：用 meta-GNN（learn to learn）：先在 cohort 上 pretrain，再對每個病人 fine-tune 出個體化網路。利用 GNN inductive 特性（GraphSAGE）讓新病人不用重訓。預期改進：可找出「對病人 A 重要但對病人 B 不重要」的調控邊，指導 personalized drug target 選擇。

🎯 4. Multi-relational Graph：基因 × 蛋白 × 路徑 × 細胞型別

現有限制：大多 single-cell GNN 只用單一關係（共表達或 PPI）。新提案：建構多型別異質圖：節點 = {gene, protein, pathway, cell type, drug}；邊 = {co-expression, PPI, GO membership, expressed-in, target-of}。用 HGT 或 R-GCN 在這多型別圖上學 unified embedding。預期改進：能做跨型別 link prediction，例如預測「藥物 X 對細胞型別 Y 的擾動」，整合 drug repurposing 與 single-cell pharmacology。

🎯 5. GNNExplainer for Ligand–Receptor 邊重要性

現有限制：CellChat 用 expression product 排序 L-R pair，無法量化「特定空間區域中哪個 L-R 對特定下游基因最關鍵」。新提案：對訓練好的 spatial GNN 用 GNNExplainer (Ying 2019, NeurIPS) 找出「對特定 cell type 預測最關鍵的 edge subset」，這些邊就是 niche-specific L-R interactions。預期改進：提供 mechanistic interpretability，可用於設計 niche-specific drug。

🎯 6. NOTEARS-GNN：可微 Causal Discovery 結合 GNN

現有限制：NOTEARS (Zheng 2018) 假設線性 SCM；用 GNN 取代後仍多為 small-scale。新提案：把 GNN 當作 non-linear SCM 的 functional form，並用 NOTEARS 的 DAG constraint 做 differentiable 學習。可加 spatial prior（鄰近 spot 更可能有 causal link）。預期改進：能在 ST 資料中找到「空間因果」邊——例如「腫瘤旁 spot 的 IFN-γ 高表現是否因果性影響鄰近 spot 的 PD-L1」。

🎯 7. Multi-hop Drug-Target-Phenotype Reasoning

現有限制：傳統 drug-target prediction 只看 1-hop（藥 → 靶）；下游 phenotype 連結通常另外算。新提案：用 GNN 在 multi-relational graph 上做 path-based reasoning：藥 → 靶 → 通路 → 細胞型別 → phenotype。用 PathCon (Wang 2021) 或 R-GCN + path attention。預期改進：解釋性藥物再利用——不只說「藥 A 可能治病 B」，還能給出 4–5 hop 的機制路徑，方便濕實驗驗證。

🎯 8. 跨物種 Spatial Atlas 整合

現有限制：不同物種 ST atlas（人、鼠、斑馬魚）目前各自分析，跨物種比較難。新提案：用 cross-species GNN：節點是 cell；邊有 (a) 同物種 spatial neighbor; (b) 跨物種 ortholog gene 表現相似度。學 species-shared embedding 與 species-specific residual。預期改進：能找出「保守的 spatial niche」（如所有哺乳類肝臟的 portal vein niche）vs「物種特有 niche」（如人類胎盤特有 trophoblast 結構）。

🎯 9. 用 GNN 模擬 Perturbation 傳播

現有限制：GEARS 預測 perturbation 後 final state，沒有「動態傳播」模型。新提案：用 graph neural ODE：dx/dt = GNN(x, A)，把 perturbation 當初始條件，模擬訊號隨時間在 GRN 上的傳播。可訓練於 time-course Perturb-seq。預期改進：能預測「擾動 5 min, 30 min, 6 hr 後的細胞狀態」，對訊號傳遞動力學研究有幫助。

🎯 10. 單細胞蛋白質 + 轉錄體聯合 Spatial GNN

現有限制：CODEX、CyCIF（空間蛋白）與 Visium、Stereo-seq（空間 RNA）分開分析。新提案：用 multi-view GNN：每個 spot 有兩個 view（RNA 與 protein），用 cross-modal attention 整合。可建構 joint niche definition。預期改進：找到「轉錄級看似同質但蛋白翻譯後修飾不同」的隱藏 niche——這在腫瘤抗藥性研究尤其重要（藥物靶點通常是蛋白）。

🎯 1. Spatial GNN + RNA Velocity for Spatio-temporal Dynamics

Current limit: STAGATE / SpaGCN use only static expression; scVelo ignores space. Proposal: treat RNA velocity (ds/dt) as node attribute, spatial graph as the structure, and learn a graph neural ODE — a "transcription velocity field on a spatial graph". Expected gain: predict "in 6 h what cell-type composition this spot will have" — major for development, wound healing, tumor invasion.

🎯 2. Cross-slice Graph Contrastive Learning (Patient-aware ST)

Current limit: GraphST assumes "same spatial domain aligns across slices", broken by patient-specific disease heterogeneity. Proposal: hierarchical contrastive learning — within-slice / patient-aware / disease-state-aware. Expected gain: on 100+ patient cohorts, separate "patient-shared disease patterns" from "patient-unique" ones — useful for precision medicine.

🎯 3. Patient-specific Disease Network via Meta-GNN

Current limit: classical GRN is population-level. Proposal: meta-GNN (learn-to-learn): pretrain on cohort, fine-tune per patient. GraphSAGE inductive property avoids retraining on new patients. Expected gain: find regulatory edges important for patient A but not B — guides personalized drug target selection.

🎯 4. Multi-relational Heterogeneous Graph

Current limit: most single-cell GNNs use one relation (co-expression or PPI). Proposal: build a heterogeneous graph with nodes {gene, protein, pathway, cell type, drug} and edges {co-expression, PPI, GO membership, expressed-in, target-of}, then HGT or R-GCN learns a unified embedding. Expected gain: cross-type link prediction (e.g. "drug X effect on cell type Y") — unifies drug repurposing and single-cell pharmacology.

🎯 5. GNNExplainer for L–R Edge Importance

Current limit: CellChat ranks L-R pairs by expression product, can't say which is most important for a specific downstream gene in a specific spatial region. Proposal: apply GNNExplainer (Ying 2019, NeurIPS) to a trained spatial GNN to find edge subsets critical for predicting specific cell types — niche-specific L-R interactions. Expected gain: mechanistic interpretability for niche-targeted drug design.

🎯 6. NOTEARS-GNN: Differentiable Causal Discovery + GNN

Current limit: NOTEARS (Zheng 2018) assumes linear SCM; non-linear extensions remain small-scale. Proposal: use GNN as the SCM's functional form with the NOTEARS DAG constraint for differentiable learning. Add a spatial prior (nearby spots more likely causally linked). Expected gain: recover "spatial causal" edges in ST — e.g. "does high IFN-γ in tumor-adjacent spots causally drive PD-L1 in neighbors?"

🎯 7. Multi-hop Drug-Target-Phenotype Reasoning

Current limit: classical DTI looks only 1-hop. Proposal: path-based reasoning on multi-relational graph: drug → target → pathway → cell type → phenotype, via PathCon (Wang 2021) or R-GCN + path attention. Expected gain: explainable drug repurposing — not just "drug A may treat disease B" but a 4–5-hop mechanistic path for wet-lab validation.

🎯 8. Cross-species Spatial Atlas Integration

Current limit: human, mouse, zebrafish ST atlases are analyzed separately. Proposal: cross-species GNN with nodes = cells, edges = (a) within-species spatial neighbors and (b) cross-species ortholog expression similarity. Learn species-shared embedding + species-specific residual. Expected gain: find "conserved spatial niches" (e.g. mammalian portal-vein niche) vs species-unique ones (e.g. human-specific trophoblast structures).

🎯 9. GNN-based Perturbation Propagation

Current limit: GEARS predicts only the final state, no dynamic propagation. Proposal: graph neural ODE dx/dt = GNN(x, A) with perturbation as initial condition; train on time-course Perturb-seq. Expected gain: predict cell state at 5 min, 30 min, 6 h post-perturbation — valuable for signaling kinetics.

🎯 10. Joint Protein + RNA Spatial GNN

Current limit: CODEX/CyCIF (spatial protein) and Visium/Stereo-seq (spatial RNA) are analyzed independently. Proposal: multi-view GNN with two views per spot (RNA + protein), cross-modal attention integrates them, joint niche definition. Expected gain: uncover hidden niches that look homogeneous in RNA but differ in protein PTMs — critical for tumor drug resistance research (drug targets are usually proteins).

import torch
from torch_geometric.nn import GCNConv, GATConv

class GCN(torch.nn.Module):
    def __init__(self, in_dim, hid, out):
        super().__init__()
        self.c1 = GCNConv(in_dim, hid)
        self.c2 = GCNConv(hid, out)
    def forward(self, x, edge_index):
        x = torch.relu(self.c1(x, edge_index))
        return self.c2(x, edge_index)
