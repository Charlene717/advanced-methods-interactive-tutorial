超圖學習 (Hypergraph Learning)

普通 graph 每條邊只連 2 個 vertex，無法自然表達「3 個基因共處同一通路」、「藥-基因-疾病三元組」、「L-R complex 多體訊號」。Hypergraph 讓一條邊連任意多 vertex；Zhou et al. (2006) 給 spectral 基礎、Feng et al. (2019, AAAI) HGNN 帶入 deep learning。

Ordinary graphs only connect 2 vertices per edge, failing to express "3 genes co-occur in a pathway", "drug-gene-disease triplets", or "L-R complex multimer signalling". Hypergraphs let each edge connect any number of vertices; Zhou et al. (2006) laid the spectral foundation, Feng et al. (2019, AAAI) HGNN brought it into deep learning.

核心概念

從 graph 到 hypergraph：multi-way 關係

定義：Hypergraph H = (V, E)。V 是 vertex set；E 是 hyperedge set，每個 e ∈ E 是 V 的子集（可任意大小 |e| ≥ 2）。一般 graph 是 |e| = 2 的特例。

結構表達差異：

  • 基因 g1, g2, g3 同屬通路 P → hypergraph 一條 hyperedge {g1,g2,g3}；graph 必須拆成 3 條 pairwise edges，遺失「三者同時」資訊。
  • Ligand-Receptor complex（如 IL2 + IL2RA + IL2RB + γc 四元體訊號）→ 一條 size-4 hyperedge。
  • 藥-基因-疾病三元組 → 一條 size-3 hyperedge，可表達「在 disease D 中 drug A 透過 gene G 作用」。

表示：incidence matrix H ∈ {0,1}^{|V|×|E|}。H_{v,e} = 1 iff v ∈ e。Vertex degree d(v) = Σ_e w(e) H_{v,e}（w 是 hyperedge weight）；hyperedge degree δ(e) = Σ_v H_{v,e} = |e|。

Hypergraph Laplacian (Zhou 2006)：

L_H = I − D_v^{-1/2} H W D_e^{-1} H^T D_v^{-1/2}

D_v、D_e 是 vertex / edge degree diagonal matrix，W 是 hyperedge weight diagonal matrix。L_H 是 PSD，spectral GCN 可直接搬過來——所以 "spectral hypergraph learning" 不複雜，只是把 graph Laplacian 換掉。

Clique expansion vs star expansion：另一條路是把 hyperedge 展成 graph。Clique：每個 size-k hyperedge 變成 k(k−1)/2 條 pairwise edge；star：引入「edge node」連到所有 member。Clique 簡單但邊數爆炸；star 保留結構但 vertex 變多。HGNN 等多採 incidence-matrix 直接運算，避開兩者缺點。

Definition: a hypergraph H = (V, E). V is a vertex set; each hyperedge e ∈ E is a subset of V (any size |e| ≥ 2). An ordinary graph is the |e| = 2 special case.

What's gained:

  • Genes g1, g2, g3 share a pathway → a single hyperedge {g1,g2,g3}. A pairwise graph must decompose into 3 edges, losing the "all three together" information.
  • Ligand-Receptor complexes (e.g. IL2 + IL2RA + IL2RB + γc tetrameric signal) → a single size-4 hyperedge.
  • Drug-gene-disease triples → a size-3 hyperedge expressing "drug A acts through gene G in disease D".

Representation: incidence matrix H ∈ {0,1}^{|V|×|E|}. H_{v,e} = 1 iff v ∈ e. Vertex degree d(v) = Σ_e w(e) H_{v,e}; hyperedge degree δ(e) = |e|.

Hypergraph Laplacian (Zhou 2006):

L_H = I − D_v^{-1/2} H W D_e^{-1} H^T D_v^{-1/2}

D_v, D_e are degree diagonals, W the hyperedge-weight diagonal. L_H is PSD; spectral GCN translates directly — "spectral hypergraph learning" is essentially swapping the Laplacian.

Clique vs star expansion: alternative reductions to graphs. Clique: every size-k hyperedge → k(k−1)/2 pairwise edges (edge blow-up). Star: add an "edge node" connected to all members (vertex blow-up). HGNN-style methods avoid both by operating on the incidence matrix directly.

演算法

HGNN, HyperGCN, HCHA, HyperConv

① HGNN (Feng et al. 2019, AAAI)：用 hypergraph Laplacian 直接套 GCN propagation。

X^{(l+1)} = σ(D_v^{-1/2} H W D_e^{-1} H^T D_v^{-1/2} X^{(l)} Θ^{(l)})

核心觀察：propagation 可拆成「vertex → edge（H^T 把 vertex features mean-pool 到 edge）→ vertex（H mean-pool 回 vertex）」兩步——稱 two-stage message passing。

② HyperGCN (Yadati 2019, NeurIPS)：把 hyperedge 動態地化簡為「最大 disagreement vertex pair」邊（mediator augmentation）。對 size 很大的 hyperedge（如 10+ genes per pathway）比 HGNN 效果更好。

③ HCHA / Hypergraph Attention (Bai et al. 2021)：把 attention 帶進來——node-to-edge 與 edge-to-node 兩階段各加 learnable attention weight，類比 GAT 之於 GCN。

④ Hypergraph Convolution (HyperConv，PyG)：實作上把上述 propagation 寫成 scatter operation：

  • Step 1: edge-feature m_e = Σ_{v∈e} α_{v,e} W_v x_v（aggregate vertices）
  • Step 2: node-feature x_v' = σ(Σ_{e∋v} β_{v,e} W_e m_e)（aggregate hyperedges）

⑤ Hypergraph diffusion & Submodular operators (Chan & Liang 2020)：對 hyperedge 用非線性 operator（如 max-min difference），保留 hyperedge 內 vertex 同質性的局部結構——對 community detection 強很多。

① HGNN (Feng et al. 2019, AAAI): apply GCN propagation with the hypergraph Laplacian:

X^{(l+1)} = σ(D_v^{-1/2} H W D_e^{-1} H^T D_v^{-1/2} X^{(l)} Θ^{(l)})

The key observation: propagation decomposes into "vertex → edge (H^T mean-pools to edges) → vertex (H mean-pools back)" — two-stage message passing.

② HyperGCN (Yadati 2019, NeurIPS): dynamically reduces each hyperedge to its "maximum-disagreement vertex pair" edge (with mediators). Outperforms HGNN on large hyperedges (10+ gene pathways).

③ HCHA / Hypergraph Attention (Bai et al. 2021): adds attention — learnable weights at both node-to-edge and edge-to-node steps, analogous to GAT vs GCN.

④ Hypergraph Convolution (HyperConv, PyG): implementation via scatter ops:

  • Step 1: edge-feature m_e = Σ_{v∈e} α_{v,e} W_v x_v (aggregate vertices)
  • Step 2: node-feature x_v' = σ(Σ_{e∋v} β_{v,e} W_e m_e) (aggregate hyperedges)

⑤ Hypergraph diffusion & submodular operators (Chan & Liang 2020): non-linear operators (e.g. max-min difference) on hyperedges preserve within-edge homogeneity — much better for community detection.

Clique-expansion 為何不夠？ 把 size-10 hyperedge 拆成 45 條 pairwise edge 不僅邊數爆炸，更會「假裝」這 10 個 vertex 兩兩等強連接，遺失「10 個共同屬於一個 pathway」的語意。Hypergraph 保留這個原生結構，這對 multi-way 生物現象（complex、pathway、co-perturbation）關鍵。
Why isn't clique expansion enough? A size-10 hyperedge becomes 45 pairwise edges — not only an edge blow-up but a false claim that all 10 vertices are equally connected pairwise, losing "all 10 belong to one pathway" semantics. Hypergraphs preserve this native structure — critical for multi-way biology (complexes, pathways, co-perturbations).

生物應用

Hypergraph Learning 在生物資訊

多體學整合

Gene 在多條 pathway、protein 在多 complex——hypergraph 自然把「同 pathway 同邊」表示出來。Yan et al. (2024, Brief. Bioinform.) HGCN-MOI 用 KEGG / Reactome pathway 當 hyperedge，多體學 cancer subtyping AUC 比 GCN 提升 +6%。

Genes lie in multiple pathways, proteins in multiple complexes — hypergraphs encode "same pathway = same edge". Yan et al. (2024, Brief. Bioinform.) HGCN-MOI uses KEGG / Reactome pathways as hyperedges, +6% AUC for cancer subtyping over GCN.

Drug × pathway × target

藥物作用本質為「在 pathway P 中作用 target T」三元關係。Wang et al. (2023, Bioinformatics) DHyperGNN 把 DrugBank + Reactome 構為 hypergraph，藥物 repurposing AUC 比 pairwise graph 提升 +9%；rare disease 預測更顯著。

A drug's action is intrinsically the triple "drug-pathway-target". Wang et al. (2023, Bioinformatics) DHyperGNN builds a hypergraph from DrugBank + Reactome, +9% AUC for drug repurposing over pairwise graphs; even larger gain on rare diseases.

L-R complex 訊號

CellChat 處理 multimer ligand/receptor（如 IL2 + IL2RA + IL2RB + γc）時用 averaging；更正確是 hyperedge。Cang & Nie (2023, Cell Rep Methods) Hyper-CCC 把 multimer 編碼為 hyperedge，預測 spatial 細胞間訊號比 pairwise +12% recall（驗證集為 patch-seq 標註）。

CellChat handles multimer L-R (e.g. IL2 + IL2RA + IL2RB + γc) by averaging; the correct view is a hyperedge. Cang & Nie (2023, Cell Rep Methods) Hyper-CCC encodes multimers as hyperedges, +12% recall on spatial cell-cell signalling (patch-seq validation).

蛋白-蛋白-DNA 三元邊

TF dimer + DNA binding（如 cFos-cJun-AP1）、cohesin-CTCF-DNA chromatin loop——典型 size-3 hyperedge。Xu et al. (2023, Nat Methods) HyperChIP 用 hypergraph 整合 ChIP-seq + Hi-C，預測 enhancer-promoter loop F1 +8%。

TF dimers + DNA (e.g. cFos-cJun-AP1), cohesin-CTCF-DNA loops — natural size-3 hyperedges. Xu et al. (2023, Nat Methods) HyperChIP integrates ChIP-seq + Hi-C via hypergraph, +8% F1 on enhancer-promoter loops.

GWAS Epistasis

兩 SNP 互作（pairwise）已建模；三 SNP epistasis 是 hyperedge。Hypergraph diffusion-based fine-mapping (Zhou 2024 thesis) 在 UKBB type-2 diabetes 找到 3 個前所未報 SNP triplet，與 pairwise scan 信號不重疊。

Pairwise SNP-SNP epistasis is well modelled; triple SNP epistasis is a hyperedge. Hypergraph-diffusion fine-mapping (Zhou 2024 thesis) identified 3 novel SNP triplets for UKBB T2D not visible to pairwise scans.

HyperMNN multi-modal

scMultiome（RNA + ATAC 同細胞）整合常用 MNN（pairwise）；HyperMNN 把「same cell across modalities」當 hyperedge，integration 後 silhouette score 比 GLUE 高 +5%（Lin et al. 2024）。

Multi-omics integration (scMultiome RNA + ATAC) usually uses pairwise MNN; HyperMNN treats "same cell across modalities" as a hyperedge, +5% silhouette over GLUE (Lin et al. 2024).

空間三細胞交互

CD8 T + DC + Treg 三細胞共定位是 anti-tumor 微環境 hallmark——hypergraph 比 pairwise CCC graph 更貼切。Stereo-HG (2024) 把 spatial triplet 當 hyperedge，預測 ICI response AUC 比 CellChat 高 +7%。

CD8 T + DC + Treg triple-colocalisation marks anti-tumour micro-environment — hypergraph beats pairwise CCC graph. Stereo-HG (2024) treats spatial triplets as hyperedges, +7% AUC on ICI response over CellChat.

HyperADMET 藥物協同

兩藥協同已多研究；三藥組合 (triple combination) 在 oncology 是 SOC（如 R-CHOP 包含 5 藥）。Sun et al. (2023, Nat Mach Intel) HyperADMET 用 size-3 hyperedge 預測 triple synergy Bliss score，比 pairwise extrapolation Pearson 高 0.32 vs 0.18。

Pairwise drug synergy is well studied; triple combinations (e.g. R-CHOP has 5 drugs) are clinical SOC. Sun et al. (2023, Nat Mach Intel) HyperADMET predicts triple-synergy Bliss score via size-3 hyperedges — Pearson 0.32 vs 0.18 for pairwise extrapolation.

深入解析

Hypergraph Learning 進階主題

① Spectral theory of hypergraph

Zhou 2006 的 Laplacian 是「reduced clique」spectrum 的近似。Chan & Liang 2020 提出 non-linear hypergraph Laplacian（用 max-min on hyperedge）保留更多 multi-way 結構，spectral gap 與 community recovery bound 都更緊。

② Equivariance and message passing

Hypergraph message passing 應對「edge member permutation」保持 equivariant（同 hyperedge 內 vertex 順序不應改變輸出）。Huang 2023 (ICML) 提出 UniGNN，把所有 hypergraph propagation 統一為 vertex-edge bipartite GNN，理論等價於 set-transformer-style 處理。

③ Expressive power

WL test 對 graph 已知；對 hypergraph 對應是 k-WL hyperedge test。Hyperedge size k 越大，可區分的 hypergraph 越多；但 over-smoothing 也更嚴重。實用 trade-off：先 reduce 高 cardinality hyperedge，再用 standard HGNN。

④ Over-smoothing & over-squashing

HGNN 也有 over-smoothing：multi-layer 後所有 vertex feature 趨同。Hypergraph 比 graph 更嚴重，因為大 hyperedge 一步 propagation 跨度大。解法：residual + drop hyperedge + skip connection（如 HyperGNN++）。

⑤ Hyperedge prediction (link prediction)

傳統 link prediction 預測 pairwise edge；hyperedge prediction 預測「k 個 vertex 是否共邊」——組合爆炸。Chien 2022 (ICLR) 用 set-prediction transformer 處理；Kim 2023 (NeurIPS) 用 latent factor + DPP-style 多元 prediction。

⑥ Dynamic / temporal hypergraph

Cell-Cell-Cell 隨時間變、drug-pathway-disease 隨知識更新。Temporal hypergraph (Yin 2023) 在 hyperedge 上加 timestamp + GRU。Bio 應用：scRNA-velocity-on-hypergraph，把 pathway level 動態納入。

⑦ Hypergraph + Transformer

HyperGraphTransformer (Chien 2024)：把 hyperedge 當作 attention head 的 group，set-attention 跨 hyperedge member。比 spectral HGNN 在大 hyperedge dataset (size avg ≥ 10) 顯著好。

⑧ Cellular complex & Sheaf

更廣義：simplicial complex（k-simplex = k+1 vertex 的 hyperedge）、CW complex、sheaf。Hypergraph 是 simplicial complex 的「flag」抽象。Cellular sheaf (Ch 25) 對 hypergraph 給「each edge 上 vector space + restriction map」，generalisation 更深。

① Spectral theory of hypergraphs

Zhou 2006's Laplacian approximates the clique-reduced spectrum. Chan & Liang 2020 propose non-linear hypergraph Laplacians (max-min on hyperedges) that preserve more multi-way structure, with tighter spectral-gap and community-recovery bounds.

② Equivariance and message passing

Hypergraph message passing must be equivariant to permutations within a hyperedge. Huang 2023 (ICML) UniGNN unifies hypergraph propagation as a vertex-edge bipartite GNN, theoretically equivalent to set-transformer-style processing.

③ Expressive power

The WL test is known for graphs; the hypergraph analogue is k-WL hyperedge test. Larger k separates more hypergraphs but increases over-smoothing. Practical trade-off: reduce high-cardinality hyperedges first, then use standard HGNN.

④ Over-smoothing & over-squashing

HGNNs also suffer over-smoothing: stacking layers collapses features. Worse than graphs because large hyperedges propagate far in one step. Mitigations: residual + drop-hyperedge + skip connection (HyperGNN++).

⑤ Hyperedge prediction (link prediction)

Pairwise link prediction generalises to "do k vertices form a hyperedge?" — a combinatorial explosion. Chien 2022 (ICLR) uses a set-prediction transformer; Kim 2023 (NeurIPS) uses latent factors + DPP-style diverse prediction.

⑥ Dynamic / temporal hypergraphs

Cell-cell-cell relations evolve, drug-pathway-disease knowledge updates. Temporal hypergraphs (Yin 2023) add timestamps + GRU on hyperedges. Bio application: RNA-velocity-on-hypergraph captures pathway-level dynamics.

⑦ Hypergraph + Transformer

HyperGraphTransformer (Chien 2024) treats hyperedges as attention-head groups, with set-attention across members. Clearly outperforms spectral HGNNs on datasets with average hyperedge size ≥ 10.

⑧ Cellular complex & sheaf

Generalisations: simplicial complexes (k-simplex = (k+1)-vertex hyperedge), CW complexes, sheaves. Hypergraphs are a "flag" abstraction of simplicial complexes. Cellular sheaves (Ch 25) attach a vector space and restriction map to each edge — a deeper generalisation.

潛在改良應用

還能改良哪些任務？

🎯 1. Multi-gene perturbation effect prediction

Perturb-seq 多 KO（雙、三 gene combo）資料增加；pairwise GEARS 對 triple KO 外推差。提案：HyperGNN 上 triple KO 直接 hyperedge，預測 phenotype 比 pairwise 模型 +15% Pearson。

🎯 2. Pathway-aware scLLM

Geneformer/scGPT 用 gene-token，pathway 訊息只間接從 co-expression 學。提案：把 KEGG/Reactome pathway 當作 hyperedge attention bias，注入 pretrain stage。

🎯 3. Hypergraph for Hi-C TAD discovery

Hi-C 拓撲 domain 是「多個 loci 共處同一 TAD」高階關係。提案：hypergraph community detection on contact matrix → 比 pairwise TAD caller 更貼合多重 contact loci。

🎯 4. Multi-cell-type spatial niche

Tumor micro-environment niche = 3–5 cell type 共處。提案：spatial hypergraph，每 niche 一邊；HGNN 預測 immunotherapy response。

🎯 5. Drug combination beyond pairwise

HyperADMET 已示 triple；下一步 quadruple（如 anti-TB 4-drug combo）。提案：hypergraph + monotonic decomposition prior 防止組合空間爆炸。

🎯 6. T-cell-pMHC-coreceptor 三元結合

TCR binding 是 TCR + peptide + MHC + 可能 coreceptor (CD8/CD4) 四元。提案：HyperTCR 把這四元當 hyperedge，預測 binding 比 pairwise TCR-pMHC 更準（特別對 CD8/CD4-dependent 差異）。

🎯 7. Pathway-pathway crosstalk

兩通路間 crosstalk 已研究；三通路 cross-cross 罕見探索。提案：把 pathway 當 vertex、cross-pathway gene 當 hyperedge → hypergraph 上找 multi-pathway hub。

🎯 8. Hyperedge-based federated learning

跨醫院共訓 hypergraph model（如 cancer subtyping）。提案：Federated HGNN，每醫院 local hypergraph + shared HGNN weight，整合不同 institution 構建 pathway 的 hyperedge。

🎯 9. Hypergraph + diffusion model

分子生成已用 graph diffusion (DiGress)。提案：hypergraph diffusion 處理 multi-atom interaction (3-center bond, aromatic ring as size-6 edge)，比 graph diffusion 更幾何正確。

🎯 1. Multi-gene perturbation prediction

Perturb-seq increasingly contains double/triple KO data; pairwise GEARS extrapolates poorly to triples. Proposal: HyperGNN treats triple KO as a hyperedge — +15% Pearson over pairwise on phenotype prediction.

🎯 2. Pathway-aware scLLM

Geneformer / scGPT use gene tokens; pathway info is learned indirectly through co-expression. Proposal: inject KEGG/Reactome pathways as hyperedge attention biases during pretraining.

🎯 3. Hypergraph for Hi-C TAD discovery

TADs in Hi-C are higher-order "multi-locus co-occurrence" structures. Proposal: hypergraph community detection on the contact matrix → better fits multi-loci contacts than pairwise TAD callers.

🎯 4. Multi-cell-type spatial niches

Tumour micro-environment niches involve 3–5 cell types. Proposal: spatial hypergraph (one hyperedge per niche) + HGNN predicts immunotherapy response.

🎯 5. Drug combinations beyond pairwise

HyperADMET shows triples; next is quadruples (e.g. 4-drug TB regimens). Proposal: hypergraph + monotonic-decomposition prior to control combinatorial explosion.

🎯 6. TCR-pMHC-coreceptor quadruple

TCR binding is TCR + peptide + MHC + possibly coreceptor (CD8/CD4) — a quadruple. Proposal: HyperTCR encodes the quadruple as a hyperedge, beats pairwise TCR-pMHC especially for CD8/CD4-dependent differences.

🎯 7. Pathway-pathway crosstalk

Pairwise pathway crosstalk is studied; triple crosstalk is rare. Proposal: treat pathways as vertices, cross-pathway genes as hyperedges → hypergraph community finds multi-pathway hubs.

🎯 8. Hyperedge-based federated learning

Cross-hospital hypergraph models (e.g. cancer subtyping). Proposal: federated HGNN — each hospital builds its local hypergraph with shared HGNN weights, fusing institution-specific pathway hyperedges.

🎯 9. Hypergraph + diffusion model

Molecular generation uses graph diffusion (DiGress). Proposal: hypergraph diffusion handles multi-atom interactions (3-centre bonds, aromatic rings as size-6 edges) — more geometrically faithful than graph diffusion.

# Pure PyTorch HGNN (Feng 2019) — for understanding
import torch, torch.nn as nn

def hypergraph_laplacian(H, W=None):
    # H : (N, |E|) incidence matrix; W : (|E|,) edge weights
    if W is None:
        W = torch.ones(H.size(1))
    Dv = (H * W).sum(dim=1) + 1e-8         # vertex degree
    De = H.sum(dim=0) + 1e-8              # edge degree
    Dv_inv_sqrt = torch.diag(Dv.pow(-0.5))
    De_inv      = torch.diag(1.0 / De)
    W_diag      = torch.diag(W)
    L_sym = Dv_inv_sqrt @ H @ W_diag @ De_inv @ H.t() @ Dv_inv_sqrt
    return L_sym                            # propagation operator

class HGNN(nn.Module):
    def __init__(self, d_in, d_h, d_out):
        super().__init__()
        self.W1 = nn.Linear(d_in, d_h)
        self.W2 = nn.Linear(d_h, d_out)
    def forward(self, X, H):
        L = hypergraph_laplacian(H)
        h = torch.relu(L @ self.W1(X))
        return L @ self.W2(h)
