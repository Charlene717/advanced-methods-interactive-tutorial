符號回歸 (Symbolic Regression)

不是擬合一個黑盒神經網路，而是直接從數據反推出「封閉形式」的數學方程式——把 Kepler 行星軌道、SINDy 動力系統、scRNA velocity 的 ODE 全寫成可讀的公式。

Instead of fitting a black-box network, symbolic regression recovers closed-form equations directly from data — writing Kepler's orbits, SINDy dynamics, and scRNA velocity ODEs as readable formulas.

核心概念

從資料到方程式

傳統機器學習擬合一個 參數豐富 的函數 fθ(x) 來逼近 y——準確但不可解釋。符號回歸 (Symbolic Regression, SR) 改而搜尋一個 方程式樹（由加、乘、sin、exp、log、變數、常數構成），使該樹評估的數值能逼近資料。輸出不是「14 萬個權重」，而是 y = a · sin(b·x) + c 這樣的 closed-form 表達式。

為什麼有意義？因為 方程式有物理單位、有對稱性、能外推——對發現自然律 (Kepler, Newton) 與生物動力系統 (mRNA 動力學、基因調控) 至關重要。

Classical ML fits a heavily-parameterized fθ(x) — accurate but inscrutable. Symbolic Regression (SR) instead searches over expression trees (built from +, ×, sin, exp, log, variables, constants) for one whose evaluation matches the data. The output is not "140k weights" but a closed-form equation like y = a · sin(b·x) + c.

Why does this matter? Because equations carry units, symmetries, and extrapolate — crucial for rediscovering natural laws (Kepler, Newton) and biological dynamics (mRNA kinetics, gene regulation).

📜

可解釋

輸出是人類可讀的公式，有單位、有對稱性。

Output is human-readable; units and symmetries are explicit.

🚀

可外推

在訓練範圍外仍可信賴——神經網路做不到。

Trustworthy beyond the training range — unlike neural nets.

🧬

物理意義

能對應到化學動力學、ODE、PDE 等真實機制。

Maps onto kinetics, ODEs, PDEs — real mechanistic models.

演算法

主要演算法家族

① 遺傳規劃 Genetic Programming (GP)

把方程式編碼成「樹狀基因」：節點是運算子、葉子是變數或常數。從隨機樹族群開始，每代執行：(1) 選擇——適應度 = 擬合誤差 + 複雜度懲罰；(2) 交叉——交換子樹；(3) 突變——隨機改節點。代表工具：PySR (Cranmer 2023)、gplearn、Eureqa。

② SINDy — Sparse Identification of Nonlinear Dynamics (Brunton 2016)

專門找 ODE 形式 ẋ = f(x)。流程：(a) 從資料估 ẋ；(b) 建一個 candidate library Θ(x) = [1, x, x², sin x, ...]；(c) 用 sparse regression（LASSO 或 sequentially-thresholded least squares）找一個稀疏係數向量 ξ，使 ẋ ≈ Θ(x) · ξ。輸出：少數幾項的線性組合即為動力方程式。

③ AI Feynman (Udrescu & Tegmark 2020)

物理啟發的「分而治之」策略：先檢測函數是否具備 對稱性（平移、尺度、可分離）、單調性 等性質，據此把多維問題拆成低維子問題，遞迴呼叫。在 Feynman 100 條物理方程式 benchmark 上正確率 ~100%。

④ Neural-Symbolic Hybrid

用神經網路先學一個 柔軟 的近似，再「蒸餾」(distill) 出符號式。Cranmer (2020) 從 GNN 中萃取 force law、Kim (2021) 用 NN attention 找方程結構。最新趨勢：Transformer + symbolic decoder (Kamienny 2022, NeurIPS) — 像翻譯模型一樣「翻譯」資料為方程式。

① Genetic Programming (GP)

Encode equations as trees: operators on internal nodes, variables/constants at leaves. Each generation: (1) selection on fitness = fit error + complexity penalty; (2) crossover swaps subtrees; (3) mutation alters nodes. Tools: PySR (Cranmer 2023), gplearn, Eureqa.

② SINDy — Sparse Identification of Nonlinear Dynamics (Brunton 2016)

Targets ODEs ẋ = f(x). Steps: (a) estimate ẋ from data; (b) build a candidate library Θ(x) = [1, x, x², sin x, ...]; (c) sparse regression (LASSO or sequentially-thresholded LS) finds a sparse ξ such that ẋ ≈ Θ(x) · ξ. The few non-zero terms are the discovered dynamics.

③ AI Feynman (Udrescu & Tegmark 2020)

Physics-inspired divide-and-conquer: detect symmetry (translation, scale, separability) and monotonicity, then recursively split high-dim problems into low-dim subproblems. Reaches ~100% accuracy on the Feynman 100-equation benchmark.

④ Neural-Symbolic Hybrid

Fit a soft NN approximation then "distill" symbolic form. Cranmer (2020) distilled force laws from GNNs; Kim (2021) used NN attention for equation structure. Trend: Transformer + symbolic decoder (Kamienny 2022, NeurIPS) — "translates" data into equations like a language model.

SINDy 的祕密武器：稀疏性假設 = 「自然律通常用 ≤ 5 項表達」。這個 Occam's razor 先驗讓 SINDy 在低資料情境下勝過神經 ODE。
SINDy's secret weapon: sparsity prior = "natural laws usually need ≤ 5 terms." This Occam's-razor bias beats neural ODEs in low-data regimes.

生物應用

SR 在生物資訊的應用

RNA velocity ODE 反推

把 SINDy 套到 unspliced / spliced RNA 量上，反推 mRNA 動力系統的 ODE 形式——可超越 La Manno (2018) 的 linear-α 假設，發現 bursting、bistability 等非線性項。

Applying SINDy to unspliced/spliced counts recovers mRNA kinetic ODEs — going beyond La Manno (2018)'s linear-α assumption to find bursting and bistability terms.

GRN 方程式

Mangan et al. (2016, IEEE TMBMC) 把 SINDy 套到基因網路，從 time-series 表現資料反推 Hill function 形式的 TF–target 動力學。

Mangan et al. (2016, IEEE TMBMC) applied SINDy to gene networks, recovering Hill-function TF–target kinetics from time-series expression.

PK/PD 模型發現

藥物濃度-時間曲線傳統用 compartment ODE。SR 從臨床資料直接反推 ODE 形式——可發現新的非經典代謝路徑。

Pharmacokinetics traditionally uses compartment ODEs. SR recovers ODE form directly from clinical data, sometimes finding non-classical metabolic pathways.

代謝通量方程式

從 13C-MFA 同位素標記資料推導非線性代謝通量律——比固定 Michaelis-Menten 形式更靈活。

From 13C-MFA isotope tracing, SR derives non-linear flux laws — more flexible than fixed Michaelis-Menten forms.

Turing pattern 發現

用 SINDy-PDE 從空間轉錄體時序資料反推 reaction-diffusion 方程，發現組織形態發生的隱藏律。

SINDy-PDE on spatial-transcriptomics time series recovers reaction-diffusion equations, uncovering hidden laws of tissue morphogenesis.

Mango (2023)

把 SR 套到 single-cell pseudotime 動態，找出每個 cell type 的「dynamics signature」——一個 closed-form 表達式而非 vector field。

Mango (Maslennikov 2023) applies SR to single-cell pseudotime dynamics, deriving each cell type's closed-form dynamics signature instead of a vector field.

潛在改良應用

SR 還能改良哪些 omics 任務？

🎯 1. scRNA-seq + RNA velocity → GRN ODE 系統

結合 velocity（時間導數估計）與 SINDy，從單一快照反推 multi-gene ODE 系統，自動發現「主節點 TF + Hill 邏輯」。可超越目前 SCENIC 的線性回歸框架。

🎯 2. Perturbation dose-response 方程式

對 sci-Plex / LINCS 多劑量資料用 SR 找最佳擬合的 Hill / Emax / log-logistic 方程式，比固定模型更彈性。

🎯 3. 新的細胞–細胞通訊定律

從多 paired 空間轉錄體資料用 SR 找「ligand 濃度→ receptor 訊號」的 functional form——可能不是線性、不是 mass action，而是某種非經典 saturation。

🎯 4. Cell cycle 循環 ODE 發現

結合 Ch 8 cycle following 與 SINDy，重建細胞週期的 phase portrait ODE，定量「卡關點」與 oscillator frequency。

🎯 5. 給 scLLM 結果加上 closed-form 解釋

對 scGPT 的 attention map 用 SR 找「哪幾個 gene 的 Hill 組合 = 該 attention head」——讓 scLLM 變成「公式 ensemble」而非黑盒。

🎯 1. scRNA-seq + RNA velocity → GRN ODE system

Combine velocity (time derivative estimate) with SINDy to recover multi-gene ODE systems from a single snapshot, automatically discovering "hub TF + Hill logic" — beyond SCENIC's linear regression.

🎯 2. Perturbation dose-response equations

On sci-Plex / LINCS multi-dose data, SR fits the best Hill / Emax / log-logistic equation per gene — more flexible than fixed models.

🎯 3. New cell–cell communication laws

From paired spatial transcriptomics, SR finds the functional form of "ligand → receptor signal" — may not be linear, may not be mass-action, but some saturation regime.

🎯 4. Cell-cycle ODE discovery

Combining Ch 8 cycle-following with SINDy reconstructs the cell-cycle phase portrait ODE, quantifying stall points and oscillator frequency.

🎯 5. Closed-form explanations for scLLM outputs

SR on scGPT attention maps finds the Hill combination of a few genes equating to a given attention head — turning scLLMs into a "formula ensemble" instead of a black box.

X = np.random.uniform(-3, 3, (200, 2))
y = X[:, 0]**2 + np.sin(X[:, 1])

model = PySRRegressor(
    niterations=40,
    binary_operators=["+", "*", "-", "/"],
    unary_operators=["sin", "exp", "log"],
    model_selection="best",
)
model.fit(X, y)
print(model.sympy())   # symbolic equation
